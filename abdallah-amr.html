Abdallah Amr
rating: +17+–x

Camera tape rolling? Yeah? Awesome.

Heyo! Rose here,1 and welcome to my CSS Basement!2 I'm 17 years old,3 and am Egyptian.4 I use she/her and zhe/zher5 pronouns. I'm the Tech Subteam leader,6 a Moderator,7 and the most based CSS Witch to ever exist.8 Oh! I'm also the original founder of the Arabic ULI and the current owner of the Arabic Branch.9

Anyways enough yap aside, here are my current pages:

Phenomenon Threat Scale (PTS)

My first page on-site, and my first component. Designed by ReyDay while discussing my then upcoming phenomenon (now P32, more on it later), PTS is a highly customizable classification scale designed and tailored for phenomena of all types.

Phenomenon 32 - Level Velocity

My first posted article, this phenomenon is centered around the dynamic of the levels in the Blue Channel. It's a mix of astronomy and string theory in a way that makes it feel unique. Also fun fact I picked 32 because this phenomenon was going to originally be called "Level Shift" and I decided to pick 1 bit-shifted 5 times as a way to honor that original name, also an homage to Level 15, the level that sparked this idea whole.

A Wanderer Journal

A collaborative article documenting the journey of a Backrooms wanderer. Participated in the writing of this, specifically pages 13 and 14, and is technically my first writing project on this site, even before P32.

Offset Timeline

One of the components I am most proud of, this small yet elegant component lets you navigate pages with multiple offsets easily and quickly, without taking too much design from the page.

Font Toggle

Inspired by the original component by CutTheBirch and a redesign by JamiMyst, this component is aimed at enhancing accessibility by giving the ability to toggle hard-to-read fonts.

And there's more to come…

If you're interested to see what I'm up to, go check out my private sandbox!10
