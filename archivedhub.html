Archived Pages Hub

The Archived Pages Hub is a gallery containing a collection of old articles or depreciated site pages that have been moved away from the main wiki.

These articles can be:

  • Pages pre-rewrite, archived by their author or custodian
  • Pages pseudo-deleted due to author request
  • Depreciated site hubs
  • Depreciated site guides

The goal of the Archive hub is to provide faster and more direct access to older popular pages or site parts without the use of the Wayback Machine. If, for some reason, you cannot find what you were looking for, try using the Wayback Machine instead!

All archived pages also have the archived wiki tag, as well as moved in the archived: category.

The archived hub does NOT fulfill the same purpose as the Trimmed Pages Hub! If you are looking for pages that have been moved away from the main list due to quality issues, please refer to the latter link.

Pages staff of the GLMP have custody upon will be automatically archived if rewritten. For pages authors or custodians own, whether the page has to be archived or deleted depends on what they prefer.
