WELCOME TO THE BACKROOMS AUTHORS FILE REPOSITORY

ENTRY REQUIREMENTS
If you've created at least three or translated at least five successful1 pages (including Entity, Tale, Canon, and Artwork pages), please take the time to create an author page and take credit for your work. Create and tweak as you desire, and add yourself to the list above alphabetically by your Wikidot username.

The format for your slot is:

||[[*user YOURUSERNAME]]||[[[AUTHOR-PAGE-LINK |NAME-OF-LINK]]]||Wanderer||

See additional entries for more information.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this database, or need assistance adding your information to it, please contact staff.
