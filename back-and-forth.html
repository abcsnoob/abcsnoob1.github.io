Anyhow, I'm going to be taking my leave for now. Remember that Iris' life rests on your decisions.

Have fun!

Forever yours,
Sally ♡

THE FOLLOWING MESSAGE IS BEING BROADCASTED FROM THE OVERSEER COMMAND.

THE CONTENT IN THIS DOCUMENT IS STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL, AND ALL INFORMATION PRESENT IN IT IS NOT TO BE SHARED UNLESS SPECIFICALLY REQUESTED TO BY THE OVERSEER COMMAND.

YOU ARE REQUESTED TO DELETE THIS REPORT IMMEDIATELY AFTER YOU ARE DONE READING IT.

Hey Michael, it's Andrew.

Three days ago, we lost agent Iris Wilkins, a guard who was stationed at Level 3's Gamma base, to an unknown assailant who breached base walls without setting off the alarm. I trust that you remember the emergency transfer that you were assigned, and that documentation of the incident was handled by another one of your crewmates, correct?

There is, however… something which I have neglected to tell you. Something I have neglected to tell all of you. Keep what I am going to be telling you now between us at all costs.

There is a group going around The Backrooms which are targeting people like you and I under the guise of eliminating corruption in our circles. They all bear instances of Object 24, and they use the powers that the masks grant them to their full advantage to fulfill said goal.

As you know, Iris' unknown assailant was reported by her to be masked. Let's just say that… this 'unknown assailant' was not really unknown at all. You can probably see where I am going with this.

Prior to her death, me and Iris had been communicating via emails. This group, known as the Masked Maidens, had been stalking Base Gamma's guards for some time prior to her emailing, and Iris had brought it to my attention that night. I knew what she had meant when she began describing her stalkers immediately. I knew what the people she was talking about wanted from me. I knew what was going to happen to her, and I knew what they were going to do to her. This is not the first time I have played the game of chess with the Maidens.

They requested for us to stop our experimentation on Object 24, something which we ultimately cannot do due to projects which I am afraid I cannot disclose to you. They put Iris' life to ransom, but that did little to stop me from continuing my work.

As far as you, me, and everyone else in The Backrooms are concerned from this point onwards, Iris died from an unknown assailant who stealthily breached Base gamma. Research is still ongoing to determine who it was and what their motives were. That is what your team will write on the report that you will submit to me. I trust that you will follow what I have just said word for word.

No mention of the Masked Maidens shall be included in this report, and you shall not speak of them at any point going onwards. It is imperative that we do not reveal anything about them to the public; I will handle them myself.

As for you, this conversation never happened, and it would be a damn shame if it did.

Overseer-C
