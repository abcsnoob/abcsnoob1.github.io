BRIMSTONE INC.
Frontrooms Canon » BRIMSTONE INC.




About Us

Globally Active, Innovative, Efficient, Environmentally Conscious.

Brimstone Inc. is the world's most powerful conglomerate, the second global power, and the first non-state member of the United Nations. With our 17 million people employed worldwide, scattered around our offices spanning 112 countries, we forged our place in the daily lives of people around the globe by actively assisting humanity. As a matter of fact, we even collaborate with governments to make the world a better place.

Below are the three megacorporations responsible for the Conglomerate's greatness:

BRIMSTONE HOLDING
BRIMSTONE HOLDING

Founder: Winston Brimstone (1854 - 1940)
Former CEO: Archie Brimstone (1940 - 2022)
Current CEO: Caesar Brimstone

Caesar Brimstone, our current CEO, has chosen to remain out of the public eye for personal reasons. Instead, all public and media communications are handled by Karin Wong, our corporation's spokesperson. No one outside his inner circle has ever seen Caesar’s face, as he grants in-person meetings only to a carefully selected few.

"A new era awaits us."

Brimstone Holding is the most powerful corporation of the Conglomerate, which share our name as we were the driving force behind its formation. As a result, the chairman position of the Conglomerate has always been occupied by a member of the Brimstone family.

Because we were the first to exploit the Backrooms, our importance and influence have grown exponentially, and we now surpass nations like the European Union and China in terms of resources, industrial and military means.

The company’s success would not have been possible without the Brimstone family whose unwavering leadership has enabled the corporation, the Conglomerate, and humanity to new heights- and will continue to do so in the future.

Subsidiaries

  • Brimstone Industries
  • Brimstone LABS
  • Brimstone Construction
  • Brimstone Logistics
  • Brimstone SEC

Brimstone Industries

Director: Carter Sovereign
Total Gross Revenue: $23 400 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: Over 12 millions employees.
Careers: Factory Operators, Farmers, Miners, Industrial Engineers.
Motto: "Resources are the Fuel of Progress."

Brimstone is historically known for being an industrial and mining giant, focusing its efforts on the extraction of minerals such as coal, iron, oil, and semiconductors alongside the production of various materials. This is why Brimstone Industries, the first ever corporation of the Brimstone family, is the most important subsidiary of the corporation. They have also established themselves strongly in the agricultural sector, owning hundreds of thousands of hectares of land and being the number one producer of wheat and beef.

As soon as Brimstone has succeeded in developing solutions capable of harnessing the materials and resources of the Backrooms, Brimstone Industries launched massive agricultural operations within Level 10, also known as "The Fields of Wheat"; water extraction operations within Level 37, also known as "The Poolrooms"; and mining operations across the Backrooms to extract Firesalt and other minerals.

Brimstone LABS

Director: Karin Wong
Total Gross Revenue: $11 100 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: 61 517 employees.
Careers: Scientists, Mathematicians, Physicians, Chemists, Surgeons, Doctors, IT Experts, IA Engineers, Radio & Telecommunications Experts.
Motto: "For a Better Future."

Brimstone LABS is the second largest subsidiary and is what makes Brimstone a pioneer in innovation, focusing its efforts on technological, pharmaceutical, and environmental solutions.

The director of Brimstone LABS, Karin Wong, who is also the spokesperson of our company, is a renowned Chinese scientist and formerly director of the CERN. She joined Brimstone by herself after seeing the potential of our corporation. What's more, her presence on social media made her a worldwide celebrity and has won the hearts of the public for being the "genius savior of humanity." Moreover, Karin has also maintained strong ties with her home country, China, which in turn has helped the Conglomerate sustain positive relations with the BRICS nations.

Working closely with the W.C.O. after the Conglomerate was accepted as a member of the United Nations, Brimstone LABS shifted a major part of its efforts in the study of the new dimension, including its various minerals for their potential of being a more ecological and energy-efficient alternative to fossil fuels and electricity. But also Almond Water and its potential to cure diseases and cancers with no known treatment.

Brimstone Construction

Director: Isaac Irons
Total Gross Revenue: $9 874 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: Over 3.5 millions employees.
Careers: Construction Engineers, Construction Operators, Construction Workers, Architects, Designers, Electricians, Maintenance Workers.
Motto: "Building Tomorrow."

Brimstone Construction is historically the second corporation created by the Brimstone family and helped Europe rebuild after the two World Wars. Today, Brimstone Construction is the leader in buildings and public works. Their innovative view of architecture and engineering redefined the design of cities and buildings with a major focus on ergonomics and eco-resonsibility. With the discovery of new materials from the Backrooms, Brimstone Construction hopes to push innovation even further, possibly breaking the limit of what is known.

Brimstone Logistics

Director: Alysson Irons
Total Gross Revenue: $8 274 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: 106 008 employees.
Careers: Logistic Specialists (Delivery and Logistics).
Motto: "Already at your door."

Brimstone Logistics can be represented as the backbone of the corporation, responsible for the smooth running of Brimstone's operations by managing its resources and the multitude of contracts between the Conglomerate and the various corporations and governments. In collaboration with Delta Holding, Brimstone Logistics also offers BtoC services such as drone deliveries, grocery deliveries, and more recently, with the Backrooms' discovery, storage solutions for customers and waste disposal companies. Thanks to them, the world will become a healthier place.

Brimstone Security

Director: Helias Garisson
Total Gross Revenue: $1 457 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: 248 936 employees.
Careers: Armed Security Guards, Security Operators, Bodyguards, Task Force Operators, Recon and Surveillance Operators, Radio & Telecommunications Specialists.
Motto: "Secure Tomorrow."

Commonly abbreviated as Brimstone SEC, Brimstone Security is a global security, defense, and arms giant. They also constitute the Conglomerate's private security and its paramilitary task force for independent military operations.

Brimstone SEC is led by General Helias Garisson, who was himself a former general of the US military. Despite the Conglomerate's neutrality regarding political conflicts involving the world's nations, NATO often collaborates with Brimstone in its operations in the Backrooms. However, Brimstone SEC is sometimes subject to arguments about its "different" way of proceeding. But as far as we are concerned, efficiency comes before bureaucracy.

A notable innovation of Brimstone SEC is an AI-powered surveillance system capable of predicting threats in advance. Demonstrating the reliability of this technology, it has already been adopted by several countries.

After the discovery of the Backrooms, Brimstone SEC now offers private security contracts for other corporations wishing to operate inside the Backrooms, but also for those who wish to explore this new dimension in total safety.


HORIZON CORPORATION
HORIZON CORPORATION

Founder: Peter McCready (1949-2006)
Current CEO: Richard McCready

"The world in your pocket."

Director: Richard McCready
Total Gross Revenue: $429 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: 135 447 employees
Careers: Field Explorers, Field Researchers, Experts in Anomalous Science
Motto: "The world in your pocket."

The McCready family is a long-standing ally of the Brimstone family, offering mutual support on many occasions. Initially, Horizon was only a collective of explorers, whose goal was to discover the secrets of planet Earth by sending explorers to the four corners of the globe, from the pyramids of Egypt to various archaeological sites and underground caves in South America, Africa and Asia, as well as the Bermuda Triangle. However, with limited means at the time, they were never able to find anything concrete and ended up refocusing their activities after the end of WW2. Finally, the W.C.O. resumed Horizon's unfinished research in 2010.

Today, thanks to our support, they have become the leading travel company and the owners of several aerial and space exploration companies. However, after learning about the existence of the Backrooms through the W.C.O. in its early days, they abruptly canceled all space programs and redirected nearly all their focus to this newly discovered dimension, drawn by its seemingly limitless potential. Due to their past history, they now form the Backrooms' exploratory branch of the Conglomerate. Horizon is now at the forefront of the Conglomerate operations conducted inside the Backrooms, often collaborating with Brimstone SEC.

On certain occasions, the W.C.O. may also call upon Horizon's services.

DELTA HOLDING
DELTA HOLDING

Founder: Stanislas Peterson (1972 - Present)
Current CEO: Stanislas Peterson

"All your desires at your reach."

Delta Holding is an American megacorporation and the leader of the retail and food sector, owning the biggest retail chains. They joined the Conglomerate after being convinced by the benefits of corporate collaboration.

Delta Malls

Director: Maeva Peterson
Total Gross Revenue: $12 700 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: 348 647 employees
Motto: "All your desires at your reach."

Their subsidiary, which manages the biggest chain of superstores in the world, including Walmart, IKEA, Auchan, Carrefour, Costco, 7-Eleven, and many more. They launched the trend of "Mega Malls" with the introduction of "Delta Malls", whose sizes can be compared to entire cities. Those malls host all kinds of stores, hence their slogan: "All your desires at reach". They also collaborate with Brimstone Logistics for their drone delivery services to simplify the lives of many.

Since the exploitation of the Backrooms began, they have been commercializing various products from this alternate dimension directly to the general public.

Delta Clothing

Director: Maverick Peterson
Total Gross Revenue: $6 717 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: 57 164 employees
Motto: "Stand out from the Others, Wear our Clothes"

Their subsidiary, which manages the biggest clothing brands in the world, including Adidas, H&M, LVMH, Nike, and many more.

Delta Restaurants

Director: Matt Peterson
Total Gross Revenue: $10 300 billion (Fiscal Year 2025)
Personnel: 299 843 employees
Motto: "All your desires on your plate."

Their subsidiary, which manages a multitude of restaurants, bakery and fast food chains.



Our Objectives


Because of the multitude of crises and the inevitable death of our civilization in the near future, we used to focus all our efforts on space exploration in the hope of finding a new planet for humanity to inhabit. But our plans have radically shifted away from space to the “Backrooms" since we learned that the World Cryptoscience Organization was conducting research about a new dimension.

Indeed, with the number of missing persons filed spiking in the state of Florida and the islands of the Caraïbe, it has been determined that the only logical explanation would be the Bermuda triangle, being the only area in the world where every attempt of exploration failed. It wasn't until the W.C.O. made the astonishing discovery of this impossible environment defying all known laws of physics, that we discovered what the Bermuda Triangle had been hiding from us all this time, finally explaining all the anomalous phenomena around this region in the same occasion.

Naturally, this discovery piqued the interest of every major government in the world. But as a member of the United Nations ourselves since June 2020, the Brimstone Conglomerate became the first private entity authorized by the W.C.O. to operate within the Backrooms. What's more, we were the first to develop fully functional solutions to exploit the many resources of this dimension, before all other corporations and global powers.

Today, we firmly believe that the Backrooms hold the key to ushering humanity into a new era.



New Elysium


Our Headquarters.

In 2017, we built "New Elysium", the first ever city and state governed by corporations.

Located in the middle of the Atlantic, New Elysium is the epicenter of technology and innovation.

This is where our headquarters are located, and this is where the future lies.

If you wish to become a citizen of New Elysium, you need to be employed by one of our corporations.

Join us in our journey to make the world a better place.

We are waiting for you.

Recent News


Two Years Ago



At only 31 years old, he is the second most influential person in the world, just behind the President of the United States.


Mr. Brimstone, it is a great honor to welcome you for this exceptional interview. First of all, we would like to thank you, as it's the first time you have accepted an interview ever since you became the new CEO of the Brimstone conglomerate! What suddenly made you change your mind?

"The pleasure is all mine. I agreed to your interview to demonstrate my honesty. Because my father was murdered by those terrorists from Iscariot, I've been told to maintain my public anonymity, and it's rather a privilege that I intend to keep. Indeed, since no one has ever been aware of my existence until my father passed away. As a result, no one other than my closest entourage ever seen my face. However, despite this habit I've developed, I am above all, a man of principle and transparency. I could simply send you my dear Karin Wong, but today, I personally want to clear up any misunderstandings surrounding myself and the conglomerate."

Thank you for the explanation Mr. Brimstone. We're sure people wanted to know why you always avoid the cameras! Well, there's a lot we'd like to know as well. Starting with your status. You see, you could technically be the richest person in the world. Right now, your company, Brimstone Holding, is worth $110 trillion alone, which is almost equivalent to the total value of US assets. You could even be richer than all European Union countries combined and countries like China! But your total personal wealth is only $41 million. Why this decision?

"My father taught me that money equals power. But what's the point of money if you spend it on useless consumer goods? My view of wealth is different. Money shouldn't be spent on extravagant objects, but rather as a means of exercising power. As a matter of fact, $41 million is all I need to live my day-to-day life for the rest of my life. Every profit Brimstone Holding earns contributes directly to the growth of my businesses and those of my collaborators within the Conglomerate. I only invest in what I believe in, and to me, that's the best possible investment. I may sound selfish and harsh, but I sincerely believe that only our conglomerate can change the world today. Because making the world a better place requires not only ideas but also money. Otherwise, you can only dream, and as far as I'm concerned, I never dream, I act."

So you're really like your father after all! And about New Elysium? We heard you and the Conglomerate paid a large amount of money, and with a large amount, We're obviously talking in billions.

"Indeed, not only did the Conglomerate had to shell out a significant sum, but so did I. I'm a 64% shareholder, which means I virtually own this city- but I consider this an exception. This isn't a personal investment or a purchase to satisfy my own desires, but rather an investment in the future. New Elysium was built with the goal of maximizing corporate efficiency: no government, no regulation, just pure business. Today, just look at the results, six years after its creation."

Our experts predict that New Elysium will surpass the Silicon Valley next year. Now, let's hope they are right! And for the last question, could you tell us more about Azure Corporation? We've heard that your relationship is somewhat strained.

"Well, we've tried to negotiate with them, even being extremely generous on the terms, but it seems they have no intention of doing business with us. And I find it highly… unprofessional to do things behind our backs, especially by sending their moles into our companies. They should be ashamed of themselves for using such petty tactics. I still don't know their true intentions, but I will personally make sure that we find out as soon as possible."

Thank you very much Mr. Brimstone, we can stop there for today. You told us more than we expected already! To conclude, do you have anything else to add maybe?

"To those who've been taught the contrary, or influenced. The conglomerate is your friend, not your enemy. The true enemy is the terrorists from Iscariot and all those who voluntarily become an obstacle to progress."


Forbes, edition of April 07, 2023.


This Year




Source: The BMS Olympus deployed in the Bermuda Triangle's region.

Tension at the Bermuda Triangle.

Brimstone's 5th and 6th fleet has been deployed.


"With the increase in attacks from Iscariot in the region, we had no choice but to intervene - we had to increase our military presence. This is why I asked reinforcements from our 5th and 6th naval fleets as well as the BMS Olympus, our own aircraft supercarrier. The duration of these measures will depend on the severity of the situation. Our world depends on this unique location, and we will defend it to the last drop of our blood."
says General Helias Garisson, Director of Brimstone Security.

As the first ever non-state member to occupy a seat at the United Nations, the Brimstone Conglomerate is attempting to prove that they are not only an industrial and economic superpower, but also a military superpower! Can we really trust a private company the way we trust our democratic governments? What if the Conglomerate suddenly changes its vision and policies? Several national leaders are questioning themselves about this uncertainty after the Conglomerate's recent decisions in the Bermuda Triangle region.

However, one thing is certain: the United States of America is convinced of Brimstone's ability to make our world a better place. Indeed, president Donald Trump just spoke this morning and made a comment about the situation:

"In a world where resources are limited, we have deemed the Conglomerate's operations in the Backrooms essential to humanity's subsistance. Indeed, nearly every conflicts of interest have ended and numerous global crises have been resolved since the Brimstone Conglomerate's intervention. The steady supply of food, energy, and basic necessities is what maintains world peace."

The New York Times, edition of March 30, 2025.


Yesterday



The War is Declared!

The Backrooms Colonists launches hostilities against the Brimstone Conglomerate.


Source: US Marines deployed to assist the Brimstone Conglomerate in Level 10: "The Fields of Wheat".

It's been two months day for day since the war between the Brimstone Conglomerate and the Backrooms Colonists officially began after multiple guerrilla operations by the latter caused the destruction of several Brimstone infrastructures. Despite the Conglomerate's known neutrality, NATO and the United States insisted that they assist the Conglomerate in the war. Indeed, we just received information that the US president sent American soldiers to help defend the various Brimstone's installations.

According to a press release from US army general John MacArthur, the scale of this war is unprecedented. Spanning hundreds of different levels, this war resembles a game of hide-and-seek more than the conventional warfare we were used to, in the confined space of planet Earth.

For context, at the summit held by the World Cryptoscience Organization in October, it was reported that the Backrooms population was warning the Conglomerate about the dangers of massive exploitation- that it could threaten the structure and integrity of these lands. This led to a temporary suspension of the Conglomerate's operations in the Backrooms under the direct order of the International Court of Justice. However, it was ultimately determined that the consequences would not be so serious. With 68% of the vote in favor and the public's support, the Conglomerate was allowed to continue its activities within the Backrooms and its war with the Backrooms Colonists was justified and supported.

"After all, the world needs these resources more than anything else. Reality may be brutal, but all obstacles to progress must and will be removed. Sacrifices should be made to achieve world peace. It's either our world or theirs; now it's up to you to decide." adds Caesar Brimstone, president of the Conglomerate.

Le Monde, edition of December 23, 2025.


Diplomatic Relations

ALLIES

THE UNITED NATIONS
THE UNITED NATIONS



The United Nations comprise 194 sovereign states including one non-state member, and is the world's largest intergovernmental organization. All members have equal representation in the UN General Assembly.

THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA
THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA



Due to our history, we maintain excellent relations with the United States of America, but these are limited to the economic sphere. However, as far as the Backrooms are concerned, the United States of America fully supports our cause and the operations we conduct within this dimension.

Currently, they are the first world power just ahead of us.

THE EUROPEAN UNION
THE EUROPEAN UNION



Just like the USA, our conglomerate and the countries of the European Union maintain excellent relations due to our history, but are also limited to the economic sphere.

Currently, they are the fourth world power behind China.

THE MAJOR EXPLORER GROUP
THE MAJOR EXPLORER GROUP



The Major Explorer Group was the largest group in the Backrooms prior to our arrival, but disbanded shortly afterwards. Because apparently, their main objective was to find a way out of this dimension, they all returned to their home without hesitation as a result. To this day, many of their former members were offered golden opportunities by both the W.C.O. and the Conglomerate, mainly for their in-depth knowledge and experience of the Backrooms. As for the former leaders of the M.E.G., the Zimmals siblings and Andrew Donovan were awarded the Medal of Honor for their service and the many humanitarian actions that saved the lives of millions inside the Backrooms.

NEUTRAL

THE WORLD CRYPTOSCIENCE ORGANIZATION
THE WORLD CRYPTOSCIENCE ORGANIZATION



The World Cryptoscience Organization (W.C.O.) is the global organization responsible for the discovery of the Backrooms, where the United Nations sends its top scientists. While not all, the vast majority of UN member states, including ourselves, hold a seat within the W.C.O.

We maintain excellent relations with the other members of the organization. However, unlike the United Nations, members of the W.C.O. remain strictly neutral on geopolitical matters. Whether on Earth or in the Backrooms.

NATO
NATO



The North Atlantic Treaty Organization unites North America and the countries of the European Union. Because of our history, we maintain great relations with the countries of NATO. However, due to our policies, we remain a neutral entity as far as the world's politics are concerned, in order to preserve our image as a global benefactor.

BRICS
BRICS



The BRICS unites Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa and many other countries, created primarily for economic purposes. Our relations with China and Russia are particularly good, which by consequent, allowed us to be trusted by the other BRICS member countries.

ASEAN
ASEAN



The Association of South-East Asian Nations (ASEAN) unites Indonesia, Singapore, Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, Philippines, Myanmar, Cambodia, Laos and Brunei. Created for economic, political, and cultural purposes, this alliance opposes the NATO and has close relations with the BRICS.

AAL
AAL



The AAL, short for Afro-Arab League, brings together most African and Arab countries with the aim of overcoming their difficulties and competing in the ongoing industrial race in the Backrooms. Their close relationship with Azure Corporation creates mistrust between us and the members of league, but we have decided it's best for us to simply avoid them.

ENEMIES

ISCARIOT
ISCARIOT



Iscariot is a syndicate of religious fanatics historically opposed to progress and capitalism, primarily affiliated with the Catholic Church. Intelligence indicates that they have been active since the 18th century. Once relatively passive, it seems that their flame was reignited after the announcement of the Backrooms' discovery. Since then, the syndicate have adopted a far more aggressive stance, regularly conducting terrorist operations against the W.C.O. and corporations associated with the Conglomerate. They ruthlessly murder our staff, sabotage our infrastructure, and undermine our efforts toward a better future. All under the pretense that the Backrooms are a "Holy Land" that must remain untouched.

They are the ones responsible for the death of Archie Brimstone, our beloved founder.
Therefore, every member of Iscariot must and will be eliminated.

AZURE CORPORATION
AZURE CORPORATION



Azure Corporation is a rival megacorporation based in Switzerland and popular for their humanitarian and environmental actions. They are our main competitor in the field of science and technology and, apart from us, the only private company with contacts at the W.C.O., which are mainly African and Arab governments as well as other struggling countries that previously benefited or still benefit from their aid.

However, since the beginning of the war, we suspect them of corporate espionnage and other questionable activities against us. Attempts have been made to negotiate terms with them, but their executive committee seems somewhat stubborn. As a result, we recently launched an investigation against them and discovered that they were collaborating with the Backrooms Colonists and other communities of the Backrooms.

In the context of our war with the Backrooms Colonists, they are therefore considered a threat to the Conglomerate. They know that we cannot engage in military retaliation because they do not have an army like we do. We will therefore be forced to resolve this problem through diplomatic actions.

THE BACKROOMS COLONISTS
THE BACKROOMS COLONISTS



The Backrooms Colonists is an organization made of peoples who have decided to stay in the Backrooms for various reasons, often because they feel they no longer belong in the real world.

The organization was created by the B.N.T.G. after the M.E.G. left the Backrooms to return to Earth. They are determined to protect the Backrooms at the cost of their lives like it is their "homeland", and to achieve that, they carry out sabotage and guerilla operations against us, similarly to how Iscariot proceed. Attempts have been made to reach a peace agreement, but to no avail. As a result, they declared a war against us in spite of ourselves, therefore, we had no choice but to designate them as a direct threat until further notice.

THE BACKROOMS ROBOTICS
THE BACKROOMS ROBOTICS



They are the technology and weapons supplier of The Backrooms Colonists. Due to their indirect involvement in our war against the latter, they are also designated as our enemies.

THE LOST
THE LOST



Their alliance with the Backrooms Colonists make them our enemies.

SUMMARY

Brimstone Inc. is the world's largest conglomerate which unites several megacorporations. The Conglomerate is an economic, industrial and also a military superpower, being the second world power just behind the USA. They even were granted a seat at the United Nation and the W.C.O. as the first ever non-state member.

Despite having a very good public image due to their humanitarian and active involvement in solving the world's problem in the "Frontrooms", they are the main threat when it comes to their actions involving the Backrooms and its populations, explained by their ignorance about the consequences from their exploitation operations, as they view the Backrooms as a disposable asset.

The population who decided to stay in the Backrooms tried to warn the Conglomerate about the dangers of this mass exploitation for the Backrooms' structural integrity but it only made the Conglomerate persecuting them and justify it for being "an obstacle to progress". The United Nations are aware of this but let this slide and rather support the Conglomerate because they desperately need these resources to "make the world a better place" and keeping the world peace, achieved thanks to the Backrooms' "unlimited" resources.

The Backrooms' remaining GOIs such as the Backrooms Colonists, the Backrooms Robotics and the Lost are the main opposition to the Conglomerate as well as all the corporate and governmental entities exploiting the Backrooms. Azure Corporation and Iscariot, on the Frontrooms side, also oppose the Conglomerate's methods due to their beliefs and wishes to bring down the Brimstone empire.
