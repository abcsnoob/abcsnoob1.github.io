CYANBEACHZONE

[ > Enter. ]

> Hi !! I'm BEACH. I use he/him pronouns, and I'm an artist around these parts.
Welcome to my CYAN ZONE !! This is an archive of some of my Backrooms-related artworks. I promise there wasn't much here before—just this big foaming sea back here and these corroded COMMAND TOWERS I haven't a clue what to do with. Hardly a good place to grow anything in, especially not palm trees.

Anyway.

Besides the readily apparent interest, I've stuck around SCRATCH way longer than any self-respecting artist should be, and continue to obsess over (and make) obscure animated series lore. I have also logged more hours of YUME 2KKI than any video game I have ever paid money for.

What follows is a collection of my Backrooms art, organized in each category by (roughly!) chronological order. Browse as you wish !!
Also, just in case— slight eyestrain & blood warnings for some of these pieces !!
