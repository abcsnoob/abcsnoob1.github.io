Author's Notes:

Explanations of my creations by yours truly.

Level 230 - The Port of Misgivings

This Level is more or less a story. It takes the semblance of a dark and dim port with unseen mysteries and dangers. In the safe parts it provides a multitude of trading opportunities, just beware of scammers in the form of Baggers. The dangerous parts, no telling what lies in them, and even if you could know chances are you wouldn't want to.

Level 171 - A Grand Illusion

Level 171 is a level of illusions and hidden dangers. In these harsh conditions, anyone who is unfortunate enough to enter this level doesn't last long. Between non-euclidean geometry, changing geography, illusions, and vicious entities, this level is not one to be explored even by the most experienced of persons.

Level Concept - Hold Your Horses

I really haven't gotten much about this level figured out. It's still very much in development. What I can say about it is its basic premise. It takes place in a sort of midway with all kinds of rides and games; even a carousel. Most of the games are operated by non-hostile entities known as Facelings, but on rare occasions wanderers may see hostile ones such as Jesters. It's a really intriguing idea and I look forward to exploring it with you guys.

Entity 230 - Baggers

These entities were written to go with Level 230. As my "firstborns", I have a strange attachment to them. In my mind, these entities are really intriguing. They're these things with these oversized bags eager to make a sketchy sale with all who cross their path. I'm really excited to say that I have big plans upcoming for these creatures.

Entity 231 - Porters

As the most dangerous known entity on Level 230, Porters tend to be what makes Level 230 as dangerous as it is. They're these sort of beings who wear masks and work gear. They appear to service ships in the docks. Anyway, check out their page.

Entity Concept - V3c70rs

I don't have much to say about this entity at the moment. What I can say is that they're a part of the future Level 171.

Entity Concept - Signal Lights

Oh boy, oh boy am I glad to finally share this with you guys! As the first official entity page for Level 171 and an exclusive entity, it adds to the mystery of the level. These entities produce the infamous illusions that Level 171 is known for. While they may look like ordinary lights they add a level of danger. Go explore these entities and see what they have to offer!
