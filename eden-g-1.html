Eden G

rating: +50+–x

PREVIOUS NAME: Eden G██████████

M.E.G. TITLE: Head Field Researcher

LAST CONFIRMED LOCATION: (Heaven)

The only confirmed image of Eden since she went missing. Note the strange orbs and her new form.

Description:

Eden G. was a former Head Field Researcher of the M.E.G. and close friend of Former Senior Archivist A. River. Eden was last heard from in a call with Overseer B, which is logged on this database entry.

Eden was a light-skinned woman of Japanese-American descent. All pictures of her that were taken before her trip to heaven have since been lost, so her exact details are only documented through written reports. She had short pink hair, and would often be seen wearing traditional clothing and hairstyles. She was an entity expert, and even learned how to communicate with certain peaceful entities after intense study. She was promoted to Head Field Researcher after teaching multiple Facelings how to use sign language, which she was greatly congratulated for. If not on a mission, she would spend most of her spare time studying in the Cygnus Archive.

Eden was one of the main field mission leaders. She would often lead missions focused on entity documentation. She always had a specific philosophy and personality that made her feel trusted by the entities she studied. While many see entities as monsters, Eden always looked at them as her equal — even the dangerous “murder-monsters” of the Backrooms. She resided on Level 11 before her trip to heaven. She would often be seen going on research missions with A. River, and would spend lots of time with hir outside of missions as well.

A log is attached below of Eden’s final documented communication with The M.E.G.

Last known call with Eden. Log ██/██/████ +
Last known call with Eden. Log ██/██/████ -

BEGIN CALL LOG

This log documents the final conversation that Eden had with any known M.E.G. operative.

Eden: Hello?

Overseer B: Eden? This is Overseer B. We need to talk.

Eden: What’s wrong?

Overseer B: (She pauses and takes a very deep, shaky breath.) River’s gone, Eden.

Eden: I’m aware. Ze went on that trip to paradise, was it?

Overseer B: Yeah. Ze did— and we have reason to believe that was hir last mission.

Eden: What?

Overseer B: Me and the other overseers have been trying to contact hir, but we haven’t been able to. Ze told us if ze didn’t contact us within a week, that ze wasn’t coming back.

Eden: (Eden pauses) I don’t know what kind of sick joke this is—

Overseer B: I wish it was, but it’s not. It’s been almost two weeks, and River hasn’t come back. M.E.G. policy is to declare hir dead.

Eden: No… No… (Eden begins to sob uncontrollably)

Overseer B: I’m sorry. Everyone is just as upset as you are.

Eden: No! I don’t care what the policy is! Send every single field researcher we have! We can’t just leave hir to die like that!

Overseer B: Ze was a friend of mine too, but we… The M.E.G. can’t lose more people. We’ve already lost enough. Just go to The Snackrooms, have a drink—

Eden: No. I’m not drinking this one away. If you truly cared about hir, you would exhaust every possible resource until we found hir.

Overseer B: Eden, we just lost River. If we lost another… We won’t lose another! Everyone from The M.E.G. is upset, but that does not give an excuse to sacrifice more lives because of the loss of one.

Eden: And you did so well at making sure the first loss didn’t happen. I guess since you’re more concerned with your own duties, I’ll take care of the situation myself. Don’t pull your ‘it’s too dangerous’ crap; you couldn’t keep me from going even if you tried.

Overseer B: Eden wa—

The call was abruptly cut off by Eden. This was the last documented record of Eden before her trip to 404. No further information has been found.


After this call, Overseer B spent countless days and research teams searching as many levels as possible looking for Eden. These efforts proved fruitless. Eden has since been declared dead.

Welcome Overseer

Stretch, Andrew, this message is for overseer eyes only. We have absolutely no idea what is going on with Eden or where she is. She’s not dead — I think — but she’s definitely not the Eden we once knew. She seems to be able to… uh… warp the reality of her body now? I just can’t describe it. We can’t let the public know that. The M.E.G. is supposed to have answers, not questions. This whole thing with River and Eden has been full of question after question. We just have to get this article out to suppress the rumors. Too many people have been asking what happened to the one that taught Facelings to speak.

From what we saw, Eden seems to be able to change her appearance by some unknown means. We even caught a picture of the occurrence. She just… approached the person, grabbed their backpack, and before we knew it, she became them. I don’t know what happened, but this information is too important to be left public. The last thing we need is panic. She, how do I describe this, fed off of their energy. We heard her whispering something about memories and nostalgia. What did that place do to her? That was definitely Eden. It just wasn’t the Eden I know. What was I thinking? This is my fault. I should’ve never let them go there…

I’ve been able to scrounge a few of her possible identities together for us to look at, but I’m not sure about any of it. I’m not even sure if these people are the same person or not. Some could be Eden, all of them could be Eden, none of them could be Eden. I just don’t know, but it’s the best we have for right now.
