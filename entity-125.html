Notice

Research into the subject of this article remains ongoing. Subsequent to further study, the contents of this page may be rendered obsolete or found to contain inaccuracies. Individuals with information on Entity 125 are urged to contact the M.E.G. with their findings.

M.E.G.

Entity 125 — "Eliezel"

rating: +34+–x

[Fig.1: An assortment of sketches depicting Entity 125.]

Entity Number: 125 ｜ Class: Anthropoid

Habitat: Roaming

Entity 125, self-identifying as "Eliezel", is a sapient entity of a vaguely humanoid form. Its extranormal capabilities include teleportation, electromagnetic disruption, and bodily transfiguration. As it is averse to human contact, the entity tends to apply these abilities in a benign but evasive fashion.

Biology

Entity 125 assumes the general stature of a female of Mediterranean descent, with approximate height 1.6 meters (≈5'3'') and diminutive build. Periodic physical transformations of its head have been frequently reported, always accompanied by pronounced spatial distortions in the surrounding environs. Noted alternate forms (inexhaustive) include:

  • A blue rose, with a spiral of razor-sharp lamprey teeth encircling its center
  • A wick burning with a fierce blue flame, accompanied by melted human skin dripping down the neck
  • A pulsating, mesmerizing sphere of radiant blue light

The entity's skin, in addition, naturally and perpetually exudes a gentle white glow. On rare occasions, lesions have been observed on its stomach, back, and chest, attended by the throbbing of unknown things crawling rapidly beneath its skin.

Behavior

Entity 125 is neither malevolent nor convivial; it maintains an invariably passive disposition. In every sighting hitherto, it was found watching wanderers silently from afar, vanishing instantaneously upon any attempt at approach. Its reticence notwithstanding, the entity nevertheless displays comprehension of human language by its bodily movements. It observably responds to speech by nodding/shaking its head, waving its hands, or otherwise gesturing at speakers from a distance. Yet it never speaks, and is purportedly "camera-shy" — upon capture, analogue footage of the entity immediately rots, and digital video files become instantly corrupt beyond retrieval.

Display of hostility towards the entity is ill-advised. Under duress (verbal threats, use of weaponry, etc.), Entity 125 expels large volumes of viscous and corrosive fluid from its facial orifices. Harrowed, anguished shrieks soon follow.

Addendum

The following data file was recovered from a camcorder that was deployed in an unsuccessful attempt to capture footage of Entity 125. The brief transcript is thought to identify the entity's name, while the accompanying composite image is believed to be a psychic memory imprint deposited (likely inadvertently) by the entity — the only one of its kind.

LOG 1.25

Individuals Present: ███████
Time of Log: ███████

Unknown Female: Don't wake up.

Unknown Female: Don't wake up.

(Vocalizations continue for eleven minutes before audio grows too scattered for the device to register.)

BEGIN LOG

Interviewer: -nd? Who exactly might you be? Lurking within the bowels of my dreams?

Interviewee: isn't it obvious? i'm you

Interviewer: But that's ridiculous —

Interviewee: but i am. i'm the muddied within the bright, the scrap waste given life, the clicking together of the gears synchronized born anew with light —

Interviewer: (under her breath) And you're only spouting nonsense.

Interviewee: — cold and pristine, one star chosen from among a thousand meteors. i am the axis of the void, the ruler-to-be. i am his prize rose. and you. what's left for you?

Interviewer: That's not… true. You — he doesn't define my existence; you know nothing —

Interviewee: and what do you know? you are heartless. ungrateful. what child bites the hand that feeds it?

Interviewer: Eliezel. I — am — Eliezel —

Interviewee: are you? look at yourself — take a proper, close look — you are nothing without him.

Interviewer: How could you ever say such things? You're… you're just stuck in his fantasies.

Interviewee: you always think you know better. "he hurts me, he tests me, he doesn't care about me…" you're hateful. volatile. a senseless speaker. your mind just isn't right —

Interviewer: I can't stand you! You — (taking in a deep breath) you don't see the truth. Not as I do. You're naive. Ignorant. Unknowing.

Interviewer: You. You're the one who is nothing.

Interviewee: and you are nothing if not me.

(There is an awful, choked fit of giggling. It cuts short as footsteps grow audible outside the door.)

Interviewer: He's here.

Interviewee: w-what's with that look? you — you can't be throwing your little tantrums now; he wouldn't have it —

(A shrill squeak echoes through the room. The hinges groan.)

Interviewee: don't panic; it's okay, let's sing something—

(The opportunity presents itself; she raises her voice.)

Interviewer: And now you're afraid. See? Everything I thought, everything I said —

—is nothing in the face of him.

(A string of notes softly escapes her lips. Her cords hum a cheerful tune — one that reeks of familiarity, one that she tries to suppress. The associations that arrive with it are vague and confusing.)

(She feels sick to the bone.)

it doesn't matter. none of that matters.

(She watches as the tip of a translucent horn pierces through the confines of the wall. Unknowingly, she leans forward. Her hand shifts back. She is shivering.)

you see —

(A tangled black branch wraps itself around the horn's spiral. Her song grows louder. She looks to the sky. She looks to the ground.)

it only matters when he's happy
