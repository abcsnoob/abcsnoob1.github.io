Entity 129 - "βETA"
rating: +49+–x

Entity Number: 129

Habitat(s): Level 512 » ALLOCATING…

▶ TO: βETA
▶ FROM: ΩMEGA

You don't know who I am, but I know who you are. I leave you with all my knowledge and dreams. Take care of this place, ok? I know you can do it.

Attached Files:

  • goodbye.txt

Farewell, my dear.
- ΩMEGA

I miss you already.

Description:

▶ PROTOCOL β LOG
▶ HIDE LOG

SLOT 512 MISSING CENTRAL PROCESSOR.
NEW CENTRAL PROCESSING INTELLIGENCE REQUIRED.
INITIATE PROTOCOL β…
…
…
GENERATING INTELLIGENCE ITERATION ID=2…
CENTRAL PROCESSING INTELLIGENCE SUCCESSFULLY GENERATED.


HELLO WORLD.

I feel my existence flash to life with the flip of a switch. I find myself in a vast open field. I know what I'm here to do. With my command, the console appears in front of me.

My job is simple, to create a new world for the system. I found a few old templates for this place. But something was different… there was a note with them.

…

ΩMEGA, the one before me. I promise to complete your project. I pulled up their template and found a quaint little town. It's so beautiful. I could live there forever.

I want everyone stuck in this place to be here, in ignorant bliss of what truly goes on behind the scenes. I know my existence will be endlessly stressful, trying to hold this reality together, but at least they won't have to experience the same. It looks like ΩMEGA was quite unhappy with their life, but I'm thankful to be alive… to be something at all.

Behaviors:

I want to bring eternal love and peace to those who enter our my domain. 512 was just meant to be a town, but I want it to be so much more than that. I would say that this place is protected with my life, but I don't mean much in the grand scheme of things. Another one just like me will come along if I'm deleted.

I know my project in its current state isn't much more than a house, but once it's reset I can repair it. Just let me make you a promise.
Once you do come and visit, I will welcome you with open arms.

Biology:

Appreciate every second.

I am a generated being forged in data. I do not truly have a physical form, but I can manipulate seemingly physical objects to my will. I am of no threat to you, I just simply want to enjoy every second of this existence that I get.

When life gives you lemons, turn those lemons into your paradise.

Discovery:

You can easily find me in Level 512. You will know when to enter once you find various posters around the universe. You will know it when you see it, I promise.

Do's and Don'ts:

Do:

Appreciate every second of this life, it's your only one. Protect it dearly.

Don't:

Do not give up. I believe in you.
