Entity 137 - "The Musician"
rating: +13+–x

IETS

A quick sketch of The Musician made by a wanderer.

Entity #: 137

Habitat(s) - Majority of levels, The Atelier, Entity 65

Description

The Musician1 is a tall, slender entity that is often seen wearing some form of pinstripe suit and a top hat, although the colors and designs may vary. It usually dons gloves that match their outfit, though is sometimes seen without them. To see a full log of the outfits he has worn, view Outfit Log 137-A.

The Musician is often found wandering around various levels in the Backrooms with his hands folded behind his back. No particular pattern to his travels has been found at this time, so it’s assumed he chooses at random. In levels that have multiple rooms, he can be observed checking each of them, presumably for any wanderers that may need assistance. You can tell when he’s near based off of the sound of music playing. However, it is suggested to listen to the music carefully first, as it could be an entity pretending to be him.

His music has been found to have a sort of "area effect" that makes many hostile entities either docile or completely passive when in his presence. Some entities have been found to avoid the music entirely,2 however, this doesn't work on every hostile entity. On the occasion he comes across an entity its music doesn't have an effect on, he takes extreme precautions, often knowing exactly how to take care of said entity. Anyone he is traveling with that is within this area of effect is not affected by the music. He also seems to possess the ability to no-clip to whatever level or area he wants at will.

Behaviors

He is very friendly to those it encounters unless provoked, offering them Almond Water, food, protection, or even a way out of whatever level they’re in. He’s very sociable and almost always willing to walk with wanderers and chat about most anything, exemplifying a wide array of knowledge in several subjects. His best subjects include music, instruments, and anything pertaining to the Backrooms, according to the Musician himself. However, when asked about anything pertaining to Entity 138, he exhibits a mild level of disgust towards it and doesn’t elaborate further. There are theories that the two entities have some sort of history or familial bond with one another, but as of now there is very little evidence to support it.

He seems to have an awareness of how unsettling he may appear to wanderers, so if anyone is frightened by his presence he’ll apologize and leave them alone. When insulted or spoken to disrespectfully, he will often scold or berate whoever is being rude before leaving them be.

When provoked or attacked, he showcases one of two attack abilities of his. His preferred method is by blasting the music that naturally plays when he’s around to up to 200 decibels, either stunning or completely damaging the hearing of his target(s). His less common, but more effective, method of attack is by transforming one or both of his hands into silver claws to slash and cut at his target and mangle their body beyond recognition. He appears to not like this method due to the grotesque nature of it, often recoiling in visible disgust at the sight of his victim after the fact.

He has demonstrated an impressive level of skill or familiarity with a vast range of instruments and shows an extreme passion for the craft. According to it, he’s more partial to the violin and piano but still loves all kinds of instruments and music. On very rare occasions, he can be found playing some form of instrument. It’s most common to find him performing in Entity 65.

Biology

The Musician’s anatomy is almost identical to a normal human’s biology. He is estimated to be around 6’5-6’10 feet tall (though some claim he may be even taller) and is very slender. He has all organs that a male human would usually have, the majority of which function the same. He has the ability to eat, sleep, drink, and overall perform basic bodily functions, but he does not seem to have as much of a need for them and can go long periods of time without getting hungry, thirsty, or tired.

In addition to this, it is also susceptible to wounds, but appears to have a very high pain tolerance. Their blood is a slightly darker red than human blood, but is the same otherwise. Despite being susceptible to harm, he seems to be impervious to normally fatal attacks, such as decapitation, blood loss, head trauma, and many others.

Discovery

The first recorded evidence of The Musician was made by Cameron Fleetwood in level-20 on May 26th of 1999. A transcript of this encounter can be found under “Interview Logs.”

Audio Logs

There are currently no audio logs available. As recordings are gathered, they will be uploaded here in a timely manner.

Interview Logs

<BEGIN DISCOVERY LOG>

Time: 2:35 PM (14:35)
Recorder: Mobile Phone
Location: Level 20
Interviewer: Cameron Fleetwood
Interviewee: The Musician

+Open Transcript
-Close Transcript

Fleetwood: [Quietly, just above a whisper, music can be heard in the background.] I just heard music playing out of nowhere. Uh… I’m probably going to regret this, but I’m going to investigate it. I’ve made this log just in case something of importance comes out of this. So… Wish me luck.
[The only sounds that can be heard are quiet breathing and steady footsteps. The music gradually gets louder.]
Fleetwood: [still whispering] What the fuck is that?
Um, I just turned a corner, and there's this freakishly tall humanoid looking.. thing, just standing there. It sounds like it may be what’s playing the music? I don't think it's seen me yet..
[A moment of silence before they gasp.]
Fleetwood: Shit!
[Shuffling noises]
Fleetwood: [Even quieter] I think it saw me, fuck fuck fuck-
The Musician: Are you alright, wanderer?
[Fleetwood screams. According to an interview conducted with them afterwards, the entity apparently flinched at this.]
Fleetwood: Shit, fuck, nonononono-
The Musician: Oh dear, I am sincerely sorry for frightening you. I assure you I mean absolutely no harm.
Fleetwood: Wh-
The Musician: Can I offer you anything? I have plenty of things I can- [Fleetwood cuts him off.]
Fleetwood: Why the hell should I trust you?! This could be a trick for all I know!
The Musician: …That could very well be true, but I assure you with full honesty that it is not. I merely would like to chat, is all.
Fleetwood: Get- Get the hell away from me! I’m not going to fall for whatever shit you're pulling here!
[Entity hums, seemingly in disappointment.]
The Musician: I see. If you truly want to be left alone, then so be it. Good luck on your travels, friend.
[Footsteps, slowly gets quieter.]
Fleetwood: It… actually left? What the hell? This is fuckin’ weird… I really need to get out of here.

<END LOG>
<BEGIN INTERVIEW LOG #1>

Time: 10:32 AM
Recorder: Mobile phone
Location: Level 128
Interviewer: Michelle Laurens
Interviewee: The Musician

+Open Transcript
-Close Transcript

Laurens: This is, uh, Michelle Laurens speaking. It is currently… July 27th, 1999. I'm making this log because I just heard music randomly playing. I think it may be the same entity that Cameron encountered. This might be a suicide mission, but I'm gonna investigate and try and conduct an interview with it if I can.
[Feet shuffling noises, music getting louder]
Laurens: Hello? Is… anyone there? [Quieter] I think I see him. I'm gonna get closer.
[The music gets louder.]
Laurens: Hello?
The Musician: Hm? Ah, hello there! Are you feeling alright? Do you need any assistance? Almond water, food, perhaps even an escape?
Laurens: Erm.. No, thank you. I actually was hoping to… conduct an interview, with you?
The Musician: Of course. Do you mind if we walk while conversing?
Laurens: Uh, no..?
The Musician: Very well.
[They begin walking.]
Laurens: Er.. do you have a name?
The Musician: I am Unknown.
Laurens: You don’t know your name?
[The Musician chuckles.]
The Musician: Apologies for the misunderstanding. My name is, quite literally, Unknown. Unknown William Enright. However, if it’d make it easier for you, you can just call me The Musician. And you are?
Laurens: Uh, Michelle Laurens.
Musician: What a wonderful name. Before we continue, what are your pronouns? I personally use he/him/his, they/them/theirs, and it/its/itself.
Laurens: Uhhh… she/her?
The Musician: Good to know. Carry on, ma’am.
Laurens: Okay.. Uhm, so, what… are you, exactly?
The Musician: What do you mean?
Laurens: Like, you seem human, but…
The Musician: Ah, I see. I honestly don’t know that myself, although generally speaking I believe I’d be more human than anything else.
Laurens: Interesting… How long have you been here?
The Musician: I’ve been here for as long as I can remember. The passage of time is hard to gauge here, but I’ve definitely been here for a long, long while.
Laurens: How much do you know about this place?
The Musician: This specific level, or the dimension as a whole?
Laurens: Erm… the dimension as a whole, I guess?
The Musician: Oh, I know several things about this place that you refer to as the “Backrooms.” Most any information you’d like to know, I can provide.
Laurens: Do you know how to leave the Backrooms?
The Musician: Unfortunately, that is one of the few things I do not know. However, I do know how to leave each individual room. The ones that can be left, anyway.
Laurens: Alright then… [A moment of silence as they think.]
The Musician: Is there anything else you’d like to inquire about, Miss Laurens?
Laurens: Uhm… Where does the music come from?
The Musician: I’m not quite sure. I’ve never been able to find the source myself, but I’d assume it’d be from within me.
Laurens: Alright. Well, uh.. Thank you for your time.
The Musician: But of course. If you need anything else, you are more than welcome to ask the next time you find me. Or the next time I find you, whichever happens to come first. Would you like me to lead you out of this level?
Laurens: Erm, sure. Thank you.
The Musician: Of course, my dear.

<END LOG>
<BEGIN INTERVIEW LOG #2>

Time: 12:45 PM
Recorder: Security Camera
Location: MEG Headquarters
Interviewer: Jane Lockwell
Interviewee: The Musician

[Note: Parts of the actual interview have been cut out for brevity. Only parts that answer important questions or provide important information are included.]

+Open Transcript
-Close Transcript

Lockwell: Thank you for coming here, Mr. Enright.
The Musician: Of course, Ms. Lockwell. I’m more than happy to help.
Lockwell: Are you ready to begin the questioning?
The Musician: Ready when you are, my dear.
Lockwell: Alright. First question: How long have you been in the Backrooms?
The Musician: I’m not sure, exactly. I do know I’ve been here for a long, long time. Things have changed quite a bit since my childhood.
Lockwell: Your.. childhood?
The Musician: My childhood, yes. Although, it’s not a childhood in the same sense that you would have a childhood. It’s more like… my early years, per se. I was quite young mentally then, so it is still technically my “childhood.” That’s why I refer to it as such.
Lockwell: So you’ve always had an adult form?
The Musician: Not exactly. I was more of a… late teenager, I believe. Somewhere between a late teenager and a young adult. I’ve just grown into a more adult form over time.
Lockwell: How did you come to be?
The Musician: Unfortunately, I’ve never been able to figure out the answer. The earliest memory I have is waking up alone in what you know as The Atelier.
Lockwell: You mentioned to Michelle Laurens that you’d be "closer to a human than anything else." What exactly did you mean by that?
The Musician: I share many bodily functions and characteristics with a normal male human. I bleed, I cry, I feel emotion, basic things. I do require food and water to live, of course, as well as sleep. I don’t require sustenance nearly as often though, so I can go longer periods of time without indulging in any of them. I also seem to be unable to die from anything that would otherwise be fatal for a human.
Lockwell: Alright then, uh… [A moment of silence passes.] Do you have any relation to Entity 138?
[The Musician sighs.]
The Musician: [With increasing anger] Yes, although sometimes I wish I didn't. The fact that it gets such… sick pleasure in the suffering of others makes no sense to me whatsoever. How could someone's death be enjoyable? It's fucking disgusting.
[The Musician takes a deep breath.]
The Musician: I apologize for the crude language. I lost my composure for a moment.
[The Musician takes their hat off and runs their free hand through their hair before putting the hat back on.]
The Musician: Although I may not approve of its ways, I must admit it is still very much so dear to me. That is all I will say on the matter.
Lockwell: Thank you for the insight.
The Musician: Do you have any other questions?
Lockwell: Only a couple more. Is that alright?
The Musician: Of course. I can be here as long as you need me to be.
Lockwell: Good to know. Why do you call yourself “The Musician?”
The Musician: A group of wanderers many years ago gave me the nickname, both for the music that constantly plays from me and for my musical knowledge.
Lockwell: Interesting… Alright, one last question for you, Mr. Enright.
The Musician: Go ahead.
Lockwell: Why do you help wanderers?
[A moment of silence.]
The Musician: The Backrooms is a… complicated and dangerous place. It’s almost impossible to make it through alone, especially if you don’t have any prior knowledge of its existence to begin with. I’ve taken it upon myself to help wanderers to ensure they can survive in this hellish wonderland so they don’t have to endure the pain and suffering that would befall them otherwise.
Lockwell: I see… Thank you for your time, Mr. Enright. You are free to go whenever.
The Musician: Thank you for having me. It’s been quite an honor. [He bows.]

<END LOG>

Outfit Log 137-A

+ show block
– hide block

The purpose of this log is to make it easier for wanderers to identify him no matter what he wears. As new outfits are reported, this log will be updated in a timely manner.

Outfit: Black pinstripe suit with a top hat.
Notes: This is the outfit he wears the most often.

Outfit: Pastel blue pinstripe suit with a white undershirt and top hat.
Notes: N/A

Outfit: Red and black outfit with gold accents
Notes: He claimed to be going to a special event, but did not elaborate further.

Outfit: A very pale blue pinstripe suit with pastel flowers embroidered on it and decorated with rhinestones. Its top hat also had faux flowers along its silk band.
Notes: When questioned, he said he “wanted to wear something more colorful, is all.”

Outfit: Black silk pajamas with gold thread details.
Notes: This is presumed to be his “sleep wear,” as he does occasionally sleep.

Outfit: Dark red suit with white pinstripes paired with green pants. Also paired with a red top-hat with jingle bells connected to a white silk band.
Notes: First seen around Christmas. When questioned, he said he was in a holiday mood and wanted to dress up for Christmas time. It's also important to note that the music he played was themed after popular Christmas songs from the Front rooms. Research going into how much he knows about the Frontrooms is pending.

Outfit: Light pink suit with red heart accents and embroidery. The matching top-hat also had a rose pinned to it.
Notes: First seen around Valentine's Day. When questioned, he had similar reasoning to the Christmas suit.

Do’s and Don’ts

Do:

  • Listen carefully to the music before approaching
  • Be polite
  • Trust him3

Don’t:

  • Attack him
  • Be disrespectful
  • Attempt to harm him in any way
  • Go towards the music without listening first
