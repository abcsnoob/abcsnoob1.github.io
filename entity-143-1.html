Entity 143 - "Snatcher Weeds"

[Hide disclaimer]
rating: +16+–x

Entity Number: 143

Habitat - Majority, The Cultivator's Courthouse

Description:

Snatcher Weeds are crimson plants that can be found on the grounds of a large number of Levels within the Backrooms. Instances of Entity 143 have been shown to be hostile to most wanderers and entities.

Biology:

Snatcher Weeds, while on the ground, are crimson in color - only turning black when pulled out. They come together in tight clumps, stretching out only when in presence of prey. Most Snatcher Weeds are capable of extending up to 5-7 feet in length, making it difficult to dodge their attacks. Such instances of Snatcher Weeds have been described as "strangely sticky" by those unlucky enough to encounter them. Wanderers have also reported an odd scent, akin to burning. These Entities are also known to be able to release toxins. While their exact chemical composition is unknown, these toxins have shown effects similar to the consumption of Liquid Pain.

Unlike normal weeds, Entity 143 leaves are incredibly sharp, capable of causing lacerations and, in rare cases, dismemberment. The stems are covered in small, sharp thorns as well. Although difficult due to their variation in length and thickness, it is possible to cut Snatcher Weeds off. Doing so will cause them to harden, which makes Snatcher Weeds very useful weapons for those lucky enough to cut a satisfying amount of their stem.

All efforts to cultivate Snatcher Weeds have yielded no results, with one notable exception: the Cultivator has been observed to be able to plant instances of Entity 143 using a vial, rumored to contain his own tears. However, it is possible to hasten its growth with sprits of Liquid Pain for reasons yet to be determined. That being said, they are capable of growing in a majority of Levels, excluding groundless Levels and Levels with temperatures below 25º F (-3.8º C).

Behaviors:

Snatcher Weeds act like normal weeds when not within a five-foot radius from wanderers or entities. However, when the targets are within range, the behavior of Entity 143 instances becomes erratic; often, Snatcher Weeds will make any attempt to harm the being before them, be it by cutting, strangling, or releasing a deadly toxin. Some, however, do not get harmed by Snatcher Weeds at all, although this is rare1. Conversely, when in the presence of The Cultivator, they seem to be under his complete control, contorting and moving to his will.

Discovery:

Snatcher Weeds were first discovered by M.E.G. Team Sacrosanct Searchers, led by Wyatt Evergreen, whilst exploring Level 10. It's to be noted that after this encounter, multiple sightings of a massive Clump were reported.

Written below is a transcript documenting the encounter in which the aforementioned team came face-to-face with a cluster of Snatcher Weeds, controlled by The Cultivator himself.

Close Transcript

Begin Log.


Vanessa Byers: "Wyatt, you can't really be serious about looking for the Cultivator's Courthouse, are you…? I mean… we don't even know if it's real-"

Wyatt Evergreen: "Shut it, Nessa. Don't you get it? Aren't you tired of being pushed around by the higher-ups, when we could be the ones doing the pushing? If we find it, we'll be respected!"

Byers: "I get it, it's just-"

Damien Whitworth: "Look, Wy, as much as I'd like to do my fair share of receiving respect, looking around an infinite, bloody wheat field for some secret Level ain't gonna cut it."

Yasmin Fisher: "Look, what have we got to lose? Besides, I'm not stealing those research notes from Tristan for nothing."

Whitworth: "That was some dumbfuckery you did, Fisher. Seriously."

Evergreen: "Do you guys mind shutting up? We're kind of recording here."

Byers: "You couldn't have told us this earlier?-"

[A slicing sound is heard, followed by multiple screams.]

Fisher: Fuck, oh FUCK! My hand! What- what the fuck- what the fuck was that- help me!

The Cultivator: "Came to smell the roses, did you? A shame there aren't any as of now."

[During this time, Vanessa Byer claims, all four members of the team were then restrained by a multitude of Snatcher Weeds at the hands and feet (except for Fisher), making them drop their weapons. Said restraints have led to deep wounds on the wrists and ankles. Byer also recalls seeing the Cultivator pouring a vial of a clear substance onto the ground.]

Wentworth: "Fuck, it's… it's you! Let- let us go!"

The Cultivator: "Ah, you know me. Flattering. I'm not one for flattery, however."

Byers: "Please, please let us go! We haven't done anything!"

The Cultivator: "You really think I'd bother coming after you if you had done nothing?"

Evergreen: "Oh fuck you-"

[A stabbing sound is heard. Byers recounts that a Snatcher Weed had gone straight through his head. More screams occur, along with sobbing from Byers and Fisher.]

The Cultivator: "Pathetic. Didn't even last long enough to hear his sentence. Nonetheless, I'll say the others... Yasmin Fisher. Many, many rumors about you. Quite the itchy hands you've got, eh? Stealing research notes, equipment... wives...."

Fisher: "Please, please, please don't kill me! I'll- I'll do anything, please…"

The Cultivator: "Negotiations? Fine. I won't kill you. But a sentence, still, must be given. Ah. Steal no more."

Fisher: "Yes, yes I won't-"

[Another set of slicing sounds are heard. Fisher's other hand is cut clean off. Screeches occur. At the same time, the sound of the recorder falling on the ground is heard, as well as a backpack.]

The Cultivator: "There we are. As for you two—Mr. Wentworth and Ms. Byers, was it? The only crime you've done is messing around with things you shouldn't. Needn't worry, though, I'll let you free, albeit a little... harmed."

[All four are dropped. The sound of the recorder getting picked up is heard.]

The Cultivator: "So... you wanted a taste of what my home is like, huh? Fine. I'll give it to you. In fact, I'll describe it myself. How's that for a reward, for not getting corrupted by the sentenced, hm?"

[No other sounds but whimpers and sobs are heard.]

The Cultivator: "Good girl. Now, take Ms. Fisher back to your base, won't you?"

[Byers says that at this point, she and Wentworth hastily take Fisher up, who fainted. She and Wentworth then ran off in the other direction.]


End Transcript.

Vanessa Byers and Damien Wentworth made it back safely, alongside Yasmin Fisher, though sustaining multiple injuries and with Fisher in critical condition. As it is difficult to track down The Cultivator’s current location at the moment, we may only await his supposed “reward” of describing the Level he resides in.

Do's and Dont's:

Do:

  • Stay at least five feet away from instances of Entity 143.
  • When encountering Snatcher Weeds, cut as many as possible to kill them.

Don't:

  • Don't get closer than five feet to them.
  • Spray Liquid Pain on the weeds.
  • Let yourself get corrupted.
