Entity 346 - “The Moon Hare”

NOTICE
Little is known about the following entity. Proceed with caution during encounters. Information contained below may be incorrect or outdated. Do not attempt to directly gather intelligence on this entity without approved coordination and management.

Entity Number: 346

Entity Name: Moon Hare

Habitat: UNKNOWN

▼ DESCRIPTION
▲ DESCRIPTION

Entity 346 is a humanoid entity seen within levels with a visible moon. It wears clothing such as lightweight shirts, large shawls, and thick floor-length skirts or tunics which obscure any view of its legs. It wears pieces of broken armor that appear scavenged and poorly repaired, frequently replacing these pieces between sightings presumably due to damage. Many of these pieces of armor have a circle carved or etched into a visible area.

The entity consistently wears a metal helmet with the hinged mouth covering removed, sloping forward at the nose to resemble an animal-like mouth or muzzle. There is a thick slit across the helmet's face plate for the eyes, although only Entity 346's eyes are visible and no other features (such as skin, nose, or eyebrows) can be seen within the shadow of the helmet. The eyes are presumed to be capable of "sliding" across the entity's face, as it has been noted that the eyes have been seen moving horizontally across the eye slit. Two rabbit-like ears protrude from the helmet, although it appears that the "ears" push out from the entity's head through holes in the top of the helmet, rather than being attached to the helmet. The fur color ranges from brown to white. It is unknown what causes these changes. Blue and silver ribbons are tied around the ears at the base along with thin black metal chains draped around, which conceals where the ears are attached. This is likely to hide the entity's inhuman nature. The entity's mouth is visible due to the lack of coverage, and appears human-like.

▼ BEHAVIOR
▲ BEHAVIOR

Entity 346 travels to and from levels which contain a visible moon in the level's sky. It is unknown what connection, if any, exists between the moon and the entity. The presence of the moon does not appear to affect the entity's strength or behavior, but it is unknown if other effects may occur. Entity 346 often sits or stands in order to view the moon, and will not move from that location unless endangered. It will remain under the moon's light, preferring to remain in the open regardless of conditions such as rain or other inconveniences.

This entity is accompanied by humans or other entities at all times, and has never been seen alone. Recorded instances of entities include clothed Facelings, a Partygoer with visibly covered hands, and Light Guides. One instance of Entity 346 pulling a massive potted bush alongside an accompanying human was reported, although it is unknown what the purpose of this was. Entity 346 is known to be paranoid and suspicious of strangers, and will not allow its accompanying humans or entities to separate from it. The humans and entities accompanying the entity will also refuse to separate from it, and those capable of communication will express hesitance to leave Entity 346's presence.

The entity is capable of human speech, although it remains silent much of the time. Communication is possible, although the entity will often ignore attempts in favor of having an accompanying human or entity respond in its stead.

▼ BIOLOGY
▲ BIOLOGY

Currently, no information regarding this entity's biology has been found.
