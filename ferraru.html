Welcome to Ferra's Author page!

Hey there! My name's Ferraru (or, simply, Ferra), and I am an aspiring writer! I love writing pages for the Backrooms Wiki, and I think this is my favorite hobby. I haven't written that much yet, but I have big plans! I suggest you take a look at my works.

Levels

Level 140 - "The Dry Hill" +43

"Level 140 is a relatively dry hill with a ≈20° incline, with a wooden house on the top of it."
My first work! I was not really pleased with it, until I added the discovery log, check it!

Level 170 - "Concrete Boxes" +53

"Level 170 is a dry field with concrete boxes laying on the ground."
Quite generic, quite good. Proud of it!

Level 155 - "The Sparkling Maze" +35

"Level 155 is an expansive abandoned maze of large brick walls and arches."
Just alright!

Level 75 - "Gallium Caves" +69

"Level 75 is a large group of narrow interconnected caves entirely made out of gallium."
Another level of mine! My best work so far, quite worthy for a read!

Level 39 - "Enchanted Forest" +55

"Level 39 is a series of twisting dirt paths with an expansive oak forest covering both sides."
Tried something more generic there, I think it turned out pretty well!

Level 149 - "The Coconut Isles" +72

"Level 149 is an expansive sky filled with floating sand isles that have coconut palms."
My page for the GOI Contest 2021! I do not know how to feel about this.

Level 300 - "The Boardwalks to the Rainbow" +66

"Level 300 is mainly composed of an incalculable number of boardwalks, hovering several feet above a bright forest and winding rivers."
Ah, this one took quite a long time to get crit, and I think it is pretty good!

Entities

Entity 61 - "Coconut Snares" +27

"Coconut Snares are aggressive frugivore rodents that hide in coconut shells, and are the only native entity on Level 149. "
My first entity! Not for GOI Contest, though!

Ported Articles

Level 225 - "Dazzling Purity" +33

"Level 225 is a series of service hallways with an unstable structure, which have destructive effects for any living being inside of them."
Ported! I expanded the idea a bit further too, I think it turned out great. Big thanks to Duceby for writing the original version! Also, thanks to Sariastuff for some additional help!

Huh, still reading? So, more about myself. I live in Kazakhstan, my pronouns are she/her (preferably) or they/them. My favorite games are Stardew Valley, Minecraft, Super Worldbox, Age of History 2, Euro Truck Simulator 2, People Playground, DELTARUNE, GTA series, Mirror's Edge, The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth, Hollow Knight, and Geometry Dash. My current hobbies include drawing fictional and historical maps, philately (collecting stamps), listening to music, playing videogames, aimlessly consuming content, and, well, writing for this wiki! I joined the wiki on April 15th, 2021. I used to be a staff member and an admin here (August 27th, 2021 - Junior Staff, October 25th, 2021 - Moderator, February 7th, 2022 - Admin, February 6th, 2024 - Author at last). I got less active over time due to changing my interests a little, starting university, and a bunch of other minor things, yet I still love this wonderful community <3
