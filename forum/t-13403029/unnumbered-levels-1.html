Moderator Post
MyrandFox 31 Jul 2022 08:54

Hello U392O,

Negative levels are no longer a distinct classification and are considered enigmatic. This was introduced because the levels are not linear, and it made no sense to have a negative category for a non-linear environment.

Those that keep the name are either from before the change was made, not being edited by their respective authors, or are named that way because of other, more justifiable purposes. Still, they differ from the normal levels as much as every other, non-negative enigmatic level does.

Should you have any questions please feel free to ask a staff member.

, Myrand
