Re: Why are perfectly good levels being rewritten?
CutTheBirch 24 Jun 2021 22:47

The reason those levels were replaced is because the original levels were deleted for having a rating of -5. Our policy is that articles that have a rating of -5 or below are deleted and then someone else can write an article in that slot. We do this so that works that are judged by the community to be low-quality can be removed from the wiki.
