Well for one, all you really need to do is come up with a tale, level, entity, etc that is meant to be funny. SPaG rules and whatnot are rather loose, but that doesn't mean that you'll still need a Staff Member's or Greenlighter's approval to get a joke article on-site. This can be done by making your article in a draft page on the Wiki's Sandbox and contacting a Staff Member/Greenlighter via Discord, the Forums, or by dm-ing one personally. More info on this may be found on the Guide Hub. Happy writing!
