Critiquer Profiles: Meet the Critics!
BlueSignet 31 Oct 2023 00:15

If you are interested in being contacted to for critique, post here! If you put your name on this list, please attempt to do a critique every 90 days. If you cannot, remove yourself from this list. In addition, it's recommended that you sign up for wikidot notifications if you haven't already here: http://notifications.wikidot.com/get.

If you have an interesting in critiquing but don't know how, please check our critique guide!

Please follow this format:

Name:
Will Crit:
Won't Crit:
Strengths:
Weaknesses:
Likes:
Dislikes:
How to Contact: (Wikidot PM? Discord?)
Availability:

This thread is just for the critiquer profiles.

DO NOT SOLICIT CRITIQUE IN THIS THREAD

endless_waters 26 Nov 2024 23:31

Name: endless_waters (or just 'endless' is fine!)

Will Crit: Any kind of Level, sub-levels, entities, objects, phenomena. I will also crit art.

Won't Crit/Do: Anything canon-related, tales, GOIs, POIs, themes. Anything longer than ~5000 words. SPaG checks. Translations

Strengths: Concept and worldbuilding, and connecting ideas to existing lore. I'm also good at assessing clinical tone, and the overall structure of a story. I have background in art and graphic design, so I can give pointers on visual page design.

Weaknesses: SPaG. I'm a native English speaker but I have learning disabilities that make checking grammar hard. I will point out major errors if I see them, but I wont proofread the entire peice. Also I don't know enough CSS to give technical help.

Likes: I love the empty, the overwhealming, the melancholy, and the strange. I don't think a level page necessarily needs a plot in order to be interesting- I'm intrigued by environments in and of themselves. I'm equally interested in seeing places that are scary, calm, beautiful, sad, or mind-twisting. And I like details about what it's like to be a human being surviving/coping in those places.
I love things that incoporate existing lore and tie different stories together. Give me a super-detailed scenario informed by existing complexities!
I also love the M.E.G. I'm not adhered to any particular canon, but I love reading about M.E.G working on a daily basis, and the experiences of their operatives.

Dislikes: Objects that are too powerful and make the Backrooms too easy. Ideas involving divinities/gods. Hugely populated places.

How to contact: I'm on Wiki and Discord (more active here on Wiki!). PM me here, or DM me on Discord. (Please don't ping.) My name's the same in both places.

Avilability: I'm EST, and available to crit one piece every Thursday morning from 9am-12pm. Please reach out to me before Thursday. If no one messages me, I'll choose something waiting for crit on Discord or the Forums.
