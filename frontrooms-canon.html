Frontrooms Canon
rating: +29+–x


Info

Written by Praetor3005 (author page)

Freely inspired from french content creator AlterHis Backrooms' video What if the Backrooms Existed?, which is the reason why I am here on this wiki nowadays. Make sure you watch it if you can!

Special thanks to MisterNemu and LiminalDoctor for the reviews, and to Greggita Mahayfaio for the canon-handling advice!

Close

On May 12, 2019, the World Cryptoscience Organization made an official announcement about the discovery of a parallel reality. With professionalism, they presented a world of thousands of pocket dimensions, which has been nicknamed "The Backrooms" by the unfortunate people who entered it accidentally by traversing reality itself. While access points were at first only discovered by nations in the enigmatic region of the Bermuda triangle, soon enough, corporations and countries alike managed to locate, stabilize, and use many gates to this realm, known as noclipping points. Reactions from citizens all across the world varied. For some, it was an escape from their harsh conditions. To others, it was a terrifying new reality that overlapped with theirs. Most of them, however, saw the potential it represented.

As time passed and after the initial reaction of the public, this new continent became a high point of interest for many countries, corporations, and even individuals. As it occured, the native beings of the Backrooms also turned their attention to their original world which started to colonize their new home.

While this new era was uncertain, people prepared themselves for a new age more uncertain than ever, as the civilizations of the Backrooms saw their world change before their eyes, for better and worse.

The Frontrooms Canon

  • Overview
  • Lore
  • Misc

0verview

The Frontrooms canon is a collaborative canon project aiming to explore an alternative history of the Backrooms, where Earth discovered its existence, as well as enter and leave it safely.

The canon is focalized on two major themes.

First, the influence of the Backrooms on the Frontrooms, anomalous or not. This can take the form of Backrooms physical or mystical elements crossing the literal border between the two worlds, having small or big consequences for Earth's societies, being contained to a simple town, a whole country, or even the entire world. Objects being commercialized, entities invading the real world, or even phenomena occurring far beyond where they should remain are all examples of what kind of influence the Backrooms can directly have on it.

Furthermore, this influence can be more society-focused. Discovering overnight a brand new world made of thousands of singular dimensions where people have been disappearing since the dawn of mankind would shake quite a lot of the foundations of our modern society. From legal struggles between corporations to modified cultures coming back to what used to be their home, the stakes are high as Earth tries to add this new breakthrough to their established societies. But can it truly be achieved?

Secondly, the canon wishes to dive deep into the influence of the Frontrooms on the world of the Backrooms. Since the start of mankind, only a limited number of people and resources from the Frontrooms managed to accidentally pave their way to the Backrooms. Now, this realm sees the consequences of the unlimited access of Earth to its anomalous content. People founding new countries to the disappointment of the existing inhabitants, corporations privatizing levels and terraforming them for profit, or even delocalized wars are all possibilities in the legal grey area that now defines the Backrooms.

Factions of the Backrooms have been used for a rather stable status quo, between forces they knew very well. Now, their entire world is shaken to its core. Their power and reasons to exist are questioned, as they have to adapt to this new reality they can't ignore. Whether it will be friendly or violent, those interactions will make or break the many groups and alliances of the Backrooms, from humans and entities alike. Backrooms Robotics losing their monopoly, Blanche being overwhelmed by the malicious intent of some Frontrooms forces or the M.E.G. debating their new purpose can all be explored in the canon.

The lore of the canon

While (almost) everything of the core canon from the wiki and the real world can be part of the canon, some specific groups, characters, or events will be created as the canon develops itself. The worldbuilding as of now is quite loose, with even the premises of the discovery of the Backrooms remaining undeveloped. The overall timeline will forge itself with the different articles, as many questions and what-ifs will be answered.

Right now, two key elements exist in the canon:

  • The existence of the World Cryptoscience Organization1: This public organization, belonging to the United Nations, was created in 2010 by an effort of many countries to avoid seeing their population fall to obscurantism and conspiracies. This organization leads all research linked to the various cryptosciences for all involved countries, as well as maintaining easy access to documents for the public to read about. Things like the rediscovery of the coelacanth species or the study of the Loch Ness monster mythos would, in this universe, be led by researchers and public country organizations tied to the W.C.O.. It is this group that managed to find the existence of the Backrooms, through the case study of a country-specific research organization. It is also the W.C.O. who will supervise all research linked to this otherworldly realm, sometimes collaborating with Frontrooms corporations or even Backrooms factions directly.
  • The discovery of the Backrooms: While the official announcement to the public was made on May 12, 2019 (yes it's the date of the 4chan post and I have no originality so what?), research has been occurring long before this, as the discovery was made while trying to solve the many disappearances that occurred in the Bermuda Triangle. In this specific region lies a huge, stable noclipping point to the Backrooms, which was made far easier to accidentally get to because of the weather conditions. After much struggle, researchers were able to not only make an easy-to-use entrance to Level 0, but also allow for backtracking thanks to their study of the region as well as the phenomenon. This was the turning point for the Backrooms research on Earth, as this case paved the way to the discovery of new noclipping points, as well as their use.

Misc

  • Expanding and referencing pages or the core canon is heavily encouraged here, as the canon allows us to see the impact of those Backrooms elements on a new alternate situation, as well as showcasing the impact of the Frontrooms on them. Things like domesticating Blub Cats or exploiting the fields of The Bumper Crops can allow for a great development of those pages, leading to very interesting scenarios. Creating new levels, entities, objects… Can obviously work here still, although they would still dive into the canon's topics.
  • Active collaborations between writers are equally as encouraged. Having multiple writers on a standalone page or a series is a great way to brainstorm and develop the universe and is a great experience overall.
  • The themes of the canon are vast, so don't be afraid of exploring different themes than the ones already used! Many themes can be explored, and many articles can be used as a base, both in terms of "what-ifs" and original concepts.
  • Some key points, like the timeline or group interpretations, will be set up by writers as the lore progresses. They will be presented here to allow for an easier collection of information for readers and writers alike.
  • Cross-canon is still possible, as it can allow for exciting the development of the alternative stories already on the site.
