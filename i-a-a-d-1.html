A Not Great But Hopeful Author
rating: +17+–x

Self-introduction


I am I_am_a_dragon (AKA Dragon Pro), you can call me IAAD, Dragon or DP. I am an 18-year-old German, but I live in Japan now, so my time zone is UTC+9.

I mainly write positive articles, but occasionally I write humorous ones as well. I hardly write horror articles because I believe I lack the ability to create a terrifying atmosphere. Because I don't know anything about the canons on this site, I also don't write articles about them.

In addition, I am still learning English as ESL student. This occasionally causes me to use the same word over and over again since I don't comprehend synonyms well, and it also makes my writing seem a bit boring (especially when using clinical tone). I'm doing everything I can to find a solution.

Finally, I should have mentioned that she is my pronoun.
