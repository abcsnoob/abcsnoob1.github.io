Wiki Backrooms Indonesia

Our Link: http://id-backrooms-wiki.wikidot.com/

Eo! Welcome to our hub!

We are the community of Indonesian speaker Backrooms fans who formed a collaborative project to work together in creating our own version of The Backrooms worldbuilding. Like other branch hubs, what we have here is the work of ID authors translated into English to bring our works to the global audience. We focus on independent worldbuilding where we emphasize the traditional aspects of The Backrooms, so you'll find that our works are quite different or strange from those of other branches. We hope that you enjoy the works made by this branch and appreciate the authors so that they'll be happy to keep writing, if you find that our works are not into your taste then our branch is not for you. Happy reading!

This page is a collection of English translations of Indonesian originals which means: we write some original articles on Indonesian site, translate them, and then post them here. If you are an Indonesian, we want you to visit the ID site just to read or even joining us! Note that our site is moderated independently and have different rules and regulations than this site, so if you join us then you're under our care :)

Read the language FAQ on the Translation Hub if you have any questions about this page.

Contact the "Contactable Staff" (below) if you have any "problem" with our content (like translation quality, the concept, and so on)

Established: 13/04/2022
Originals / Translations: This site hosts originals only.

Levels

A Level means a "world" that become part of The Backrooms, there are many different types of Levels as a result of the creativity of authors. However, not every world concepts can be considered a Backrooms Level as some concepts are more akin to a clone of Earth. The branch emphasizes isolation and traditional liminal style, so it's no wonder most of the levels are empty and boring. Well, this is exactly what Backrooms should be: a collection of empty worlds with nothing inside them but you and a world that strangely gives you a sense of familiarity and slowly drives you insane.
