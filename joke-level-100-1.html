Joke Level 100 - "Washing Machine"

{$title}Languages:
















rating: +59+–x

Level 100 is the 101st Level of the Backrooms.

Description

weeeeee

Level 100 resembles and works like the inside of a regular washing machine. When inside of the level, you will be shrunk down in order to fit in the washing machine. After a certain duration, the washing machine will activate and begin spinning rapidly, potentially leading to injuries. It is recommended to leave the level before this happens.

Bases, Outposts and Communities

Due to the natures of this level, it is impossible to set up bases and outposts.

Entrances And Exits

Entrances

You can enter this level by spinning.

Exits

No-clipping through the washing machine will bring you to a random level.
