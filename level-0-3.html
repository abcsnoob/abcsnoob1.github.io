Level 0.3 - "The Icy Rooms"

Level 0.3 is a sub-section of Level 0.

Description

The description of this sub-section is nearly identical to Level 0. It's just that… there's ice. EVERYWHERE.

And it's so damn cold.

Target: Level 0.3

Sector: A891-01

Objective: Defrosting and Class-B Remodeling

Task Force: 104-CX

Task Force Members: Planes, Chris, Havec, Evie

Start Date: 09-02-2019

Due Date: 12-31-2019

Additional Equipment(s): Blowtorch x2









Additional: After that catastrophe in remodeling Level 0, we believe in you all that you shall bring a good set of clean, pristine, and comfortably warm rooms! So, you all better actually work with what we have provided you! Understood?

Sincerely,
Major Took

We are working hard to remodel Level 0.3!

Summary

We must say, these icy rooms are a mess, but rest assured, progress on fixing that is going smoothly! See those disgusting carpets laced with frost, coarse as gravel? We replaced them in the blink of an eye. See those bare walls sprinkled with scraps of palely frozen wallpapers, that can detach at a moment's notice? We covered them with our improved design. See those gigantic cubes of ice scattered throughout, within rooms so small in comparison that only barely contains them within their thin margins? These blowtorches excellently took care of them. Havec is doing some great work to pump out those gas for it!

What a mess… Who knew that walls can have frostbites too?

The remodeling mission is going smoothly! We've completed all of the walls, wallpapers and carpets, I think

Summary

We have replaced the old carpets with this softer and comfier wool carpet, polished the walls, got rid of these yucky wallpapers and layered an objectively more aesthetically pleasing wallpaper on them, and we are just working on the minor stuff! Unfortunately, we can't use our blowtorch anymore, but regardless, we should be able to finish this very soon, with the four six of us.

If there is one issue, it would be the air vents… this stuff keeps pumping out frosty mist that tends to ruin everything some things! We tried to block it off, but it didn't work too well. Ah well, I'm sure that'll be fixed soon enough!

We'll also be revamping those outdated and stubborn things hopefully
