This is a tale about a man from the frontrooms. His name was Mattheo, he was 27 years and had an lovely life. A wife, one daughter, and two sons. It was a pleasant autumn afternoon, after the family decided to make a vacation. They had a favorite place, a secluded place where they could escape the everyday stress and the noisy city life.

The family, while going to the vacation with car, sang happy musics, while they going with 87 mph on the highway, completely unaware of the imminent catastrophe.

It only take 1 second, in which the situation escalated to the worst possible scenario. A crash, a blinding flash, screeching metals, A chain reaction, leading to mass collision. Cars colliding, like the domino effect, explosions echoing through the asphalt, lives ending in crying arises between steel and fire. Tears mingled in pools of blood and oil.

Mattheo, being unconscious was rushed to the nearby hospital. After his awake he asked what was going on.

Dr. Banez: “You were rescued unconscious from a terrible accident. We managed to stabilize you, but had to perform two operations for internal bleeding and multiple fractures.”

Mattheo: “Where is my family?”

A silence, which say more than thousands of words, broke in. The doctor hesitated and revealed the reality, which hit Mattheo a million times harder then the crash.

Dr. Banez: “I’m sorry! Mattheo, your family didn’t survive the accident!”

Mattheo had the shock in its life. The roughness of this words, hit him harder as on the accident was. He cried a lot in the next days, just to realize, he murdered his family and must life a live alone in the thick darkness around him.

He regret every single day and every single breath, he could live. Life was literally the hell on earth for him. He was emotionally flattened, the crash scene tortured him every night, he sees the faces every time he tries to sleep. He was tired, but he couldn't recover anymore.

But he got a life goal. To prevent others, making the same mistake, he had once did, to prevent others, to live that one second, which decided the life and the death. He became the best police officer the city has ever seen, hard outside and wounded inside, a never ending bleeding from a never closing scar of life.

One day he see a speeder. A woman, in panic, as it would seem that she flee from something. But what Mattheo saw there, was not a woman which had panic, he saw his formerly self. The woman, just remembered him to someone. It was… his wife. Aesthetically and physically, she was strikingly similar to his wife. “Marie, is that you!?” said Mattheo, “No…! That would be too good to be true!” while his eyes glazed, as he remembered the situation.

He turned on his blue lights and siren and pursued the car in which the woman was driving. Over the loudspeaker, he ordered the woman to slow down. But the woman took the exit directly onto the highway, where Mattheo once lost his family.

The chase ended with almost the same outcome, but the woman just barely managed to avoid the disaster and crashed into the pillar, causing the car to catch fire due to sparks from friction.

The woman told Mattheo, why he was running away. His psychopathic boyfriend would kill him and his two daughters, which shortly after the mother exited the car, they exited the demolished car either. His boyfriend “Winston R.” was a famous serial killer, which was newly captured by other colleagues.

Mattheo warned the woman, unnoticingly to reveal his bleeding wound: “Don’t put your life in danger like that! You and your children are important! Live! Cling yourself to life! Rules and we police officers are there to prevent disasters and to constantly remind people how important rules are. Don’t let this fucking one second by not paying attention, you could have been your downfall, which can decide between life and death!” but he saw not the woman, he had rescued at the gnaws from death, he saw his own wife and the last disscusion they’ve made. He collapsed.

He knelt on the shattered asphalt, the smell of burnt rubber and gasoline stinging his nostrils. He looked into the eyes of the woman and her children, who were staring at him. “It was my fault!” he whispered, and then tears came up and released like a waterfall and cried in silence.

“I made the mistake and wasted that one second, in complete absence of myself!” The sirens wailed, but they sounded distant, unreal. Mattheo felt the highway's cold seep through his uniform, but it was a different kind of cold, one that additionally paralyzed him from within.

He pressed his hands against his aching chest, where the scar of his failure laying, which would never will be healing, nor be win the love again. In that moment of complete psychological surrender, in that deepest desire to undo that one second, he heard something. An echo of silence and shattering reality.

The ground beneath his knees didn’t crumble, but blurred. The lights of the emergency vehicles dissolved into a misty, warm glow.

He closed his eyes, wishing only for one more moment of peace. One second in which the trauma didn’t exist. The highway lights melted into a warm glow. The sirens faded into a hum. When he opened his eyes again, the highway was gone. The uniform was gone. He was in a warm, empty room that smelled of coffee and safety…

And a voice whispered:

“Here it is. Your reclaimed second. Use it until it expires again.”

And at that very moment, his comfort and happiness began.

Open your second?
Be quick! Time is ticking ...

Level -1: The regained second

SURVIVAL DIFFICULTY:

Class “Oasis”

  • Nostalgic Memories
  • Healing Properties
  • Temporary Level Existence

Description

Level -1 is a mysterious, unnumbered level, which first discovered by Mattheo.

According to Mattheo’s report, this level has no fixed structure or characteristic, it simply manifests itself and then disappears immediately.

It was comforting. Mattheo saw his family, his happiest moments with him, the bad times as well as the good times they had gone through.

It felt strange, almost like an alternate reality. A reality that felt like it existed between the frontrooms and the backrooms. As if it were a stopover.

It was a manifestation of perfection… or imperfection? A life without any dangers, it was the only place, where he smiled again.

He saw, how his family get to the vacation destination and all that happy time, that remained cloaked from him.

It had been a memento of his life, a reminder not to give up the fight, no matter what might happen. It was worth to fight for surviving.

It was worth to see it, all the laying beauty,
It was worth to hear it, all the nice sounds and memories,
It was worth to feel the air, under your palm,
It was worth to love the life, which was given to you.

The time here in this level, flows unnaturally slow. It was thousand times slower than the usual timeflow. Mattheo had 16 minutes and 40 seconds time, to compensate his 1 second, which he wished for to correct it.

Within the 16 minutes and 40 seconds -which are derived from the real one second-, the wanderer not only relives the memories, but is completely immersed in them. The scent of Grandma’s apple pie becomes measurably more intense, a child’s giggles sound crystal clear, and the warmth of the sunlight feels like a physical embrace. Time doesn’t just expand, it allows the wanderer to consciously and fully process each emotional anchor point before the liberating moment of powerlessness arrives. It’s time to forgive yourself.

Forget about the past, create your future,
Life is the greatest gift, that was given to you!
Survive!

The Legacy of Mattheo

Although no visible entities are present, some wanderers report hearing a faint, static radio noise at the edge of their consciousness. Sometimes this noise is punctuated by a deep, mournful voice that invariably whispers the phrase, ”Pay attention to your second”. This is the only physical trace left by the guardian figure Mattheo, confirming the level’s existence as his legacy.

Entrance and Exits

  • The entrance is not well known. It was only rumored, that the wanderer must be experienced a trauma directly related to 1 second of escalation.
  • After the 1 second passes in this level, the wanderer automaticly pass out and wakes again, with no more memories to the past, at Level 0.
