Level 11.1 - "Private Enterprise"
Asset 11.1 - "Private Enterprise"
rating: +37+–x
  • Revision One
  • Asset 11.1 Memo
  • Revision Two

Do not share or reproduce this document without the express permission of the Backrooms Nonaligned Trade Group

Asset 11.1.

Value: High

Priority: High

Description:

Asset 11.1 is a large level controlled by the B.N.T.G. The level is a medium-sized city composed primarily of brick-built mills and cathedrals. It is located within Level 11, making it finite in size, although exact measurements have yet to be taken. Certain variations make it distinct from Level 11, such as its general layout and resources.

Communities:

Other groups universally recognize 11.1 as being apart of the B.N.T.G.'s territory, and as such, none can be found operating within the city. Exploration-based organizations such as the M.E.G. and U.E.C. are allowed to enter the area under B.N.T.G. supervision, however this requires the payment of several fees depending on the length of their stay.

Settlements and outposts in Asset 11.1 are considered B.N.T.G. property, and are taxed accordingly at the end of each fiscal year. Prominent settlements have been listed below:

The Remnant

Population: ~250

History: After the widespread collapse of The Followers of Jerry, many refugees fled Level 11 and entered Asset 11.1 in hopes of escaping prosecution from groups like The Visionaries of Berry. They eventually founded The Remnant, a small shanty-town near the edge of 11.1.

Though they were independent for quite some time, the B.N.T.G. eventually annexed the region and encouraged The Remnant populace to submit to their law. Since then, the community has flourished under the watchful gaze of the Nonaligned Trade Group. Continued religious worship of Jerry has been outlawed.

Endburg

Population: ~600

History: The oldest known settlement within Asset 11.1, Endburg avoided the B.N.T.G.'s control for several years until a lucrative deal was struck with the towns council. Afterward, the region became apart of the overall B.N.T.G. territory, and their currency was gradually switched over to traditional B.N.T.G. credits. Prominent town leaders who opposed this transition were relocated to Level 11 to avoid further controversy.

Calculated Value:

The potential for Asset 11.1 as a future "super-settlement" of trade and commerce is extremely high. A majority of the B.N.T.G.'s yearly budget is devoted to the growth and success of 11.1, with a large return to be expected within the next few decades. According to a census done last year, a staggering amount of Level 11 denizens are considering moving to 11.1 in the near future— a total of 0.53% of the entire Level 11 populace.

From the office of the B.N.T.G. Head Council…

Greetings fellow employees, valued customers, and frequent clients.

Effective immediately, Asset 11.1 is no longer considered to be apart of the B.N.T.G.'s Level Unification Program. In layman's terms, this means that the level will not be recognized as B.N.T.G. territory, and that any of our settlements and operations within it will be withdrawn immediately. The numerous settlers already housed in Asset 11.1 will be given a choice to either re-locate to another B.N.T.G. Level, or to risk unsupported immigration into Level 11.

Likewise, B.N.T.G. patrols and trading caravans are under strict orders to avoid Asset 11.1. Any B.N.T.G. representatives found within the area post-exodus will be fired quickly. It must be stressed that any and all future settlements in Asset 11.1 are NOT apart of the B.N.T.G., and instead represent other miscellaneous Backrooms organizations.

These orders have been sent in light of requests from a brand-new investor of the B.N.T.G.— a group who have wished to remain anonymous to the public as of right now. Know that their interests and goals support both what is best for the B.N.T.G., and for the wider Backrooms populace!

Do not share or reproduce this document without the express permission of the Backrooms Nonaligned Trade Group

Asset 11.1.

Value: Low

Priority: Low

Description:

Asset 11.1 is a private level owned by B.N.T.G. Investors. Entrance and exit into Asset 11.1 is strictly monitored per the investor's request, and B.N.T.G. activity is kept to an absolute minimum.

Communities:

The B.N.T.G. has no remaining communities, outposts, or settlements within Asset 11.1. All construction projects in the city have been cancelled.

Since the withdrawal, other group activity in the area has increased significantly. Most notably, The Eyes of Argos have begun suspicious patrols throughout Asset 11.1— B.N.T.G. Investors have requested that these patrols be observed and reported on heavily.

Calculated Value:

The Investors generous donations have superseded the projected income that Asset 11.1 was expected to net, rendering the need for such territory unnecessary. As such, the area has no intrinsic value to the B.N.T.G.

The Investor's Message

Do not share or reproduce this document without the express permission of the Backrooms Nonaligned Trade Group


With our recent departure from Asset 11.1, there have been many concerns amongst B.N.T.G. supporters in regards towards our new Investors. In response, the Head Council has reached out to The Investors and asked for them to address these questions.

Their response has remained unedited:

Greetings Nonaligned Trade Group representatives and citizens.

We would like to begin by confirming that our investment and subsequent purchase of "Asset 11.1" was a much warranted and thoroughly-planned marketing venture. Our corporate needs far outweigh the Nonaligned Trade Group's plans for such a district, and we believe that you will find this paradigm shift to be for the greater good. Rest assured that this new development has truly moved the needle, and that our collaborative synergy will be all the better for it.

We also wish to take discussion surrounding "Asset 11.1" offline. This area is now a private enterprise, and the golden handshake we offered your group should be more than enough to pivot future investigations away from the area, be they internal or external. We are excited to see your action plan regarding this concern of ours.

Lastly, we hope that this partnership can continue for the foreseeable future. Our innovative business strategies coupled with the ingenuity of your group should result in some real, tangible breakthroughs.

Give us 110%.

—Your loyal Investors.
