Level 188 - "Causing's Button"
Folklorecon 2025 » Level 188 - "Causing's Button"
rating: -16+–x


Info

The Author is xuziqi.

Special thanks to calf-0 for correcting some inappropriate sentences!

Close

You'll never understand what I felt when the big red button truly appeared in front of my eyes.

The Origin

It was Aug 12 2025, I was just jogging along an average streets within The Endless City. I kept thinking about what one of my closest friends told me the day before. He said that there was a Discord user named "Causing" no-clipping into a weird space by accident 3 years ago.

Is it just a fabricated Urban Legend? Maybe no one knows. After getting home, I started searching online.

To my disappointment, it was almost impossible to view past messages on Discord, especially in an unfamiliar server. I therefore resolved to find a screenshot of the original post. It was painstaking to find the source in the tremendous amount of fragmented information, but after hours of effort, I succeeded. The screenshot is shown below:

I'm afraid perhaps the screenshot isn't properly cc-licensed and I can't display it. But I can tell you the general content of it below:

There was a youth who lived in the original Level 131. One day he left his room for work as usual. But when he opened his door, something outside attracted him. It was a big red button, right on the floor next to the door. He pushed it with great curiosity, then passed out and woke up in a new and strange place.

He was extremely shocked by the buttons which dotted all the surfaces within the new space. Beside each button was a piece of note paper which seemed to describe its function(s). He pressed a few at random for seconds, then no-clipped into The Skating Rink. This was all he said.

That was the first time I learned of the legend. At the time, no one, myself included, could have predicted that I would eventually explore this rumor and make it a part of my life.

The Exploration

The Legend attracted me a lot. I thought if I can no-clip into the same level, I'll take advantage of the buttons to make everything better for me. But the discoverer didn't tell anything about the entrance. That's why I kept exploring the rumor.

I focused my attention on the place he had no-clipped from. It was the original version of Level 13. I thought the entrance must be closely linked with this old level. However, I couldn't enter it. All of its entrances stopped working several years ago.

I resolved to go to the former entrance and try my fortune. At the entrance of a closed-down apartment building, I found a notice. On the notice, it explained that the building was closed due to the sudden appearance of many big red buttons on various floors. These were exactly the entrances I needed, weren't they?

Finding a way into the building, I thought, might lead me to them. But where was the way? Every possible entrance seemed completely blocked. I stood pondering for a long time. After that, I thought I should possibly go and look for the way in instead of just thinking.

Then I walked around the building. I found there was a little hole in the ground. I made it bigger and then went in. In the hole was a long hallway crowded with piles of soil and excretions. The way was wet, dark, narrow and stinky. I held my breath as tightly as I could and went as fast as possible. And when I got to its end, I saw……

A button. A big red button.

The Misadventure Within the Level

I pressed the button without any hesitation. Just as what happened to the original discoverer, I passed out, fell into a void and deep nick between the two levels, then woke up in that strange space. This brought on an overwhelming sense of discomfort and dizziness. I wearily lay down as soon as my mind got clear.

After a few minutes, I stood up and carefully looked around the surrounding. Everything is new and clean. Although I'd heard of it before, I felt so amazed with the environment of the level. The walls were smooth and the buttons were almost everywhere except for my underfoot.

I gathered my thoughts, and then made what would turn out to be the worst decision: I pushed a button at random. Unfortunately, it was a button which had a function of making the user bounce, just like a ball in a pinball game.

Then I started comically bouncing up and down in the level. While doing this, I hit many buttons by accident and that caused something serious. For example, I sent hundreds of messages "lol" in a minute in a Discord server. This caused me to be severely punished. But the most terrible thing was yet to come: my right thumb was suddenly and cleanly cut off. How scary that was!

After bouncing for minutes, I finally no-clipped out of that nightmare—seemingly through one of the buttons themselves—and into a peaceful place. As a loud "Fuck" echoed through the street, I went straight home and began to write a report of the level.

After around 3 hours' work, I finished the report and submitted it to the M.E.G. Database Section. They acted so fast that they made sure and documented the level only for three days. The link of the document is put below.
