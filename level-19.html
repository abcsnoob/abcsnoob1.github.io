First contact with Pending Approval 19.

Pending Approval 19 probably just gonna be designated as Level 19 if Im honest. is the X Level of The Backrooms. It takes the appearance of some dank old attic, feels oddly familiar like every other place in here.

Description:

a dirty old attic, floors are creaky and the whole place is gonna trigger my damn asthma. Level PA19 is a lot of endless cramped basement. Cobwebs are common and as are boxes. The boxes contain little to nothing need to revise this to fit with the usual tone, god clinical is a drag.

PA19 lacks in any source of natural light, with the entirety of the Level only being illuminated by an extremely faint glow that comes from no known origin. and some of the floorboards emit this lovely orange glow from whatevers under them, its so warm, reminds me of my Grandma's home.

Theres also some furniture, ranging from tables, ottomans, bookcases, and sofas. All of them have a feel of a care home, and are incredibly dusty Im going to stab whoever invented clinical tone.

Bases, Communities And Outposts

There are no established Bases, Communities, or Outposts on this level. and I honestly doubt there ever will be, the dust-lined walls alone will kill anyone with lung problems. Although the warm glow beneath the floorboards is nice, maybe a place could be set up around it. I'm sure we could survive around it.

Additional Content

It should be noted that Level PA19 can mess with your head, creating voices and inflicting nausea. yeah I know Im missing the important lingo, but if you care so much about this sounding right why not edit this yourself.

Writing this a few hours after, just got the entrances and exits to do now. But the hallucinations are getting stronger. Im seeing all sorts of colors, my mouth is filling with bile, and I swear Ive blacked out who knows how many times in the past hour alone. But I think looking and thinking about the orange glow beneath the floorboards helps a ton. Its helping me, at least.

Entrances And Exits:

Entrances

Well I got here via Level 1, just a couple meters away from the main M.E.G. base, I forget what stupid name it's being given. I was sent here by one of the new 'teams' being made. I just crawled through the hole in the wall that we've seen for weeks. Getting in was fine getting out, though. That's going to be an issue.

Exits

There are currently no catalogued exits. the warm glow will be your exit.

Level 19 upon its initial discovery.

Level 19 is the 20th Level of The Backrooms and was discovered on December 1st, 2014. With the Level itself bearing the form of a dank, dilapidated and cluttered attic.

Description:

Level 19 is a complex series of randomly shaped attic spaces, packed with a heavy clutter of boxes, vintage furniture, and other miscellaneous items. While the exteriors of these items are frequently found damp, dust-covered, and dirty, the interiors of these items will, more often than not, be in near pristine condition.2

The boxes within Level 19 often contain vital foods and a wide amount of cataloged, anomalous objects. Upon an item being removed from a container, there is a possibility it will begin to decay at a rapid rate3. This decay effect affects more than just inanimate objects, however. A select amount of individuals may feel effects nausea, paranoia, general dizziness, and may be subject to periods of blacking out or hallucinations. It is currently unknown if this extends to physical decay.

Within some of the floorboards of Level 19, a warm, fire-like glow may emanate from underneath, shining up through the flooring. This glow has an odd, alluring effect that can attract wanderers by instilling a feeling of comfort and tranquillity. Oftentimes pulling direct memories from the individual and altering them to be directly connected to this glow.

The Orange Glow is the term coined to describe the faint, warm glow that emanates from under the floorboards of Level 19. While it's currently unknown where the glow originates or how the light can alter memories, it is heavily theorized that either this glow creates the decay effect in Level 19, or that it amplifies it.

While not known to be sentient, this glow has previously infected the mind of others to spread the knowledge of its existence, which has caused unsanctioned edits to be randomly added on both this article and others on the database in the past. For this reason, it is heavily unadvised to enter Level 19.

Bases, Communities And Outposts

There are no known Bases, Communities, or Outposts on this level.

Entrances And Exits:

Entrances

Level 19 can be accessed via climbing into random holes within Level 1. One exists just off the north exit to Base Alpha, though a further 15 have been found by the M.E.G.

Other entrances include Level 18, which is done by turning around and leaving the level upon arrival, and level-183 via old manholes on the traversable paths of its sewers.

Exits

One can exit Level 19 by falling through any of the non-glowing floorboards or crevices, resulting in a variety of outcomes. Such as appearing on Level 38, Level 202, level-432, and Level 654.

Alternatively, occasional, broken or damaged doors can lead to Level 289, Level 5, Level 12, or Level 20.

As a third method of exit, finding either handheld games or small arcade devices within the boxes can teleport one's self to Level 40, or Level 212.

Finally, the presumed perimeters of Level 19 cause intense blurriness and general decay that is usually apparent on Level 19, however experiencing these processes at the boundaries of Level 19 will take you to level-140.
