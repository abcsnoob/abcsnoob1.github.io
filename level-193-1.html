Level 193 is the 194th Level of the Backrooms.

Description

An image of Level 193

The level consists a graveyard stretching across an infinite plane, with an unreachable forest surrounding its perimeter. It is blanketed in a non-obstructing mist. Level 193 is filled with unmarked graves, each 2 meters apart. Occasionally, there are empty graves which appear as though dug open from the inside. Open graves will sometimes have various scattered debris and heavily used items around them. Each undamaged grave contains a corpse, and under no circumstances should one disturb the soil. The level or an entity living within it appears to be sentient, and has the ability to influence the database with false information about itself. However, this only extends to text-based entries. To prevent this, data is written down then transcribed and uploaded as image form. Although this causes formatting and database issues, this is regarded as a positive effect as the entity's file altering is too powerful.

Entities

Under further analysis by M.E.G. operatives, the fog itself appears to be a collection of microorganisms. When inhaled, they travel through the nose and to the amygdala, evoking an extreme response of guilt and sadness. This is theorized to be the reason that only people undergoing loss can enter this level. The fog or, more likely, the entity controlling the fog seems to enjoy torturing people by persuading them that their loved ones are alive. This is used to distract victims and lure them into the graves to slowly kill them. M.E.G. operatives have also seen an unknown type of entity dig itself out of the graves. Physically, these entities resemble humans in advanced stages of decay, and on closer inspection some were even recognized as wanderers who had died in the Backrooms. Upon escaping the graves, however, these entities run toward and eventually enter the forest, despite the level being theoretically infinite. The open graves have also begun exhibiting a psychological effect that compels people to crawl inside. Wanderers who crawl inside are to be considered lost and should not be rescued.

Bases, Outposts and Communities

  • There are no bases or outposts on this level.

Entrances And Exits

Entrances

  • Wanderers who are coping with loss rarely appear in this level after sleeping.
  • A written account of a wanderer who awoke buried within a grave on this level was found next to an open grave. The text claims that the wanderer died in Level 8. The veracity of their revival or supposed death is unconfirmed.

Exits

  • The exact method of exit is unknown, but all who have escaped Level 193 report to have come to terms with their loss and have managed to resist the level's influence more than others.
