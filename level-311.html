Level 311 - "The Yellowrooms"              



SURVIVAL DIFFICULTY:

Class pending

  • Newly Discovered
  • Unsecured
  • Extremely Difficult To Exit


The image produced when attempting to photograph Level 311.


The Yellowrooms are a brand new level of the Backrooms, first recorded on March 31st, 2025. The level is identical in both appearance and properties to Level 0; the three distinguishing properties it has are:
1: Everything in the level is slightly more yellow, but the difference is unnoticeable to the unaided eye.
2: There is an unreasonably large sign saying, in red impact font: " Level 311 ". Why it's there is unknown, but possibly due to the level being self-aware.
3: Every picture taken of the level turns into a near-perfect copy of the original image of Level 0, the difference being that the whole picture is the same amount more yellow as Level 311 actually is.

For the rare few who lack knowledge about Level 0, both levels are a series of likely endless yellow hallways and corridors. While in either level, it is impossible to meet either entities or other humans due to a currently unexplained isolation effect.

A few days before this page was posted, there was a several-day-long argument over whether or not the level exists, due to its properties making it sound unbelievable. I, (likely to become former) General Public Database moderator Koala, real name Zachary Smithson, originally dismissed the level as a hoax. After coming here, I realised I could not have been more wrong. I'm unsure if I will make it out of the level alive. Please, never make the same mistake as I did.

Entrances and exits:

Entrances:

Entry to Level 311 is likely one of the main causes of death in Level 0. About 30% of its walls are just slightly more yellow; it seems that touching any of these walls will bring one here, likely by accident. Fortunately, as the level is so new, it has so far likely had a low fatality rate. I may be the first life it claims.

Exits:

Level 311 can be exited by running into the giant sign at precisely 10mph (16.09344kmph) — there is almost no margin of error, making exiting extremely difficult — which will send you to Level 1, like Level 0 usually would. It is recommended to run with your arms in front of you to reduce the risk of bodily injury.



Of course, G.P.D. site policy dictates that pages with a low rating will be deleted. Those pages are often like this one: short and lacking in uniqueness. But, in this case, an unpredictable number of future lives are in your hands, reader. You wouldn't want them all to go to waste, right?

So, will you ensure the further protection of humanity?
