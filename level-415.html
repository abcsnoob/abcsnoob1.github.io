Level 415 - "Forest Trail"
rating: +30+–x

SURVIVAL DIFFICULTY:

Class 1

  • {$one}
  • {$two}
  • {$three}

Description

Representative photo of Level 415.

Level 415 presents an extensive trail that traverses a dense forest, predominantly surfaced with asphalt and measuring approximately 4 to 9 meters in width. Various exploratory records suggest the infinite or nonlinear nature of this trail within Level 415. Within this level, wanderers are incapable of inflicting long-term damage upon the trail, as it undergoes periodic restoration, promptly repairing any inflicted damages and causing personal belongings placed on the trail to vanish simultaneously. The majority of the trail remains a flat and straight trajectory, although occasional steep segments may arise. Occasional forks can be found along the course; however, most are regarded as the traps set by entities; yet in rare instances, these forks may lead to alternative levels. Many wanderers within this level report a distinct aroma reminiscent of freshly cut grass permeating through the air, although its origin remains elusive.

At Level 415, the normal day-night cycle is nonexistent as perpetual darkness engulfs the surroundings. No celestial bodies grace the sky of this level. A wanderer reports a temperature range of approximately 1 to 15 degrees Celsius, with unvarying weather conditions prevailing without any discernible fluctuations. Occasionally, during their journey, the wanderer unexpectedly encounters an extensive swarm of black insects that have been confirmed to be illusory manifestations believed to be a customary phenomenon in this realm. Remarkably, this level does not have Wi-Fi signal.

As part of Level 415, streetlights and benches can be seen everywhere.

The wanderers exploring Level 415 have observed that benches frequently appear on both sides of the trail, predominantly crafted from wood with a few exceptions made from alternative materials. All benches within this level are situated at an identical horizontal position. As a result, certain benches are suspended in less inclined sections while partially embedded or even completely buried underground in uphill segments. The presence of these benches is limited to the left and right sides of the trail, and they possess immovable and indestructible properties. Illumination within this level solely emanates from streetlights positioned alongside the trail. It is noteworthy to mention that any external light sources such as flashlights, candles, or lighters fail to provide sustained illumination within this level due to compelling evidence suggesting that the streetlights absorb all light except their own radiance. Speculation arises regarding whether these streetlights potentially induce perpetual darkness in Level 415.

The Level 415 area is exclusively illuminated by streetlights, as previously mentioned. However, investigations conducted among wanderers who have ventured into Level 415 reveal diverse perspectives on the color spectrum emitted by these streetlights: they can appear blue, white, yellow, purple, or can even randomly switch between different colors. The researchers assert that there is no discernible pattern in the colors observed by wanderers. Furthermore, when multiple wanderers simultaneously observe the same streetlight at a specific location and time, each individual perceives different colors. Similarly, photographs of these streetlights captured using devices1equipped with photography functions also demonstrate this phenomenon when viewed by different individuals.

The forest area of Level 415 is incredibly vast. However, due to the absence of external light sources, exploring the depths of the forest is currently unfeasible. The forest remains shrouded in darkness, rendering it inaccessible. Although the forest within Level 415 is generally deemed safe, events such as falls and collisions caused by a lack of light sources and the overall lack of data regarding the forest, it is not fully understood. Therefore, for safety reasons, it is strongly advised that wanderers refrain from exploring the forest. The woodland boasts a rich diversity of plant species, including some unidentified varieties. Additionally, scattered throughout the forest are clusters of edible berries. Most trees in this woodland belong to the frontrooms category, with only a few exceptions falling outside any classification belonging to the frontrooms.

A flat trail, picture from Level 415.

Occasionally, wanderers may experience moments of cognitive disorientation during their journey, engaging in repetitive actions2 for several hours. However, upon regaining consciousness, these wanderers exhibit no recollection of the preceding hours and the underlying cause of this phenomenon remains elusive. Nevertheless, wanderers with heightened focus can easily surmount this cognitive predicament.

While forests lining the trails are a common sight, there are infrequent occurrences where lakes of varying sizes manifest on one side of the trail. Passersby often witness peculiar phenomena near these lakes such as benches suspended above the water or anomalous events involving streetlights or trees emerging from the lake. The composition of these bodies of water has been determined to be regular freshwater and after treatment it is deemed suitable for consumption by wanderers.

The soil in Level 415 is highly fertile, facilitating the flourishing growth of a wide variety of flowers, plants, and crops. The mechanism responsible for maintaining perpetual vibrancy among the vegetation in this level remains currently unknown.

A steep trail in Level 415.

The resources on Level 415 are abundant. In addition to the previously mentioned water and berries, it is common to find suitcases positioned next to benches in this area. The number of suitcases gradually decreases as the level is accessed over time. These suitcases typically contain a substantial quantity of almond water and personal belongings, yet no evidence of human presence, such as fingerprints or saliva have been detected. Occasionally, they even include royal rations. For some reason, once all the items inside have been taken by wanderers, the opened suitcases vanish when unobserved.

Within this level, there are a large amount of ways to obtain resources, namely through various non-functional but full vending machines. The following list enumerates the automatics.

Rarity level Name
■Abundance Almond water
■More Compressed biscuit
■More Lucky O’ Milk
■Medium quantity Dairy drinks
■Medium quantity Puffed food
■Less Canned fruit
■Very few Portable instant lobster bisque

Turning Trail at Level 415.

It is worth noting that the number of items in this level is constantly diminishing, which seems to be related to the consumption patterns of the wanderers in this particular level. For example, when Level 415 was initially discovered, there was a scarcity of portable instant lobster bisque due to its high demand among wanderers seeking its nutritional value, ease of storage, and convenience. The perfect portable instant meal.

Wanderers may encounter individuals masquerading as security personnel or janitors who swiftly vanish from sight around corners. Investigations have conclusively determined that these individuals are not wanderers, and all efforts to trace their whereabouts have proven futile.

Starting Point

Starting Point.

Although the basic spatial structure of this level undergoes nonlinear bending and distortion, there are still areas that remain unaffected. Serving as a distinct area within Level 415, the starting point can be regarded as an independent space detached from the main body, deviating from the conventional model of time and space by encompassing the entirety of Level 415. Wanderers have the opportunity to observe various sectors surrounding this starting point, even discerning divergent behaviors among their counterparts. As wanderers approach proximity to the starting point, they enter into their corresponding domains.

The majority of the wanderers who reach Level 415 inevitably find themselves in this location. Our research has revealed that the starting point possesses a compelling effect that deters wanderers from lingering and prompts them to swiftly depart from it. This effect intensifies as the duration of exploration within the starting point increases. Wanderers who venture beyond the confines of the starting point will be transported to a specific area within Level 415, devoid of any recollection regarding their departure route from the starting point. Additionally, due to various factors, recordings capturing their departure from the starting point cannot be played.

Entities

The only entity that stands out is Entity 415-A, while there are no other entities present. The following documentation record pertains specifically to this level’s entity.

[+] Open Article: Entity 415-A
[-] Close Article

Entity 415-A: The Fork

IETS

Entity Designation: Entity 415-A
Habitat: Level 415

Description:

The fork is a deceptive entity that mimics a trail, appearing almost identical to the level trail. It cunningly attaches itself to the trail, masquerading as a junction and enticing unsuspecting wanderers onto an incorrect route, putting their lives at great risk.

These treacherous forks can be easily identified by their flawed disguise, such as:

  • Misalignment with the original trail.
  • Narrower width.
  • Noticeable changes in material composition.
  • Slight variations in streetlight height compared to others or differing shapes.
  • No benches are found.
  • Trees appearing in the middle of the road.

Wanderers can easily distinguish this entity through simple judgment. However, despite the obvious flaws, it is prone to confusion with the level exit, thereby contributing to an escalation in the threat level of the fork.

Behavior:

The fork in the road does not actively seek out humans and exhibits clear hostility towards any living beings, entities or individuals who attempt to approach it. It subjects them to torment until their demise, after which their bodies vanish without a trace. It is speculated that the fork in the road uses its ability to decompose both the wanderers’ corpses and their possessions.

Through various records of wanderers encountering forks in the road, it has been observed that the entity frequently inflicts various forms of torment on trapped individuals. Subsequently, once the wanderers meet their demise at these forks in the road, their remains are systematically collected. The following compilation provides a comprehensive record of the diverse treatments to which Entity 415-A subjects its victims:

Record number:Lv415-5-A-01

Description: This event is commonly experienced by most wanderers who find themselves trapped within the entity. The Wanderers become ensnared in a cunningly crafted illusional trail, where the entrance to Entity 415-A gradually assimilates into the deceptive trail itself. The interior of this treacherous trail is manipulated by Entity 415-A, transforming it into a confined loop space that compels wanderers to endlessly return to their starting point regardless of how far they traverse in any direction. Devoid of sustenance or provisions, wanderers ultimately succumb to starvation within this illusory labyrinth due to an absence of supplies.

Record number:Lv415-5-A-02

Description: Event 02 is similar to Event 01, but the difference between them lies in the fact that Entity 415-A persistently shrinks and distorts space in order to expedite the demise of wanderers. It is highly probable that wanderers will succumb to fatal compression during their journey towards starvation, as originally intended. This occurrence is believed to stem from Entity 415-A’s prolonged period without sustenance, prompting it to capture fresh prey for immediate consumption.

Record number:Lv415-5-A-03

Description: The cause of Event 03 differs from Events 01 and 02. It is speculated that Entity 415-A has no interest in the wanderers and merely intends to engage in deceptive tactics with them. As the wanderers venture further into Entity 415-A, cracks of various sizes accompanied by vibrations emerge along the camouflaged trail in Event 03, posing a risk for the wanderers to inadvertently fall into these crevices. All communication was lost with those who fell into the cracks. However, there exists a potential means of escape during Event 03 by simply retracing your previous steps. The probability of Event 03 occurring is extremely rare.

Bases, Outposts and Communities

Due to the infinite and/or non-linear nature of Level 415 and the special effect of the starting point, wanderers exploring this level face significant challenges when it comes to encountering other wanderers unless they enter together. This also poses difficulties in establishing communities on Level 415, leading some groups to abandon the idea altogether. However, during their exploration, wanderers may encounter temporary bases established by other individuals, such as multiple outdoor tents along the trail, or small huts constructed from soil, wood, tin sheets, and/or cardboard. These temporary bases often contain a limited number of items, and many wanderers tend to leave behind excess belongings at these locations. To assist unfamiliar wanderers with information about Level 415, members of M.E.G. are encouraged to place paper reports regarding this level in any temporary base they come across during their exploration.

Entrances And Exits

Entrances

  • Level 432: Find a relatively dark corner within this level, curl up your body in the shadow of the corner, close your eyes and wait for a few seconds. The wanderer will feel their body moving rapidly, and after about a minute, they will feel like they are slowly stopping. The wanderer will successfully reach Level 415. This is also the only stable entrance to Level 415.
  • Level 563: There is a probability that the forest on the right side of the road will lead to Level 415.
  • Level 712: The door that appears upon entering the mountaintop has a very low probability of taking the wanderer to the Level 415 trail.

Exits

Leaving this level is relatively difficult, and some wanderers may even spend several years leaving, and unconventional No-clipping methods3 will not have any effect on Level 415.

  • Very few unique forks will lead to the levels in the following report, but due to the presence of Entity 415-A, the exit from the level is easily confused with Entity 415-A.

Discovered levels reached after leaving through a safe fork:

  • Level 39
  • Level 60
  • Level 77
  • Level 469
  • Level 901
