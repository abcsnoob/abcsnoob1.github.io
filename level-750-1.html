rating: +35+–x


Info

Content Warning:

Themes of alcohol consumption and intoxication.
I do not condone substance abuse. If you must, drink responsibly and in moderation.

Author's Notes:

Written by Endrew1

Imo nightclubs can be a magical experience. When you combine electrifying music, dazzling lighting,
uninhibited dancing and great friends, you feel——for a moment——infinite.

Inspired by my personal experiences clubbing and the Theatron (Colombia).
