Level 807

SURVIVAL DIFFICULTY:

Class habitable

  • {$one}
  • {$two}
  • {$three}

A picture of Level 807. Taken by B.N.T.G operative Amanda Rythers.

Description

Level 807 is one of the safest levels in the Backrooms. However, it contains non-Euclidean geography, as going in a straight line for long enough loops wanderers back to where they started. Despite this, one's chances of getting lost in the level are close to none. This is because the level is relatively small, with an area estimated to be less than 200 square kilometers1. The level follows a 24-hour-day-night cycle consistently, having a Sun similar to that of the Frontrooms but not a Moon.

The terrain of Level 807 is almost flat, making constructing settlements in the level very easy. The presence of the forest prevents settlements in the level from being discovered by unwanted visitors, which causes them to be relatively secure. The soil in the level is also incredibly fertile, allowing for easy crop growth. Additionally, the level also contains many natural springs, with water that is safe to drink.

The flora of Level 807 is consists primarily of pine trees and milkweed. However, Level 807 appears to have almost no fauna, despite being a forest level, only containing various colonies of carpenter ants and honeybee hives.

Level 807 contains a temperate climate similar to that of the Frontrooms, with temperatures ranging from 23.5°C to 27°C2. However, rainfall in the level is much more frequent than would be typical for a temperate climate, as the entire level rains 1-3 times every day. These rains vary in intensity and time, ranging from 30 minutes to 3 hours. The cause of this is hypothesized to be due to the level's overall small size as the clouds of the level are forced to occupy a small space. Because of this, it is recommended that one carries an umbrella with them to the level.

Resources

Level 807 contains a wide variety of resources including timber, honey and beeswax. However, most of the level's resources are contained underneath the ground, where a variety of ore veins can be located. These include, but are not limited to: iron, nickel, copper, and gold. Most settlements stationed in the level are focused on extracting these metals from underground.

Notable Bases, Outposts and Communities

Due to the level's high amount and free availability of resources, fertile soil and fresh water, settling in the level is incredibly easy, with the M.E.G, B.N.T.G and the U.E.C containing the largest outposts in the level. Listed here are the notable settlements of Level 807, though do note that many more minor outposts exist in the level.

Mine-B07

M.E.G - Base Cedar

  • Largest settlement in the level.
  • Open to trading and recruiting.
  • Mostly self-sustaining, with a spring nearby for water supply and a medium sized potato farm for basic food supply.
  • Commands Mine-B07 to extract metals from the level.
  • Around 100 operatives permanently stationed here.
  • Contains a makeshift blast furnace to smelt metals mined from the level.

B.N.T.G - Collection outpost

  • Second largest settlement in the level
  • Used to be part of Base Cedar before the spilt.
  • Pays Base Cedar a fee to smelt metals extracted by them.
  • Contains a bee farm.
  • Not self-sustaining.
  • Receives food imports from other levels and Base Cedar.
  • Not open to trade.

U.E.C - Unknown Name

  • Unknown amount of members.
  • Believed to be set up for resource collection.
  • Do not approach.
