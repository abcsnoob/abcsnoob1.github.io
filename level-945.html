Level 945 is the 946th Level of the Backrooms. It is an infinite ocean with an island at its center. It was discovered by Elijah Makaraig and was further explored by Team "Quick Match" leader, Micheal, and Narda Makaraig during a search and rescue operation.

11:10

An aerial shot of the island of Level 945.

Level 945 is the 946th Level of the Backrooms. It is a seemingly infinite ocean with an island at its center.

Description:

The currents of the ocean of Level 945 lead to an island theorized to be the center of the level itself. The island is a land mass with a lake at its center that is always submerged in a fog. It was discovered by Elijah Makaraig and was furthered explored by Team "Quick Match" leader, Micheal and Narda Makaraig during a search and rescue operation.

The level has a day and night cycle similar to that of the Frontrooms. Exploration of the lake at the center of the island has not been conducted by the M.E.G due to the thick fog, making it difficult to traverse in. Currently, there are no recorded entity encounters or sightings in the level.

  • Exploration Log - Team Quick Match - 1
  • Exploration Log - Team Quick Match - 2

16:50
After the corrupted file entered the system, we started searching through any level that fits the description of an infinite ocean with an island. Through enough searching we found that a certain senior archivist had recently travelled the level that seems to be repeatedly described in the file. They said to be careful because something is in the level. I don't know if they were bullshitting me, but there's no one and I've been on the island for hours now.

17:15
I found the girl in the boat with me. Narda was hiding under one of the bags I brought. I was going to contact Base Gamma to let them know that they lost her from their custody but she told me not to, practically begged me not to. I just gave up, she's probably terrified.

19:00
It's been two hours and still no sign of the girl's brother. The island is much more bigger than I thought. I think the forest is getting quieter too. Narda doesn't seem bothered by it too much.

19:40
The kid ran off! She just started running off. I caught up to her and she kept pointing at the distance and shaking my arm. I looked closer and I saw the ends of a fog.

--

[ RECOVERED AUDIO LOG ]
Micheal: Narda be careful! We don't know if there's anything in the island!

N. Makaraig: He wrote that he was here! The fog!

Micheal: There's no one here.

N. Makaraig: Elijah!

[ crunching of gravel can be heard. Narda continues to shout for Elijah]

[ loud splashing and sloshing ]

N. Makaraig: [ distantly ] Do you see him?!

Micheal: Wait… Wait!

[ more splashing can be heard ]

Micheal: I found him! He's alright!

Note: After we got out of there, Elijah was unresponsive. We tried asking him how he was able to send the original corrupted file of the level but he wouldn't say a word. He wouldn't even talk to his sister. Currently the Makaraig siblings are in Base Omega, and hopefully they don't just run off again this time.

Bases, Outposts and Communities

There are no bases, outposts and or communities in Level 945.

Entrances And Exits:

Entrances

  • Following the sound of running water in level-18 will take you to the level.
  • Stepping in a puddle in Level 200 has a rare chance of taking you to Level 945.

Exits

  • Staying in the island for a week will take you to Level 62.
  • Holding your breath underwater for two minutes will take you to Level 944.
  • Sailing against the currents of the level will take you to Level 4.
