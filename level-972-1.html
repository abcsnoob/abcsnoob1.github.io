Level 972 - "Let's See Your Sins."
rating: +26+–x

SURVIVAL DIFFICULTY:

Class Atlas

  • Unsafe
  • Confirmed Presence of Deities
  • Considerable Religious Presence

An unknown wanderer being judged on Level 972 Adult Facelings dematerialize after a certain amount of time.

Introduction

Level 972 is the 973rd level of the Backrooms.

This level appears to be a large field, with perpetually clear skies and a large population of Adult Facelings.

Description

This level seems to occur on a large field with a partly cloudy sky. This sky has a gas that emits from it, assumed to be a gas version of amphetamine. Adult Facelings completely crowd sections of this level and isolate wanderers from each other. These facelings are fixed and cannot be forcefully moved, making it nearly impossible to move through them. The facelings move towards the sun's direction in the sky; because wanderers cannot forcefully move them, the Facelings will eventually push the wanderer with them.

l00K b3K

Addendum 1

Level 972 appears to take wanderers at random, as proven by the overwhelming account of wanderers claiming that they have seemed to see visions of facelings before their capture. These facelings are not facelings, but other humans disfigured far beyond comprehension to the point where their faces ceased to exist. This was found out when u/bitcha33m0fo posted on Reddit saying he had found a faceling wearing the same clothing he had last seen from his partner before going missing. Over time, more and more accounts of this have risen, to the point where it can be fully supported that these facelings are instead extremely disfigured, kidnapped humans.


These facelings will constantly push beings until they feel the presence of some higher power, known as "False God." This is not any specific higher power found in the frontrooms but rather an omnipotent and omniscient anomaly found within this level. It will begin to speak to said person (most likely telepathically) and will eventually "judge" the person to see if their sins outweigh their good deeds. Along with that, the amount of adult facelings begins to dwindle.

l00K b3K

Addendum 1.5
A conclusion has been reached throughout multiple tests and drone interviews with the false God and observation of these facelings. False God appears to have convinced itself in its lies. It believes that it is God and that everyone in the backrooms must worship it. It assumes that the only way to "worship" it is to undergo the faceling transformation process. It dispatches these facelings to steal wanderers at random and "judge" them.


If the anomaly deems them "Holy," the person rises into the clouds above and will fall into Level 63. As a result of being "Holy," they will also find that extra supplies are now on themselves, ranging from Almond Water to other small items, like weapons and food. Even Royal Rations has been reported a few times. If it deems the person "a sinner," they will fall through the ground and land onto Level 666, which subsequently determines their fate from there.

Bases, Outposts, and Communities

Due to the sheer impossibility of human interaction within this level, there are no known bases, outposts, or communities.

Addendum 2

There is one cult on this level known as "followers of >\%{{*=." They are a highly hostile group of humans who have or are undergoing the human-faceling transformation process. They have been seen kidnapping wanderers by stalking them. They worship the false God on this level and appear within the field and the city of this level. They are the facelings that surround any wanderer entering the level at the field level. It is theorized that these facelings are instead trapping the wanderer, and they know and want to ensure that the wanderers are trapped between them, presumably to restrict their movement. On the city portion of this level, they act as sonars of some sort for the false God.

Entities

Adult Facelings

This level is crowded with an abundance of adult facelings, which cannot be forcefully moved. These facelings will completely lock the person from moving further into the crowd. They don't
seem to disappear or move anywhere, so they are most likely there to act as a barrier.

False God

This entity is exclusive to this level, and it has the appearance of the wanderer's depiction of God. It has complete omnipotence and omniscience within this level. This being is only visible via individual hallucinations, allowing multiple wanderers in entirely different places to see it simultaneously, giving False God an apparent ability to be ubiquitous. This entity also can noclip wanderers in and out of this level. this entity is solely confined to this level, theorizing that false God is the level itself."

Entrances And Exits

Entrances

Holding any religious item from any religion may trigger no-clipping randomly into this level.

Exits

Once the anomalous entity inhabits this level finds you, if it judges you "worthy," you will be no-clipped into Level 63, and being deemed "a sinner" will no-clip you into Level 666.

Level 972 - Your sins have been shown.

SURVIVAL DIFFICULTY:

Class amended

  • {$one}
  • {$two}
  • {$three}

The City portion of Level 972.

Description

Level 972 takes the appearance of a destroyed world. People can hear the screams and shouts of humans at this level in significant distortion. This level has an entirely red sky that emits a gas, which stresses the wanderer quickly. One can become lost within level 972 quite easily, as there are hordes of entities blocking certain roads, meaning that traversing level 972 is almost impossible. What makes this worse is that wanderers must cross level 972. If they do not run constantly, they will be found by FALSE GOD. If they get caught, their fate from there is so dangerous that no wanderer has witnessed it without dying to it.

    "N0""Y3S"

    Addendum 3

    This level seems to be separated into two isolated portions: the field and the city. The field, as described above, can only be reached by having some religious item on you, and the city's entrance is by being randomly chosen by a cult follower and brought here by them. You cannot reach either area via each other, and they seem to act like individual levels.

Outposts

God Save Us/Halflings
This base comprises religious wanderers who have been trapped on this level, they are constantly on the run, and it is nearly impossible to find them. They seem to worship the false God on this level. They are not hostile, but it is best not to interact with them as they have been seen attempting to sabotage wanderers into the hands of the FALSE GOD.

Entities

FALSE GOD

This entity is the main threat of this level and essentially chases around wanderers. It knows the location of all wanderers in this level, and its speed is just barely slower than the average male running speed. This entity is exceptionally hostile and has complete sentience, although it cannot be reasoned with

Other Entities including hounds, smilers, skin stealers, reviooks, deathmoths, clumps, and wranglers appear on this level regularly. Instances of Denizens have also been rumored, but the M.E.G has yet to confirm this.

Entrances and Exits

Entrances

The only proven way to enter Level 972 is to be brought here by a cult member.
