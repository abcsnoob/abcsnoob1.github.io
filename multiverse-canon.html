Dear friends and colleagues,

Welcome. Truth be told, I have had my mind set on the multiverse ever since my first article for this amazing writing community, Level 365: "Your Worst Enemy," posted on June 13, 2022. My allegiance to the concept is evident in most of my submissions (see Goerman's Multiverse Chronology below), whether it is a level, entity, object, phenomenon, or tale.

What a wild and wonderful ride!

Goerman

The multiverse exists in any combination of three states:

  • Physical - matter and space
  • Temporal - time-related
  • Ethereal - psychic or metaphysical

Of course, it is only a theory.

Dr. John Rhinehart, PhD

This canon is owned by

Robert Goerman

Mankind is like life in a drop of water.

We are ignorant of what lies beyond.

For some, being trapped in the Backrooms is their first taste of another reality in the multiverse. My story is different. Ordinary people on Earth are discovering extraordinary things, and I found myself documenting these events. People are encountering nonhuman beings in bustling cities, in the middle of rural nowhere, and even in their homes. This is bigger than landed UFOs and so-called "aliens." How can we explain chilling accounts of temporal anomalies or witnesses trespassing on parallel worlds and returning while still walking on Earth?

When given the choice, I made the Backrooms my destiny. This place embraces the multiverse to the nth degree. I have awakened. 

Anne Dunne
Former author and paranormal scholar
Psychic warrior M.E.G. Team "Epiphany"

Premise

Earth and our vast universe are but a single drop. Beyond this observable cosmos, there is a literal ocean of other universes. The Backrooms reality is, apparently, an important "hub" for portions of the multiverse. It is believed that some Backrooms entities, such as Sightless Seers, are extra-dimensional in origin.

One storyline of this canon follows the exploits of Anne Dunne, Amy Cochrane, and Dr. John Rhinehart, PhD, of the M.E.G. Team "Epiphany" as they explore the Backrooms and discover its ties to the multiverse. It involves threats like the Fun War1 and the larger Subtle War2. Humanity is not a welcome species.

There are many ways in which alternate realities might influence the Backrooms. Other storylines must exist. Feel free to discover the treasures of the multiverse.

Contribution Suggestions

New contributions and collaborators are very welcome.

Here are a few ideas:

  • Articles involving time, time anomalies and temporal manipulation.
  • Articles involving other realms, realities, dimensions, or universes touching, affecting, or merging with the Backrooms.
  • Articles that utilize entities, objects, phenomena, and more that might originate from those realms, realities, dimensions, or universes touching, affecting, or merging with the Backrooms.
  • Remember, this is the multiverse. Everything, everywhere, all at once.

Canon Crossing

  • Canon crossing is encouraged!
  • Once again, this is the multiverse.
  • Have fun writing!

Goerman's Multiverse Chronology

[PLEASE CLICK HERE FOR SUMMARY]
[CLICK HERE TO CLOSE]

1969

  • While Americans land on the moon, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics conducts "Operation Wonderland" in secret. This project no-clips KGB scientist Nikolai Ilyushin - (codename White Rabbit) - now known as Doctor John Rhinehart, PhD, to the Backrooms.

1970

  • Once his survival and new identity become stabilized, Rhinehart begins researching the process of causation in the Backrooms, which leads to the yin and yang of Phenomenon 7 - "Liminal Echo" and Phenomenon 33 - "Autocorrect" being eventually documented. Liminal Echo exists as a widespread condition of the Backrooms that is detrimental to wanderers. Autocorrect, on the other hand, manifests in situations that are immediately beneficial to targeted individuals.

1973

  • Deacon Duncan no-clips into the Backrooms, becomes Partygoer Zero and creates Partygoers.

1977

  • Amy Cochrane no-clips into the Backrooms on her 18th birthday (July 7th) and is found by Alberto Sanchez.

2010

  • First MiB attack that Marvin Matthews witnessed, pre-M.E.G.
  • The SS Adventure is built on Earth and disappears.

2011

  • Three groups merge to form M.E.G., but it is not named or official yet.
  • "The SS Fun" (actually the cruise liner Adventure from the Frontrooms) is now the anomalous headquarters for the Partygoers. It maneuvers around the Backrooms by no-clipping from level to level.

2012

  • M.E.G. is made official.
  • Second MiB attack and Marvin Matthews is now a high-ranking M.E.G. member.
  • Team “Party Over” is created, led by David Overbrook, whose law enforcement mindset is stubbornly convinced that Partygoers are not anomalous, but are actually a cannibalistic wanderer cult disguised as entities.

2013

  • Forty years after the fact, a dying Daniel Kebab gives a "deathbed confession" to tell his story about the creation of Partygoer Zero.

2014

  • Levels 365 and 710 are discovered. Teams “Looking Glass” and “Harbinger” are formed. Amy Cochrane and Alberto Sanchez join “Harbinger.”
  • A mysterious "One in the Ring" on Level 710 is studying human mental processes. It is believed that other realms, maybe even the Frontrooms, can be sighted within the ancient Harbinger Arch directly below the Ring in the sky. It is also rumored that a few wanderers have allegedly stepped through the arch without emerging from the other side.
  • M.E.G. confirms that Partygoers are truly undead chimeras and Team “Party Over” is disbanded. Rhinehart studies Partygoers to find weaknesses.

2015

  • Anne Dunne no-clips from Earth to Level 365 after being warned to drop her paranormal research by the Men in Black, joins Team “Looking Glass.”
  • Sightless Seers studied by Rhinehart.

2016

  • Seer Tea is explored by Anne and Rhinehart early this year.
  • M.E.G. Security Technician Alberto Sanchez of Team "Harbinger" is "enchanted" by a Man in Black. (Wanderers are directed to The Visitor section of the article describing Level 710 for more information.) Another Security Technician, Amy Cochrane, is ordered by Station Chief Marvin Matthews to rush Sanchez to M.E.G. Base Alpha immediately for treatment. We learn of an MiB plot against Anne Dunne.
  • Alberto Sanchez is Dead On Arrival at Base Alpha. Amy Cochrane meets Anne Dunne in Anne's Lament and decides to protect her, ignoring orders not to get involved, and hopes to avenge Alberto.
  • Anne and Amy flee from their deadly pursuer in Anne's Escape. We learn that the Men in Black are not human and Anne saves Amy from MiB enchantment. We see something else: a mysterious power is helping the girls.
  • Anne Dunne sets events into motion and the One in the Ring on Level 710 is finally identified before it sacrifices itself to destroy the Man in Black in Anne's Farewell. She walks between worlds before finally finding Level 414, physically and magically transformed.
  • Team "Epiphany" is formed with Dr. John Rhinehart, PhD (science), Anne Dunne (psychic), and Amy Cochrane (swords).

2017

  • Liminal Forest or Level 345, also known as Nostalgic Park and Happy Land, is a Backrooms theme park on the threshold of human consciousness. The park supersedes time and space and something there is studying human memories and emotions.

2018

  • Entity 123 is a spectral being responsible for the demise of several residents of these Backrooms and the Frontrooms. It often takes the form of an evil doppelganger (or double) of the victim. It can also manifest as a horrifying event or circumstances beyond the victim's control, with every encounter revolving around the name of its prey. Is your name on its list?

2019

  • The wanderer in bed 13 during the Happy Dance incident at the LVL-4 Sleep Shelter has been identified. A wallet found in a backpack contained both his driver's license and military identification, revealing our visitor to be "19-year-old" Brian Foulkes of Cleveland, Ohio. If this ID is not counterfeit, then the date of birth for Mr. Foulkes has not occurred. The credit cards in his wallet display expiration dates more than twenty years from now. Three slightly worn coins (two dimes and one quarter) display mintage marks for years that are yet to exist.

M.E.G. discovers Level 246 or “Dinosaur Alley,” Team “Epiphany” investigates. Team “Watchdog” formed later this year

2020

  • M.E.G. discovers “SS Adventure” link to Partygoers
  • Phenomenon 30 - "Orbs" are found in digital photographs across the Earth and the Backrooms. What are they?
  • Entity 190 - The Men in Black can't stop other "alien" beings from visiting Earth and interacting with humans. They can only appear after the fact and eradicate threats to Earth's quarantine.

2022

  • Planes of existence were created beyond space and time to confine the most dangerous criminals and beings across the multiverse. Diabolical sabotage during one failed escape attempt sent one such extra-dimensional penal colony crashing into the Backrooms to become Level 234.
  • Level 246, also known as "Dinosaur Alley," has been described as "the sinkhole of the multiverse." Time makes this level unique. Every inch of terrain here inexorably echoes certain years on Earth, and "time travel," in a manner of speaking, is accomplished by walking across this temporal landscape. The "Age of Consequences" plays hide-and-seek among lofty plateaus. Mount Olympus holds Facelings from the distant future, aliens, and cryptids, while the One of the Divine Path tells Anne Dunne about the "Subtle War." The fate of humanity is at stake.

2023

  • Fun War between wanderers and Partygoers begins.

2025

  • Fun War: Turning Point details crucial battles in the war with the Partygoers.

Try one of these levels for inspiration:

1). A multiverse penal colony crashed into the Backrooms to become Level 234. There are ninety-six species imprisoned on this penal colony. Only four are described and mentioned by name: Vrag, "Demon," Tattatt, and Aelok. This means that wanderers can discover as many as ninety-two different entities!

2). Level 246 has been described as "the sinkhole of the multiverse." This Backrooms vivarium features dinosaurs, humans, and cryptids such as Bigfoot and Mothman, as well as your typical gray aliens and gnomes. So-called erratics (items and lifeforms) from various moments in time (past, present, and future) from across the multiverse can appear without much warning.

We cannot forget to mention the B.N.T.G. gold mine: crystals from the Stygian Sea. These onyx particles are powerful sensory and emotional experiences from across the multiverse in crystallized form. Holding a single crystal in the palm of one's hand releases that memory into the mind. The sensation lasts for the duration of contact with the flesh. It can be used over and over again, without end. The trade group painstakingly sorts these emotions and places the tiny bits into glass vials, manufacturing bottles of everlasting heaven or hell for eager customers.

3). Liminal Forest or Level 345, also known as Nostalgic Park and Happy Land, is a Backrooms theme park ripe for some wild tales. This park uses a pay-as-you-go system. Riding rides, purchasing food and beverages, or participating in one of the many Midway-style games and attractions costs visitors one (1) ticket each. A single ticket sells for one visitor memory and is purchased in strips of ten. These memories are chosen at the discretion of park management and all transactions are final.

Multiverse Disclaimer:

"Given that the multiverse is everything, everywhere, all at once, it should come as no surprise that Frontrooms/Backrooms knowledge and events might differ from universe to universe. The moment that you imagine something cannot possibly happen, it already has."

We post this disclaimer whenever an article contradicts known lore.
Footnotes
1. The Fun War is a series of skirmishes between chimeric, undead entities known as Partygoers and wanderers in the Backrooms. Other entities like Facelings and Frowning Husks are drawn into the fray.
2. In the Subtle War, the enemies of mankind sail the seas of time to make tiny changes here and there. Subtle changes.
