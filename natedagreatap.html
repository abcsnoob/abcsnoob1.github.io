About me

Face Reveal >:D

Hi! I’m Nate, but you probably know me as Natedagreat563. I’m a pretty active member of the community, and you’ll probably see me frequently on the Discord. I officially joined the community in late January of 2021, but I’ve technically been a part of the community since about October 2020! I joined staff in August 2021 and became a moderator, but took a long break in January 2022. I rejoined staff in August 2022 and left again after about a year. I mostly hang around the server when I'm bored.

I’m an 19 year old guy with Autism, and I like to do a lot of things. I play several instruments, and have perfect pitch. I also love to cook, bake, go on long walks and purchase a bunch of random stuff from a store or gas station, and code. I like to draw but I’m not great at it so I don’t really show people my art. I am bilingual in Spanish and am trying to learn Arabic. I’m relatively active on the Spanish branch and have even done a few translations for them! I LOVE music and hope to major in it when I go to college. I’m also super into lore-heavy indie type stuff including but not limited to Five Nights at Freddy’s (I know that one’s pretty shocking isn’t it), Gravity Falls, Marvel, etc.

I’m one of the more tougher critics on the site, and extremely selective. I’m a huge stickler for thorough descriptions, unique concepts, proper usage of dashes, and flow. I reflect this in my writing as well, always enforcing my personal “At least 5 paragraphs in the description” rule. I also am an expert at offset pages and known colloquially as “The King of CCSearch.” Are you having trouble finding an image? Simply ask The King himself, and he shall provide! In all seriousness, I get really lucky with CCSearch and manage to find some fantastic images for both myself and other people. If you need some assistance, shoot me a dm or ping! I’m happy to help ;).

Entity 600
“The Saberzoa”

    • _
    Access File (+)Close File (-)

    3rd Place Scrapcon 2022 Entry

    Entity 600 - The Saberzoa

    Authored solely by Natedagreat563, based off of SnomWriting’s original draft.

    “Saberzoa are often used as messengers throughout the Backrooms in combination with their related object, Spirit Links. Saberzoa seem naturally drawn to the gems used on these objects. To deliver a message via Saberzoa, give it a letter while telling it the name of the person it is destined to. While the exact methods are unknown, Saberzoa can track the recipient and deliver the message regardless of distance.”

    Oh boy, this article definitely has a VERY long backstory. Where do I begin? This all started a while ago back in April 2021. Me and a bunch of people were talking on TS and suddenly conceptualized an entity altogether. What if we had Jellyfish with hats in the backrooms? Preposterous, no? This group included Karmaa, Snom, Saria, Tag, Zask, Deer, and myself. Originally, we were gonna make this for the Omenscript Canon and release a bunch of articles all connected to each other. However, as these things go, the project eventually fell through and just… never happened. We had old unfinished articles rotting away in the sandbox seemingly forever. That was until Scrapcon happened. I saw the article was once again up for grabs and took the opportunity. I took the original idea for the saberzoa and turned it into its own thing, not connected to any sort of canon. Afterwards, I touched up my old draft for the hats themselves. Now, here we are! Almost a year and a half later, these guys are finally on site, and it’s amazing to see that finally happen. I hope you enjoy this article after all the craziness it went through to be here.

Enigmatic Entity
“Icarus Procidens”

    • _
    Access File (+)Close File (-)

    Featured Article for July 2022
    Annual Feature Article from 2022

    Enigmatic Entity - Icarus Procidens

    Authored solely by Natedagreat563.

    “Choices are some of the worst things that can happen to a man. One mistake can cause him eternal regret, anguish, and sorrow. The thought of ‘if only’ plagues his mind, constantly bombarding him with fantasies of what could have occurred, had things gone differently. The wonder when his decisions will catch up to him will eat at his mind until he finally loses control. All the power in the world, but still too little to do the one worthwhile thing you wish it could provide.”

    A year. That’s how long this crazy article took. Man does it feel surreal to finally publish it. This entity came to me in the middle of the most random movie ever: A house with a clock in its walls. I was watching it with my family, and at the end the idea just popped into my head. Why? I literally have no idea. I immediately rushed to the dms of Etoisle to pitch the idea and Etoisle loved it. Minor suggestions and tweaks were provided that majorly helped early on.

    I finally started the first draft in August of 2021. I actually made Icarus’s design of skeleton with cloak based off of an old sans OC I made in an undertale roleplay in middle school. That’s quite embarrassing to admit, but true. I didn’t have a very clear plan of what to do. Usually when I write my articles, I have a very clear beginning and end, but this was very on the fly. Counting up the amount of times I’ve fully rewritten an offset, it would be 13. And major rewrites to various portions were made more times than I could even begin to count. Offset 1 alone was 7 rewrites. Offset 2 had a complete conceptual rehaul 3 times. Offset 3 was rewritten once but took many months of mental conceptualization. Offset 4 was an epiphany that came to me that took a while to pull off.

    Here we are. A year later on July 30th, and the article is finally done. The reaction the community has given me to the most ambitious article ever posted was more than relieving, and proved to me that all my work finally paid off. I think I’ve finally written my masterpiece. Man does it feel good.
