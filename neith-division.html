Our Explanation

We are The NEITH Division, a secretive splinter division of the Backrooms Bureau of Administration and Research (B.B.A.R). We were created in 2006 by our founder nicknamed "GRELL".
Grell was the first of us to realize that the B.B.A.R.'s rivals and the B.B.A.R. itself posed problems for the stability of civilization in the Backrooms. He realized that future wars were inevitable and how dangerous they would become in an environment like the Backrooms.

Our Insignia

All of that helped us realize what must be done in order to create a proper Government. The B.B.A.R seeks to enforce law and order rather than control, but we are not the same. We know that, in order for humanity to reach the level of control they had in the Frontrooms, they must seize the opportunity given to our parent government to control the other factions from the shadows. With groups like the Major Explorer Group and The Unbound Explorers Coalition butting heads with each other; they have already proved that war will inevitably be provoked by their attempt to overthrow each other.

As of now, we realize that we, the NEITH division, and the B.B.A.R. have the high ground in terms of technological advances. Therefore, we must absolutely prevent this cataclysm from happening.

Log of Persons of Interest / Relevant Organizations

Persons of Interest

Callum Andrews

Classification: ■ Allied
Description:

Callum Andrews is the CEO of the allied group: “ the Backrooms Robotics” as well as the leader of SOL. He is one of our greatest allies and provides our organization with technology able to perform the tasks needed in order to prevent the other groups from surpassing us in terms of technological advances.

Nara Kagan / The Augur

classification: ■ Capture if found.
Description:

Nara Kagan was a fortune teller which has had reports of being perfectly accurate in every single one of his interactions. Nowadays he is currently on the run from M.E.G officials. It is currently believed that he resides somewhere within Zone-004 but this is only speculation. We need to keep an eye on him, as someone with such an abnormal ability could easily be exploited by other factions.

Groups of Relevance

Backrooms Robotics / SOL

Threat classification: ■ Allied

They are the ones who made the breakthroughs of the NEITH division possible. Yellow and the Backrooms Robotics are one of our greatest allies and provide us with the technology needed to advance and modify existing Assets1

The Anti Entity Agency (A.E.A)

Threat classification: ■ Allied

An agency of specialists who acts as pest control for entities that cause us problems. They will get most jobs done for a hefty payment, which we can always afford. They are very professional at their jobs.

The Shuffle Squad

Threat classification: ■ Allied for now

One of our newfound allies and greatest bases of operation. After their Leader "J. Nesko, The Conductor2" was slain by the Terrorist Group known to us as The Red Syndicate we seized the opportunity to claim the newfound land by allying ourselves with The Shuffle Squad in promise of keeping their level stable and active.

However, Their Alliance with us may be threatened by the possibility of J. Nesko being alive via communication Through RAI-317-C.
Heavy monitoring is recommended.

The Onslaught Station

Threat classification: ■ Allied for now

One of our newfound allies and greatest bases of operation. In a similar fashion to how Shuffle Squad lost their Conductor and almost their entire zone, The Onslaught Station lost theirs and almost went out of business from its instability. After the near destruction of Zone-158 we made a deal with the members of Onslaught Junkies United to protect the Onslaught Station if they allowed us to use the level as a Training Outpost for newer recruits of Task Force Pyramid3.

Once again however, Their Alliance with us may be threatened by the possibility of Ozkon4 Being alive, luckily however there doesn't appear to be any proof of Ozkon returning.

Other Groups of Relevance

The M.E.G.

Threat classification: ■ Interact only when needed

The most famous of the Groups. They are a Faction which allowed Humanity to traverse lands they would normally know nothing about. They possess a large portion of territories within The Backrooms and have a public database in which which they share all kinds of information regarding the Backrooms. Whilst being a grey area of the higher factions they have shown cases in which they have made controversial decisions or have raised tensions with other factions. Heavy monitoring is recommended.

The U.E.C.

Threat classification: ■ Interact only when needed

The Unbound Explorers Coalition is a large Backrooms group and perhaps one of the most dangerous of the main 3 factions. They rival The Likes of M.E.G and the B.B.A.R in terms of territory, though unlike the others they made their earnings by using more controversial means.

The Eyes Of Argos

Threat classification: ■ Do not interact unless forced to.

An ancient yet still active vigilante group supposedly worshiping the Pantheon deity "Argos". They are an active threat to our operations due to their efficiency and surprisingly advanced arsenal.

The Masked Maidens

Threat classification: ■ Do not interact unless forced to.

A group of Masked vigilantes who frequently target those who they consider to be “corrupt”. They actively hack into our databases and attack members of our organization. Despite not being a large group, they are a massive threat to our operations because of how surprisingly efficient they are.

Amongst here is a log of our known affiliates and members.

GRELL

Rank Status: ■ Administrator and Creator of The Neith Division.

Mike Leonard

Rank Status: ■ 3rd In Command. Currently the manager of The Onslaught Station and Neon Shuffle Outposts.

Daniel Lance

Our Task Forces

Task Force JEWEL

The agents and spies of our organization. Their purpose is to penetrate the other groups’ territories and, above all, to transmit valuable information from their databases to ours. Task force JEWEL also plays a role in obtaining items from the outposts of other factions. We owe them a lot for all the information about the Zones and Assets they obtained for us.

Task Force PYRAMID

The Masked Soldiers of our organization. These are the people who are sent to directly infiltrate or head into the other groups' territories. They are also tasked with capturing our Persons of Interest / Assets. Usually, they are the ones getting their hands dirty and thanks to them, we can be safe and protected during our operations. The task force PYRAMID operates in numerous locations.

Task Force IRIS

The scientists / engineers of our organization. The IRIS branch is one of the most important of our organization. They are the ones responsible for our equipment, our weapons and the modification of Assets. Without them, we wouldn't be able to thrive like we are now.
Task Force IRIS is stationed in numerous designated Zones5

RAI = Registered Asset Item

RAE = Registered Asset Entity

Zone = Levels.

Thanks to the combined efforts of all the members and allies that took part in our efforts, we had managed to capture and modify the many Assets and Creatures of The Backrooms to the organization's benefits. The following is a list of Assets and their modified counterparts:

Affected Asset Before Neith Improvements Upon Neith Improvements Successful results?
RAE-66 A parasitical reaction caused by Zone-88's Shockers. When a human is fused with a shocker their conscience will be erased and they will be transformed into a Shocker Ghoul A parasitical reaction caused by Zone-88's Shockers. When groups of people interact with Shockers, they will be affected by RAE-66 and be transformed into Shocker Ghouls. Success
RAE-131-A An Entity which appears as a skeletal being made of electrical static. RAE-131-A uses the screens found on Zones and hunts through them, by using the TV screens as its own portal entrances to hunt its prey. An artificial intelligence system used by our organization which will summon skeletal beings to prevent intrusive entities and wanderers from maliciously acting against our Staff. Mostly a Success.
RAE-220 The 3rd child of the Pantheon deity "Gatekeeper" which takes the form a black house cat with glowing white eyes and black liquid leaking out of its ears. When fed, Entity will regurgitate a key that, when used allows users to go to any zone they wish to. None. The creature escaped before experimentation could begin Failure6
RAI-317-B A series of small transparent handguns created by the Conductor of Zone-317 which is identified by the Neon Shuffle as "Phantom Wormholes". When fired, the pistols will teleport the user to any place that the bullet lands. Living things hit by these pistols will not be affected and instead, they will be injured in the same way as if they had been hit by a regular bullet. A series of small transparent handguns that when paired with a NEITH marker will make drawn on surfaces act like a checkpoint. upon use the modified Asset will teleport the user to any drawn surfaces. Success, however the items are limited in number. Efforts to manufacture the Asset is underway
RAI-71 A highly volatile weapon resembling the Frontroom gun model Gyrojet MkII. Contains high destructive power, and can anomalously reload after all bullets are used up, but bullet trajectory is highly inaccurate, as well as having a chance to explode upon firing. Rate of fire is also horrendous, with a firing rate of 5 minutes per shot. A relatively accurate weapon using RAI-28 as a coolant to prevent explosions. Maintains high destructive capability as well as decent rate of fire. Success, however the items are limited in number. Efforts to manufacture the Asset is underway.
