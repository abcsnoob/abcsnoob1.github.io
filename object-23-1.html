"I don't usually come across burglars in the Cygnus Archives. But when I do, I send them packing with my trusty shotgun loaded with Clumpshot, the signature ammo of the Gilded Armory."
-Blanche Von Haderach

"It works well on wretches too."
-M.E.G. Overseer "Kat"

Those pesky Clumps are hounding you again aren't they? Say no more! One blast of our signature ammo, Clumpshot, is enough to blast them to kingdom come! With our patented technology, the pellets detonate in the target after penetration, ripping apart the insides of those menacing monsters! 24 packs now for just 1ℬ, or our Father's Day special: a 36 pack and a shotgun of choice for 5ℬ!

"Ever since Clumpshot became popular, I've had a harder time impaling people on my spear. This stuff really stings, you know? Buy it if you don't wanna be impaled."
-Tiny

Development

You're probably wondering about the story behind our signature ammo. It was early 2005. Without groups like Emstable to bring about order, many wanderers were fending for themselves. Bandits preyed on the kindness of the Ariane Circle, and no one knew who to trust. Our founder, Miles McCain, took it upon himself to invent a new ammunition to protect his family. His invention: Clumpshot! It was revolutionary! An overnight success on Level 13!

"I never leave home without it."
-Chasoderma James IV of Venosha Clan Eterlados

Where to find it

You can purchase your very own pack of Clumpshot at any of our franchises! Our most popular ones are next to Malt Mart on Level 11 and in the capital of Boveta on Level 650.
