We at Backrooms Robotics are proud to announce our latest product: RoboPets! RoboPets are the newest, high-tech, cutest companions you will ever have! You can customize your pet to your liking as you order it from the Backrooms Robotics website(link at the end of this informative article)!

Description

Introducing the brand new RoboPets! RoboPets are a customizable, cute companion that can help their wandering owner with any task that they need! They are designed to track distance and recognize paths to make travel easy, obey your and only your every command using our top-notch voice recognition technology, and have been programmed with a self-defense mode and the M.E.G. database to recognize anything you may come across on your journeys, along with you being able to track them with the RoboPets app! They are built with durable, entity-resistant titanium, fire-resistant faux fur, and even have a special compartment in their lower abdomen area to store Almond Water and other valuables! RoboPets are the perfect wandering companion for surviving the Backrooms. Never have to worry about entity attacks or wandering alone again, and order your RoboPet today!

RoboPets come in a variety of pet types, patterns, and colors to appeal to their owner! There are six different general pet types, with each one being customizable in all sorts of ways: Bird RoboPet, Cat RoboPet, Dog RoboPet, Gecko RoboPet, Hamster RoboPet, and Rabbit RoboPet. Parts of each general build can be swapped out for other parts that are more to your liking, all of which can be done during the ordering process on the Backrooms Robotics website!

Note: Each RoboPet has the Backrooms Robotics logo and serial number on its back left foot for identification.

Bird RoboPet

Bird RoboPet

Bird RoboPets are generally designed similar to a parrot or parakeet. They stand at about a foot tall and weigh three pounds. They have a hookbill beak and globular eyes, with a thin layer of feathers that mimic birds from the Frontrooms.

Bird RoboPets can fly up to 30 feet high, which makes them very good for scouting out levels and areas. They can also rest on your shoulder for easy transportation. Their form of self-defense utilizes their large beak and sharp talons, being able to pierce through 0.25-inch1 glass. They can also record conversations and repeat what you say by tapping their head when you want to start and tapping again when you want to stop.

Bird Robopets have a starting price of 10 Karma, though the price may differ based on customizations.

Cat RoboPet

Cat RoboPet

Cat RoboPets are generally designed to be similar to a short-haired tabby, with striped patterns varying from each person's custom order. They often stand at one foot tall when on all fours and 2.5 feet tall when on the hind legs, and they weigh around five pounds. They have a wide headframe, round paws, and are covered in faux fur to mimic a cat from the Frontrooms.

Cat RoboPets can run up to 25mph and can jump up to five feet high. They can also easily distract and trick most entities, along with having the ability to hide in very small spaces. Their form of self-defense utilizes their sharp claws on all four paws and their canine teeth, both of which are made with solid steel that can pierce through the skin of most entities. They have night vision and excellent hearing, making them very useful for exploring dark areas or avoiding entities.

Cat Robopets have a starting price of 15 Karma, though the price may differ based on customizations.

Dog RoboPet

Dog RoboPet

Dog RoboPets are generally designed to be similar to a dog of the Beagle breed. They have folded ears, round eyes, and large noses. They often stand at 1.25 feet tall when on all fours and 2.5 feet tall when on the hind legs, and they weigh around five pounds. They have a large, muscular body, large paws, a long muzzle, and are covered in faux fur to mimic a dog from the Frontrooms.

Dog RoboPets can run up to 15mph, and can jump up to three feet high. They have a very keen sense of smell and can sense and recognize any entity within a 1-mile radius. Their form of self-defense mostly consists of their sharp teeth, which they can use to injure any common entity. Their sense of smell not only helps them be aware of entities nearby but of fellow people and resources as well, making them a very good companion for exploring.

Dog Robopets have a starting price of 15 Karma, though the price may differ based on customizations.

Gecko RoboPet

Gecko RoboPet

Gecko RoboPets are generally designed to be similar to a crested gecko. They have 240º vision, protective scales covering their body, and webbed feet. They are often seven inches long and weigh 50 grams. They have small bodies, short legs, and large tails.

Gecko RoboPets can run up to 9mph, making them the slowest out of all of the RoboPets. However, Gecko RoboPets are also the smallest, making them easy to carry and hide, and they are also the only RoboPets that can climb walls. They accomplish this using their feet, which have thousands of microscopic hairs that allow them to stick to the wall with ease, making it very easy to scope out small areas and areas you would not be able to reach otherwise. Along with this, Gecko RoboPets can camouflage with any environment using our latest color-matching technology. Their form of self-defense is mostly centered around their claws and teeth. However, they are unable to do significant harm to enemies, and work best as distractions.

Gecko Robopets have a starting price of 5 Karma, though the price may differ based on customizations.

Hamster RoboPet

Hamster RoboPet

Hamster RoboPets are generally designed to look similar to a hamster of the Syrian breed. They have large front teeth, small paws, and round bodies. They are often 8 inches long and weigh around 5 oz. They have short limbs, large eyes, and rounded ears.

Hamster RoboPets can run up to 10 miles an hour, and they can run up to 12 miles an hour using their complementary RoboPet ball. Hamster RoboPets are the second smallest RoboPet, making them easy to carry and hide. Their cheeks have similar functionality to the Worn Sack. They can store any item with no capacity limits, as long as the item can fit in their mouth. Their form of self-defense utilizes their large, sharp teeth to bite and injure enemies.

Hamster Robopets have a starting price of 5 Karma, though the price may differ based on customizations.

Rabbit RoboPet

Rabbit RoboPet

Rabbit RoboPets are generally designed to look similar to an Eastern Cottontail rabbit. They have oval-shaped ears, short tails, and large teeth. They are often 1.5 feet tall and weigh three pounds. They have short limbs, round bodies, and a large nose.

Rabbit RoboPets can run up to 15mph and can jump up to five feet high. Their teeth are made of pure titanium, allowing them to chew their way through any common material found in levels. They can create burrows large enough to hide an average human in 15 minutes! Their form of self-defense utilizes their hind legs to kick or their large teeth to bite their opponent.

Rabbit Robopets have a starting price of 10 Karma, though the price may differ based on customizations.

RoboPet Care

RoboPets are by far the easiest pets to take care of! They don't need to eat or sleep, they just need to be recharged at night using their standard RoboPet charging pad, which has been designed to look like a typical pet bed. RoboPets can be easily trained to do tricks and/or be better fighters, which can be done by doing daily training sessions with them!

Each RoboPet also comes with our Robotics Insurance, meaning you will not have to pay for any naturally occurring repairs! These include rusting, wire deterioration, and movement jamming. However, any repairs that do not occur naturally are not covered by Robotics Insurance.

It is important to note that while RoboPets are immune to fire, water, and most Backrooms entities, they cannot handle high voltage, short-circuiting at 30 volts. Please keep your RoboPets far away from any sources of electricity that exceed this limit.

Order Now!

Order your RoboPet at the Backrooms Robotics website! We'll deliver your RoboPet right to your doorstep/droppoint!
