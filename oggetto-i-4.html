Object I-4 - "Talents in a Box"
rating: +13+–x


Info

Written by jyj_52

Translated by XRaggiX.

Huge thanks also to DrAkimoto and Yesserning for the valuable critque on the page!

Greenlit by ForestIsWatching

Close

Languages:Languages:
















A tin can of Object I-4, reciting "Science".

Object I-4 is a collective name used for a large range of foods more commonly referred to as Talents in a Box. They can be used as a food or, in certain departments of the M.E.G., as a way to train recruits.

Description:

Talents in a Box are a food source that can be found in the majority of Italian levels of the Backrooms. Each instance of Object I-4 is different from the others, both in shape and colour. However, they are all united by the fact that they are found inside small boxes or cans made either of aluminum or tinned iron sheet.

Each can is marked by a signature either printed or engraved on it. If the can does not present any signature1, it is unadvised to consume it. Colour and language of the signature can vary depending on the instance.

An instance of Object I-4a without any signature.

Properties:

The cans of Object I-4 do not present any anomalous properties, but the same cannot be stated for its internal contents.

The content inside the can will present itself as common packaged food. The one who consumes the food2 will aquire some knowledge, a talent, or skill connected to the signature found on the box. The skills gained from instances of Object I-4 are not always obvious or exactly connected to the signature.
