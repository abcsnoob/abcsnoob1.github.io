Info

The hub contains spoilers. Because of the complexity of the canon and the sheer amount of worldbuilding, I have to explain things in detail. You can read the "Main Storyline" Levels first or this hub first… The decision belongs to you my friend \_()_/

Author note: This canon can be considered as a "what if" scenario, for the most part, and isn't part of the original reverence lore. "Origins" is an original piece only showing some aspects of the actual reverence without diving into the lore intended by its original authors.

Close

Corruption is a parasite that starts from a single host. Leave it some time and it will multiply, again, and ever again until everyone is infected.

In the "Origins" canon, humanity is on the verge of collapse, threatened by its own kind.
The major groups of interests and human communities have fallen after the attacks of December 25, 2024. Starting from this date, a new era for humanity has begun.
An era of terror where both humans and entities can threaten your survival.
Where no one can be trusted.

There is however one last remaining resistance, one last hope for humanity to rebuild.





Created by: Charles Delameuh

Factions

Liberation

The infamous faction of the reverence, founded by the undercover reverence member Father Richard Simmons Delacroix, known as "Overseer S" when he was infiltrated within the M.E.G.

Despite sharing the same objective as the reverence, which is returning to the past. Liberation is rather a separate faction and identifies itself as an organized religious fanatical group. They are the ones behind the attacks of December 25, a global-scale terrorist attack that led to the fall of nearly all groups of interest and communities.

After their successful assault, they massively strengthened their power and assets, established an oppressive regime all over the Backrooms, and recruited many new "apostles" to prepare for the final assault that will determine humanity's fate in the Backrooms forever.

They do not kill, nor have they ever killed, any entity, even if their own lives are threatened. Because if one of their apostles were to kill an entity he or she would get punished on the spot. This can't be said for humans where they persecute them as much as they can.

Site 770 is Liberation's first established outpost that became their main center of command. Although cut off from the rest of the Backrooms, Liberation uses it as an advantage to keep its activities secret and secured. This includes their various projects, operations and classified data, all stored in their main database.

As it is impossible to communicate with the main headquarters from the Backrooms main lands, Liberation had to set up another command center: Site 4. Level 4 being a relatively safe office space was considered the most strategic location for this role. Site 4 is therefore the core of Liberation's activities within the Backrooms main lands, with "Control" as both Site Director and Chief of Security.

Site 11 is a large research center located in the District 7 of Level 11. They have all the equipment needed at their disposal to carry out all kinds of experiments, and for that reason, Site 11 is one of the most protected assets of Liberation.

Site 3 is a telecommunication center located in a large control room within Level 3. Site 3 is also one of Liberation's most protected assets, as they control all unsecured communications feeds and Wi-Fi flux on the Backrooms' main lands. This allows Liberation to eavesdrop and spread disinformation on almost every device connected to the Backrooms' network.

God, Entity 997-A, The Sacred One, Our dearest Creator, Saint Nature… All these terms refer to the mysterious entity supposedly controlling everything within the lands of Liberation. However, the entity has absolutely no power or control outside its realm, and therefore relies on Liberation to carry out its wishes within the Backrooms. It is theorized that the entity can create several manifestations of itself, each with a distinct consciousness but all linked to its host. Its very existence is uncertain as nobody witnessed its physical existence; some might say that it is just a hallucination or a phenomenon. The Patriarch is the only known human to have directly communicated with this "entity".

The Syndicate

More commonly referred to as "the Resistance", the Syndicate is a coalition founded by the Masked Maidens, including the Eyes of Argos, the U.E.C., the Backrooms Robotics, the Eternal Repository, the Ariane Circle and the remain of the M.E.G.

They are the last remaining opposing force to Liberation and the last hope for humanity.
However, due to the severity of the situation they can't afford to play it safe. Leading them to engage in morally questionable acts, but more often in spite of their will.

object 770-A

An object unique to Paradise 770.

Object 770-A, named "Heaven's Fruits", can satisfy your nutritional needs for eternity, as long as you eat 8 of those berries a day.

Additionally, a mix named "The Holy Grail Mixture" is made from this object. The mix can heal your worst injuries and provide you with temporary unlimited stamina.

object 770-B

An object unique to Paradise 770.

Object 770-B, named "The Divine Fruit", can grant you near immortality for a short amount of time. The rarity of this object makes it one of the most valued object of Liberation.




A cave filled with object 770-C

An object unique to Paradise 770.

Object 770-C, named "The Crystals of Chaos", can trigger an explosion equivalent to 1000 kg of TNT, generating a lethal blast of over 50 meters radius. This object has a pivotal role in Liberation's armament technology.


A chaos device

A manufactured object based on Object 770-C.
Produced at the Pandora Institute in Paradise 770.

Object 770-S, named "The Chaos Device", is a TOP SECRET object of Liberation and used specifically for their operation : Operation Liberation. Throughout the "sowing phase" of the operation, this remote miniaturized "atomic bomb" has been planted in every strategic points.

object 771-A

An object unique to Paradise 771.

Object 771-A, named "The Crystals of Calling", can attract and control nearby entities through an effect called "the Beacon effect". The "beacon effect" can't affect humans and native entities of the lands of liberation.




object 771-S

A manufactured object based on Object 771-A.
Produced at the factory in Paradise 771.

Object 771-S, named "The Harbinger Device", is another TOP SECRET object meant to be used specifically for "Operation Liberation". This object can be compared as a leash for entity. The device amplifies the existing properties of Object 771-A and features an additional function that can turn any entities hostile.

Object-77X blueprint

CODENAME : PROMETHEUS CANON

A TOP SECRET weapon prototype of Liberation meant to be used against the gods of the Pantheon and the Iron Fist.




object 227-A

This crystal is only found inside Entity 227-X.

Object 227-A, named "The Rift Crystal", generates invisible rifts able to teleport small objects to any levels of the paradises network (Paradise 770, 771, etc.), except Paradise 997 and 772 because of their properties. However, if some conditions are met, the power of the crystal can be increased significantly to be able to teleport bigger and heavier objects.

object 227-X

An Armoured Personnel Carrier (APC) made by the Backrooms Robotics.

Object 227-X, nicknamed "World Breachers", are based on Object 227-A: "The Rift Crystal", allowing them to transport large amount of peoples. Additionally, the vehicle is equipped of a camouflage system and almost indestructible armor.

According to Andrew Callum, the Backrooms Robotics CEO, the production of these vehicle is not so difficult as it seems, both logistically and material wise.
