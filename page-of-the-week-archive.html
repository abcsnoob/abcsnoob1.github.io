What is Page of the Week?

Page of the Week(or POTW) is a weekly randomized article that was posted anytime in the history of the site. POTW aims to provide additional visibility to all things that the site has to offer, new or old! We hope that with inducting random pages every week, we can encourage folks to read something they usually wouldn’t and to help recycle older pages that aren’t quite up-to-date, or to help the newer ones get more widespread recognition.
