An instance of Phenomenon 38 occurring in the open areas of Level 29.



DESCRIPTION


Phenomenon 38 is a recently recorded interesting and beneficial phenomenon that occurs randomly across all levels in the Backrooms. Unlike most phenomena, Phenomenon 38 does not cause any harm or adverse effects.

When Phenomenon 38 happens, an open door will appear far away in the wanderer's sight. Behind the door, a scene looks very different from the area around it. This scene usually shows a bright, open place outside with natural sunlight. And it often has things like clear blue skies. These things make people who see it feel peaceful and hopeful.

The scene behind the door is clear, but people are not sure about the door itself now. No wanderer has ever said they went through the door, so researchers and experienced wanderers cannot draw a simple conclusion about it. It is unclear whether the door is a existent physical object or merely an illusion triggered by Phenomenon 38.

Once a wanderer sees this door, the recommended course of action is to move in its direction as soon as possible. Observational data and analysis have shown that after traveling a certain distance toward the door, the chances of locating an exit / a no-clippable spot from the current level increase dramatically. This is considered a part of Phenomenon 38, and also an important evidence for this phenomenon to guide and assist wanderers seeking escape.

After a large-scale investigation by the M.E.G., an interesting fact about Phenomenon 38 was discovered: the frequency of Phenomenon 38 tends to increase under specific psychological conditions experienced by wanderers. In particular, when individuals find themselves wanting to leave their current level for a long time or when they have spent an extended period wandering without reaching an exit, Phenomenon 38 is more likely to occur.

Phenomenon 38 has helped dozens of struggling wanderers escape from dangerous levels ever since its discovery on December 3rd, 2024.

This effect is not caused by the medication taken, but is based on the patient's psychological hope for recovery.

Maybe you still don't understand, but it doesn't matter; this document will make everything clear to you. Oh, and it also includes the reason why I made up this page. I apologize for my disapproved behavior, but you will be able to understand that Phenomenon 38 is so amazing and valuable.
