Ahoy there reader! I'm Positive123 and pogsitive123 or Pogs in discord. My favorite color is green(u can tell from the theme used). I enjoy politics in writing and stuff like collaborations between unlikely parties, which is why most of my articles here feature two or more groups.

My first level and article! This one is a bit mid, with the information feeling redundant at times. Will most likely rewrite this at some point in the future. Initially I planned on having ForestIsWatching to crit it as well, but my patience wore thin after a month of waiting(as I was second in his queue), and requested that he remove me from the queue. In hindsight, I should probably have waited out for the crit. Posting this article was also a bit of a hassle as I thought that offsets would load for submission pages (thank you very much Abdallah Amr for resolving this) which caused it to be announced 5 hours after it was posted.

My first object! I got into writing this because I wanted to revive the Eternal Repository and expand on the Church of the Veiled at the same time. Fun fact: I originally planned on giving up on this concept at some point as there was a major lore contradiction inside the article which drove the entire plot. However, Schulzenreich took an interest in it which motivated me sufficiently to solve that contradiction, so shoutouts to him! Also got very lucky that r a t i f was just coming back to give me the greenlight. Thanks!
