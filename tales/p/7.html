Tales

Welcome to the Backrooms Tale Hub

Tales include the many articles written on our site that do not fit into the other article types. They range greatly in both content, format, and themes. Many of these pages focus on exploring the lore behind various levels, groups, and other aspects of the Backrooms. There is no right way to write a tale and no restrictions regarding format expectations—your imagination is the only limit!

Below you will find a list of our current Tales organized by Title, Genre, and Author for ease of access. We recommend giving them a read, as there really is something for everyone to enjoy!

System Administrator Note: This archive is designed to be a comprehensive list of all Backrooms tales written on this wiki. Please use the navigation tools below to properly locate the tale of your choice. If you discover that a tale is absent, please contact either the tale author or staff for assistance.
