[‚ô´] Level 44-FR (translated by scutoid studios!) (+12)

‚ñ∂ Commentary
‚ùö‚ùö Close

Massive thanks to scutoid studios for taking the time to translate my very first page ever! It was initially posted in early 2022.

You can really feel the influence of 2021 Tech Support in this one. I was quite new to the inner workings of the community, meaning I didn't dare to stray too far away from the commonly accepted format. Yet, I did already want to bring my own twist to it, while also trying to create a setting that could resonate with people from Belgium and Northern France. This is where "Strip-Tease" comes in.

Okay, not a literal Strip-Tease, it's a documentary series known for its raw portrayal of people from various paths of life, some normal and others… much less so. There's very little if any input from the directors, leaving the stage entirely to those who are the subject of those documentaries, for better or worse. This hugely inspired me in the making of 44-FR, allowing the main character (the unnamed woman) speak for herself. Her retelling is most probably fragmented, but it is honest.

In terms of themes, misplaced and false nostalgia is out there. The village is a lost memory of a life snatched away by the horrors of war, yet a group dances on its grave, falsely celebrating what is seen as land perfect to reestablish a lost glorious past. The nationalistic undertones of the directorate are very much intentional.
