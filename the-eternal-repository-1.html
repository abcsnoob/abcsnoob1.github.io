"Forever Loyal. Forever Eternal"

| THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |
Finding the Forgotten, Mending the Found.

| What is the Eternal Repository? |

We are a collective of people who share the value of anonymity. No one in our organization know who the one beside them is, other than a codename one chooses upon entering the group. We are also known as information barterers, as that is how we trade with people. If you want information from us, you will have to give our proxies something in return. No exceptions, no secret deals.

| Our Mission |

We wish for anonymity to protect those who are weak when they are visible. Our goal is to cloak those who wish to stay behind the veil of mystery, help those who need information, and prosper in this hellscape of endless levels. No realm is free of its deals, and we are full of them.

| Members |

The Master Administrator
Close information

| MEMBERS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

V. Valentin - Master Administrator

The Master Administrator of the Eternal Repository, V. Valentin established this group in 2016 with the goals of anonymity, informational bartering, and prosperity. He is almost never seen unless large meetings are taking place, and when so he is always accompanied by two Sentinels of Navy. Other than this little he allows to provide, Valentin is completely anonymous to the rest of the Repository.

|"From the shadows can bring to the light."|

The Sentinels of Navy
Close information

| MEMBERS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

The Sentinels of Navy - Eternal Guardians

The Sentinels of Navy are the guard force of the Eternal Repository, and are given most of the armor and the best weaponry we can trade for. These soldiers protect whoever possible, mainly accompanying Hands of Trade. Unlike the rest of our convent, they will openly speak to other wanderers not associated with our organization.

|"Forever protect, forevermore."|

The Cloak Crafters
Close information

| MEMBERS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

Cloak Crafters - The Shrouded Forge

Cloak Crafters are the ones who create our uniforms, and are also one of the only ones that will ever see the face of a member who is in the Repository. They will create cloaks, vests, coats, armor, and most importantly our masks. These members are highly respected among our ranks for what they do for us.

|"We are what you mend, forged in eternal darkness."|

Hands of the Trade
Close information

| MEMBERS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

Hands of the Trade - The Whispers

Hands of the Trade, or more known as H.T's, are the ones you will most likely see before joining our group. They are in the same boat with the Cloak Crafters, as they are the others who will see the face of whoever joins. These people are the main workforce of our cause, being the ones who initiate trade between others for information. They are silent and only talk to the other party when needed. If you come to a Hand of Trade, you should already know what to bring. We're not going to say it for you.

|"Words unspoken, secrets foretold."|

The Infiltrators of Wrath
Close information

| MEMBERS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

The Infiltrators of Wrath - The Burning Flame

The Infiltrators of Wrath are a new branch that we are currently testing with. Currently it's going very well and shows very promising results. We like to keep tabs on people to predict any possible dangers, and we already have informants in the Major Explorer Group, the Unbound Explorer's Coalition, and The Eyes of Argos. We aren't trying to attack you, friends. We only wish to protect ourselves.

|"The flame burns eternal within us."|

The Data Diggers
Close information

| MEMBERS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

The Data Diggers - The Withholders

The Data Diggers are the brave souls who go to collect information for us, and are the main backbone of our workforce. Without them we would not be existing today. These men and women venture alone into levels that have not been documented by other organizations, and if discovered wear normal clothing. Some groups are desperate for information, and we don't fancy our informants to be taken into custody. If joining our ranks, this is where you will start.

|"Loyalty to bravery, forever eternal to The Repository."|

| Teams / Divisions |

We have only a few divisions within our ranks, but we make sure they are relentlessly trained until their performance is perfect. We want to make sure that your future trades are pleasant.

Division Alpha: The Guards of Erebus
Close information

| DIVISIONS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

The Guards of Erebus - Keepers of Shadow

The Guards of Erebus are the highest order of our protective forces. They are the most disciplined, the ones with the highest honor, and the most loyal to the Repository's cause. Although they are these great things, the Guards of Erebus refuse better gear and force themselves to deal with what they have. They can be compared to the Old Guard of the United States Army.

Other than this, they wish to remain completely anonymous.

|"We protect what those are afraid to see."|

Division Beta: The Osmium Shield
Close information

| DIVISIONS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

The Osmium Shield - Those on the Front Lines

The Osmium Shield is our defensive force, and the ones who will always be where the battle is. While we wish to remain neutral with all parties, there are times where war is unavoidable. For that uncertain future, we have established our protectors.

|"The war is imminent. We are your sword and shield."|

Division Gamma: The Sparks of Kuraz
Close information

| DIVISIONS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

The Sparks of Kuraz - Forming the Foundation

The Sparks of Kuraz, or simply the Sparks, are members who wish to develop bases and outposts for the Repository. They are generally staffed at every possible location, providing their skills of maintenance, repair, and internal upkeep to make sure we are at our best. They are also in charge of logistics and housing for our members, taking care of beds, food supplies, water reserves, and more in that field.

|"Every great organization requires a backbone. Here we are."|

Division Epsilon: The Scourges
Close information

| DIVISIONS OF THE ETERNAL REPOSITORY |

The Scourges - Material Collection

The Scourges are a smaller division of the Repository, being our main material gathering source. These units scrounge through every level, finding materials that may be useful, and bringing them back to Repository Databases. The collected materials, which usually consist of copper, iron, sheet metal, and rarely gold. Materials are pushed up to Cloak Crafters, who mend everything into what we use today.

|"Gathering the scraps for the products of the future."|

More divisions will be added in the future as our member count grows.

How to write for the Eternal Repository

Writing for us is a bit different than other groups in this dimension of hell.

We don't have too strict of a template, but what we do require is that you use our theme, which can be found here.

Custom SD Class
We finally have a custom SD class, made by CutTheBirch. We can't thank you enough, Cloak Crafter.

Here is how our SD class looks:

No Dangers | No GOI Presence | No Environmental Hazards

When creating your article, you may want to have audio files inside the article. We have a certain template we follow, which is outlined below.

Audio File Template
ERAF:####/(Letter)
For numbers: Random numbers, they don't matter.
For letters:
A - Administrative file: For the Master Administrator only
B - Bravo file: For Sentinels. Mainly combative orders.
C - Charlie file: For Cloak Crafters, Gamma Division, and Epsilon Division. Usually material orders and inventory
D - Delta file: For Data Diggers. Exploration focuses, reports, and updates.
E - Echo file: For all personnel.

Trading is very important to our group. We only release very specific things under our own name to the public. When making an article, make sure you trade it to another group. Currently, we have relations with the M.E.G., U.E.C., Masked Maidens, and the Lost. More will come in the future, such as the Eyes of Argos which we are discussing with now.

Go to http://navyeod-24.wikidot.com/ and look under "Files" to find the group you want to release your article to. Insert the code as follows:

Article Signatures
[[= image (insert link here)]]

Remove the space between "= image" to make the code work.

Make sure you add "the-eternal-repository" tag at the bottom of your page after posting.
