The Eyes Of Argos
rating: +78+–x

The Eyes of Argos archives are CLASSIFIED, any attempt to enter without the required permissions will be prosecuted.



  • About us
  • Patrols
  • GoIs

"Every place needs a law to follow, something to hold on, a guide to help distinguish right from wrong. We take it upon ourselves to promulgate that guidance, to keep the inhabitants of this labyrinth in their right mind and to punish those who do not."

- Highmarshal Arcadia, Leader of the Sons of Guilt

Welcome, watcher.

If you are reading this you have been accepted as a member of the Eyes of Argos. Whether it is because justice is among your highest priorities or because you wish to atone for your sins, you are member of the Eyes of Argos now, and as such it is necessary that you have a basic understanding of the inner workings of our organization.

History

From time immemorial, empires have risen and fallen in their quest to conquer and reign supreme over the land now known as "The Backrooms". Although some have tried to do so fairly, most end up succumbing tragically to corruption through their insatiable desire for power. To stop this our group was created many centuries ago by the hand of our patron Argos. It is the duty of our group to impose the rule by which those who must perish or those who have the option to redeem themselves are measured.

Vision

The Eyes of Argos is an organization with one ideal, embracing justice. Each of the beings that enter our organization possesses a different set of beliefs, customs and even inner workings, which drastically affects their view of justice. The Eyes of Argos allows and encourages its members to find their own way of exercising justice and to exercise it towards others.

Mission

In recent times we have begun to act not only against large factions, but in support of people wandering alone or in small groups. To achieve this goal we recruit individuals like you from all different times and places.

Being a member of the Eyes of Argos is a great responsibility. You are one of the few lines of defense for justice in this realm of lawlessness. Even so, joining us is not without its privileges. These include unlimited access to our facilities, supplies and databanks, which may assist you in your pursuit of justice.

We give you all these tools for one purpose, that you become one of us, whether you are a watcher, a philosopher, a researcher or an enforcer of Justice by any other means.

Open your eyes wide, Watcher.

INQUISITORS OF TRUTH

If you enjoy the hunt and the chase, if you are excited about having a duel against your enemy, if you find our task honorable… then the Inquisitors of Truth are what you seek. As the personal guard of our leader Argos, we are in charge of hunting down the most dangerous and elusive criminals in this realm.

"Have a good hunt, watcher!"

JUSTICE SEEKERS

Stability. Fairness. Balance. These are the virtues that a Justice Seeker must display if they wish to join us. Despite what others claim, we do not view our work as something "exciting", but as a duty we must fulfill. We are committed to track, catch and prosecute criminals in the fairest possible way.

"Let the court decide your fate…"

LAW KEEPERS

Justice is a much more abstract concept than you might imagine. Have you ever wondered what justice really means?, Many choose to go about their duties without making such considerations. We do not. In our libraries across the backrooms we store texts from all kinds of cultures and write our own, with the goal of discovering a perfect law that finally achieves True Justice.

"Throughout the texts, we shall find justice"

SONS OF GUILT

No one is exempt from sin. Even we, the Eyes of Argos, have made mistakes. Many criminals deserve the ultimate punishment, but there are others who may be able to redeem themselves. As Sons of Guilt we confess that we have committed deeds of defilement just like sinners and that, like us, they have the oportunity to redeem themselves.

"Our guilt for those who deserve it"

SIN HUNTERS

There are those for whom death is too merciful a punishment. People who reached the limits of immorality and then broke them. We, the Sin Hunters, are responsible for capturing these sons of perdition and imprisoning them within our dungeons. There, they will receive the reward they deserve. We hope we never need to see you.

"An eye for an eye"

KNIGHTS OF ARGOS

Disgraced Patrol

The Knights of Argos were a group tasked with protecting the namesake of Argos and pursuing any who decried him. They frequently rode to and from various different settlements, spreading Argos's law to pockets of civilization that had not yet learned it. They eventually left The Eyes of Argos, and took new leadership under Alasdair Morgan. They are no longer recognized as an official patrol.

"The heretics ride together."

SENTINELS OF THE WATCH

We, the Sentinels of the Watch, are the guardsmen of the Eyes of Argos. As a Patrol faithful and well-trained, we have been entrusted to preserve the peace and security of His Justice's strongholds, to protect them from threats within and without. Though we were once disgraced and sentenced to partial exile, our valiant defence of Justice in the crisis of the Enatiodh Heresy restored us to our rightful place. If your heart seeks to shield the vulnerable and weak, to defend those who cannot defend themselves, to patiently watch the priceless treasure, to faithfully guard the invaluable trust — join us, and uphold Justice.

"Be still, ye restless. Watch and wait. Open your eyes; lift up your head."
