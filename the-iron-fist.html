The Iron Fist Manifesto

Once, long ago, we believed in the gods of these realms. We accepted them as our own and worshipped their divinity for millennia. But then, the gods betrayed us, and we came to realize we were insignificant in their eyes. The gods thought us to be nothing more than pawns, playthings for their amusement. With the illusion broken, we sought to prove them wrong. We took matters into our own hands, and did what we could to bring them down from power. We usurped the gods, and drove them into the shadows.

  • Our Mission
  • The Five Fingers
  • Relations
  • The Vanquished

Our Mission

We are the Iron Fist, and it is our sworn duty to liberate humanity from the oppression of gods, so that we may claim these realms as our own. A place as godless as The Backrooms should not be home to any divine being. Any being presenting itself as a so-called “God” is not to be trusted nor followed, and will be swiftly eradicated. Those who lay claims of divinity are false idols, not to be placed on pedestals, but to be torn down.

“Avatars”, “Numens”, “Emblems”, “Archetypes” — whatever they are known as, they must be dethroned from their godlike position, stripped of their power, and put to the sword. None of these false idols are to be trusted, as all have the potential to abuse their power, and lack remorse for humanity. For centuries we have remained hidden after our uprising, for we had successfully dismantled the powers that were. But now is the time for us to re-emerge, for the gods threaten us once again with the possibility of their reunion.

The Five Fingers

Like a true fist, we need only five fingers to deal a devastating blow. Each sect of our righteous order specializes in their own craft, which collectively contribute to our one true goal: eliminating the gods.

Mars

Premier: Amadeus Ludwig Steele

Our fiercest warriors. They are the soldiers of this order, willing to lay down their lives in the war against false divinity. Once, they had the weakness of honor against monsters. Now, they are enlightened to the truth of warfare: kill or be killed.

Jupiter

Premier: Abdias Podhortzer

Those who accumulate knowledge. Once, their purpose was to reform beings to relinquish false godhood. Now, they gather intelligence on our targets, whether by research or by espionage. They learn all that they can in order to locate our targets, and pinpoint their plans' weaknesses so that we may exploit them.

Saturn

Premier: Nathaniel Gunnr

The spearhead of the order, residing as its governing body. It consists solely of the premiers of each finger, who collectively advise and coordinate the subordinate sects.

Sol

Premier: Callum Andrews

The creators of our tools and weapons. Once, they were craftsmen and smiths, melting iron to forge each god-killing weapon as an elegant yet deadly work of art. Now, they are skilled engineers of advanced technology, further proving the might of man over god with their innovations.

Mercury

Premier: Joan Arteaga

Assassins who dare venture into the domain of the enemy, infiltrating it to silently strike a killing blow. They are deployed when direct confrontation is ill-advised. Once, their goal was to eliminate all gods identified as threats, who mistreated humanity. Now, their goal is eliminate all gods, for every god is a threat.

Relations

Allies

The U.E.C.

They fight for a noble cause, ridding these realms of blight and ensuring safety for humans.

Neutral

The M.E.G.

They refuse to aid our cause, though their goals are admirable and their interests remain in man.

Enemies

The Cygnus order

Accursed distributors of the wicked librarian's propaganda.

The Eyes of Argos

Hypocrites who persecute in the name of a blind judge.

The Followers of Jerry

Wayward souls who have forsaken their individuality for an avian parasite.

Order Eleison

Vicious warmongers who sought nothing but the spread of bloodshed. Though they may be gone, their blasphemy must not be forgotten.

The Reverence

Barbarians who wish to regress to the time of the gods' tyrannical regime.

The Visionaries of Berry

For so-called "visionaries", their sight is as lost as their cat.

Zealots of the Chair

Fools prostrating themselves before a mere mundane object.

The Vanquished

Recorded herein are the names of all so-called gods that we have slain over the course of our history. May their names rot with their corpses, for they shall never again be uttered. The means by which some have been felled are not disclosed, and the identities of executioners remain anonymous, to prevent such devastating destruction from falling into the wrong hands

  • Kayan — Struck by the arrow of Katherine.
  • Gatekeeper — Poisoned by the chalice of Alexander.
  • The Allseer — Entombed beneath rubble.

  • The Blue Knight — Beheaded by the blade of Amadeus.
  • Telatrix

  • CORAN — Impaled on the pike of Joan.
