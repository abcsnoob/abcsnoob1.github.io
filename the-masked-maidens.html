Welcome to the Masked Maidens Database.
rating: +83+–x

The information within the M.M. Database is restricted from public view and may only be accessed by Volto Mask Wearers and above. Please input your Mask info to proceed.

To Mask is to Reveal.

  • The Purpose
  • Mask Sectors
  • Objectives

Corruption. Weeds breaking and cracking through what was meant to be a safeplace. Corruption hides behind clean, smiling masks. To be reading this, dear Wearer, reveals to us that you have either been a victim of that corruption or you desire to shut it down yourself. And that is what we plan to do. Like the weeds, we can and will plant ourselves wherever we require - and with the curse that is our masks, our newfound powers, and visions - we will bring down corruption for good. Maybe within others, too.

Should you require to contact your Sector Leader or the Madam herself, please refer to the next tab. May your truth be unmasked.

Sector 1: O.M.

Sub-Sector Colombina - Leader: Madam Evangeline Holmes. Leader of the most powerful Sub-Sector and the entire group itself, she is to be treated with the utmost respect. She oversees the management of all Sub-Sectors as well as holding the final decision on every and all matters. Her sector specializes in the supervision of Levels all Maidens have been in - the only exception being Level 906). Advice and questions about the state of the Backrooms outside of our stations and beyond are to go to her.

Sub-Sector Bauta - Leader: Rafael Lawson. He and his Sub-Sector specialize in interrogation. Also considered the brute force of the Sectors, they are the first line of defense and usually the ones hunting down the subjects they are interrogating. Most questions on the information of certain subjects are to go directly to him.

Sub-Sector Pantalone - Leader: Amanda Wright. Her sector specializes in the organizing of information from Sub-Sectors Bauta, Volto, and Kitsune as well as the creation of theories in regards to Wall Masks themselves. Because of the effects Pantalone Masks have on them, in-person communication is highly avoided unless necessary.

Sector 2: B.M.

Sub-Sector Volto - Leader: Jacqueline Guzman. Sub-Sector Volto specializes in deterring wanderers and unwanted groups from obtaining information about the group. Their primary way of doing this is by going out to areas where wanderers are plentiful and spreading rumors about the group (as well as current events) to confuse others and keep them from learning the truth. At the same time, they obtain information from wanderers as well - however, this is more so a rare occurrence than most.

Sub-Sector Kitsune - Leader: Kokona Sagobe. Her Sub-Sector also works on gathering information through a more specific process. Using the information on wanderers gathered by Maidens in Sub-Sector Pantalone, they disguise themselves as a loved one of a wanderer and gain information from there. On more desperate notes, they will also use their disguises to take things from said wanderers (although… we try not to have this happen).

Sub-Sector Arlecchino - Leader: Landon Chester. Their Sub-Sector act as the distractions unit and so tends to accompany other Sub-Sectors on missions to lure unnecessary wanderers from driving anyone away from the task at hand. The current leader, Landon Chester, is currently missing from all Maiden Stations. As a result, the Sub-Sector is currently being handled by their sister, Phoebe Chester.

Sector 2: N.M.

Sub-Sector Buskin - Leader: Farai Sobel. Farai's Sub-Sector is considered the second line of defense as well as the hunters of wanderers who simply know too much. They spend much of their time outside of Maiden Stations, scoping out for any person unmasked with the knowledge they should not have without the Leaders' permission. As ironic as it is, they are the group usually paired with a Pantalone mask-wearer.

Sub-Sector Oni - although this is indeed a separate sector for Oni Mask wearers, only two people have worn the mask - Ethan Nelson and Adrian Ortega. This is due to the fact that most of the Oni Mask wearers were subjected to the experiment and, as a result, were nearly wiped out during a deadly portion of it. Adrian has taken the responsibility of watching over the Sock Sector with his sister, Katrina Ortega. Ethan Nelson, also known by outside parties as the Gardener, considers himself to be the judge and executioner of ill-intentioned wanderers. Because of his story, power, and creation of the Clumps, it is important to respect this man and his wishes. No one but Madam Evangeline, Sub-Sector Pantalone Leader, Amanda Wright, and Sub-Sector Arlecchino Acting Leader, Phoebe Chester, is to engage in private conversation with him.

Sub-Sector Sock - Leaders: Bauta Mask Wearer Katrina Ortega and Oni Mask Wearer Adrian Ortega. Because of their inability to stay aware enough due to their violence and delirium, twins Katrina and Adrian Ortega have taken the responsibility of watching over Sub-Sector Sock. They are considered the final line of offense - however, because of a lack of need for such defense, few are only brought on missions for important occasions while most wearers are going through testing phases in the hopes of getting them to be aware of their actions

Current Objective: Ex-rescue trainee Adeline McCaffery has taken the mask. Currently, we have a team down in Station 3 that retrieved her and took her to Madame Blanche's library. due to a surprise attack made by the weeds. Madam Evangeline will be making a personal visit there, to brief Madame Blanche and the others.

Primary Objective: Find the source of corruption within the M.E.G.'s many groups. Leading suspect group: Division Trial Testers. Only then will we receive justice for the pain they've caused.

Secondary Objective: Find a way to reduce the long-term effects of Excess Effect Levels. We need to stay as aware as possible. To feel air upon our face again depends on us staying as mentally healthy as possible… as impossible as it may be.
