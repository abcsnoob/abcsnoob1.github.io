Tom Von Haderach
rating: +52+–x
ID: Profile : Tom Von Haderach

[NAME]: Tom Von Haderach

Alias(es): None
Last Known Location(s): Level 906, Level 797
Known Affiliations: Blanche Von Haderach

Description:

A younger-looking Tom Von Haderach, as depicted in the Backrooms Trading Card Game.

Tom Von Haderach is a young male, age 18, easily recognizable by his shoulder-length, wavy blonde hair, and the black leather jacket he's usually seen wearing. Information on Tom is scarce. It is known that prior to the writing of this entry, Tom was often spotted roaming numerous levels with unknown purposes, aiding wanderers along the way. The M.E.G. encourages any and all wanderers who have encountered Tom to provide reports, as to consolidate the information present on this page.

Shortly after the discovery of Level 906, Tom broke into Base Alpha via unknown means with the intention of contacting the M.E.G. He then proceeded to reveal his allegiance to Blanche, and claimed that he had been traversing the Backrooms, "running errands" for her. These errands range from collecting items (most notably books or instances of Object 25) to delivering messages to various entities and groups of interest.

The last reported sighting of Tom Von Haderach, which occurred approximately a month ago, took place in the newly discovered Level 797, where he was seen roaming around carrying a flag of unknown design. It is unknown if Tom is related to the anomalies affecting that level's page. The current whereabouts of Tom are unknown.

M.E.G. Base Alpha Incident Report #143

The following is a transcript of an audio file recorded during Tom Von Haderach's intrusion into one of Base Alpha's lounge rooms. Besides Tom, all four members of Team New Horizons were present. Tom reportedly entered the room casually through the front door.

Tom: So uh… hey?

Dawn: Who the hell are you, and how'd you get here?!

Tom: Woah, woah, chill! Name's Tom Von Haderach. Does that ring a bell?

Dawn: Von Haderach… like Blanche? You two related?

Tom: Eh… kind of? I got taken under her wing when I got here a while back.

Faith: Told you she had motherly vibes!

Axel: *laughter*

Killian: Explains how you got into Base Alpha. She probably got you here.

Tom: Nope, I got here my way, but that isn't relevant. You guys wrote the database entries for… ah fuck it, I forgot the numbers.

Axel: Wait, did you just say "fuck"?

Tom: Yeah, what about it?

Axel: She lets you do that!?

Tom: Hell no, but I can keep my language in check when I'm there. By your reaction… I'm guessing you couldn't do the same.

Axel: Shit, kid, I got hit with that IRL mute button!

Tom: *laughter*

Dawn: I'm assuming you're talking about the Level 906 and Entity 140 pages?

Tom: Yeah, I uh… got told by Blanche to introduce myself. Since you guys now know about the Cygnus Archive and all… so here goes! Officially, I’m her butler. Not that she needs one, you probably already know she’s more than capable of taking care of the Archive herself. The one thing she can’t do, however, is leave. That’s where I come in.

Faith: So you're like an outside agent?

Tom: Heh, that’s a fancy way of putting it. I’m a messenger and an errand boy. I've been picking up some of her books and a bunch of Babel Balm for her. You need to get to Blanche, I’m your best bet.

Dawn: Strange that she didn't mention you at all while we were there… so where can we find you?

Tom: Take the path less traveled. Now, if you’ll excuse me, it’s almost tea time, and I would hate to be late.

Axel: What the fuck does that mean?

Tom: Means I gotta go right now, or I’m getting lectured! Seriously, though, you guys haven't found the place yet… I think I have just enough time to give you a little map.

Dawn: A… map? To where?

Tom: The path less traveled. A massive forest path said to connect all levels… or at least that's what the books say. Haven't gotten around to finishing the map just yet…

Axel: Think it's that forest level the rumors won't shut up about?

Tom: Not sure, but I've gotta say, if you do end up going there, bring a small team and don't tread on the plants. There's a friend of mine there who really cares about the flora. Here's your instructions. Catch y'all later!

Dawn: Wait, where do you think you're going?

Tom: I'm… leaving? I know my way out of here.

Dawn: Nearest exit's the other way…

Tom: Ah… thanks. Oh, Blanche gifted you a pendant, huh? That's neat. I'll be sure to drop by sometime soon. For now though, I've gotta run… see ya!

End of log.

During this encounter, Tom Von Haderach provided team New Horizons with a set of instructions that led to the discovery of Level 797, and would become the only known way of entering the level. Additionally, M.E.G. operative Killian Teles managed to place a small microphone on Tom's jacket.

The following section contains transcripts of some of the audio captured with said device.

(A ringtone is heard. Tom picks up. The other side of the conversation, presumably Blanche, could not be recorded.)

Tom: Hello?

Tom: Yeah, I think. It went well, they seem like nice people.

Tom: I shouldn't take long to get home, just have to deliver one last letter.

Tom: Yes, it's Eytelis'. I'm almost there.

Tom: Oh, Gani told me to thank you for the tea leaves, and wants you to pet Berry for them.

Tom: Okay, I'll be there quickly, don't want to miss out on tea time, especially not before lessons…

Tom: Very well.

Tom: (singing) Under the sacred briars, never stop, just go, I don't care how many more scars I gain! Bearing the pale fires, I'm going as far as I can- (silence)

The noises of running can be heard for a few seconds, then abruptly stop as Tom seems to bump into something.

???: Ow! Who the hell- Ah. Of course. Tom Von Haderach. Haven't seen you in quite some time, young man! I- uh, didn't see you coming.

Tom: You, the guy who sees both past and future, didn't see me coming? I don't believe you, Uncle Janus…

Janus: You got me! Oh well, what brings you here? I'm sorry for not visiting as often as I'd like, I've had my hands full lately…

Tom: Eh, don't sweat it. I'm just on my way back home. The M.E.G. found out about the Cygnus Archive, so I had to introduce myself to them. It went well, I think

Janus: Oh, I see, I see. Before I forget, I found one of Blanche's books. It's uh… right here. I suppose she'd want it back…

Tom: Oh hell yeah, I've been looking for this one for months now! Thanks, uncle J!

Janus: I'm glad I could help. Tell me, have you been doing well?

Tom: Yeah, it's been a fun week overall. I was hoping to pay Kaiser a visit, but I gotta get back home… it's almost tea time, and I'm gonna have a lesson or two after that. I'm hoping Argos doesn't go too hard on me.

Janus: I see, I see. Don't you worry, you're a tough one! Send Blanche my regards, will you? Also, before you leave… there's a little something on your jacket, young man.

Tom: A microphone… clever move, M.E.G.

The microphone's transmissions stopped at this moment. It is assumed that the equipment was either thrown away or destroyed.

Further investigation is required to confirm Tom's ties with other entities and people of interest in the Backrooms. As such, and due to his position as Blanche's outside agent, keeping an eye on Tom Von Haderach is one of the M.E.G.'s priorities.

The M.E.G. again requests for any wanderers who encounter him to report any relevant information. A collection of interview logs pertaining to people who have claimed to encounter Tom Von Haderach can be found below. These interviews were conducted by Faith Silva of Team New Horizons.

Interviewee: Millia Volkov
Age: 29

Faith: So, Millia, when and where did you run into Tom?

Millia: It was about a month ago, in… uhhh… Level 153? I think that's the one with the bookstore. I explored it for a little bit, and then he walked into the bookstore. I think he was looking for something, or someone, I don't know. He noticed me, walked up to me, and told me that the place was dangerous, and that I had to leave…

Faith: I see… what happened next?

Millia: I just sort of stuck with him. He guided me through the level while we looked through the bookshelves, told me to look for a signature in a book…

Faith: Blanche's signature, probably. What else happened?

Millia: We ran into some Deathmoths, never a good experience…

Faith: You can say that again…

Millia: He didn't kill the deathmoths, though. He used some… weird light trick to drive them away. I don't really understand what he did, he didn't even seem to use a flashlight, but it worked alright.

Faith: Woah, that's a new one. Can you describe it in more detail?

Millia: I don't think I can, he told me to look away…

Faith: That's okay. What happened next?

Millia: I found a book with the signature, he told me to drag my finger across it, and it took me to this really nice library level…

Faith: Level 906. We just came back from an expedition to it, made it a page and everything.

Millia: Oh. I see. Were you invited for tea, too?

Faith: Yup! Blanche does that to every guest, apparently.

Millia: It was a nice place. I was there for a week before returning home in 11. I didn't see this Tom guy in the time I was with Blanche, though.

Faith: Do you have anything to add?

Millia: No, that is all.

Faith: Okay. You're free to leave. Thanks for your help!

Interviewee: Jonathan Valentine
Age: 26

Faith: So, Jonathan, tell me about this Tom guy.

Jonathan: So, I was taking a day off, right? Went to Level 817 to grab some stuff, and then I ran into the guy, looking through some scrap.

Faith: What did he look like?

Jonathan: Blonde, shoulder length hair, grey-ish eyes, and a leather jacket with this winged emblem on the back, never seen it before.

Faith: Do you know what he was looking for?

Jonathan: Nope. Don't think he knew either. Not until he found something, that is.

Faith: Can you tell me what that something was?

Jonathan: An old, roughed up arcade stick. The guy went running to Kaiser's house as soon as he found it, mentioned someone called Penelope, too. I don't know what happened next, left around that time.

Faith: Alright. Anything else?

Jonathan: Not really, but… Are you free tomorrow?

Faith: What?

Jonathan: Going out to explore Level 151 later today. Say, what if we took a trip to the Snackrooms after that?

Faith: (laughter) Sure. I'm free. Don't get the wrong idea, Valentine, this ain't a date. I'm still with Dawn.

Jonathan: Heh, guess it's my lucky day!

Faith: I got no more questions. You're free to leave. Hoping to see you later.

Interviewee: Sasha Bettencourt
Age: 20

Faith: So, Sasha, where'd you find Tom?

Sasha: Level 11.

Faith: What were you doing at the time?

Sasha: Not important.

Faith: Alright, so… what happened?

Sasha: I was just… trying to get my hands on some cigs, and this blonde guy just popped in, started asking questions, trying to get me to quit it… really annoying.

Faith: What was he wearing?

Sasha: Jeans… boots… a leather jacket with this weird patch on the back, and a plain white t-shirt. Had a mask in one hand and a bucket of ice cream on the other.

Faith: Alright, that checks out, for the most part… the mask and the ice cream are a bit odd. Did anything else happen?

Sasha: Told him to buzz off… a few times. He was… annoying, to say the least. Said I was lucky 'cuz he had to leave. Otherwise he would've bugged me even more.

Faith: Oh. Anything else…?

Sasha: Nope. Can I leave now?

Faith: (sigh) Yes. You're free to go.

Interviewee: Josef Gardiner
Age: 31

Faith: Okay, Josef. Where were you when you ran into Tom?

Josef: It was in.. Level 170. Trying to get from there to 10 and then 11.

Faith: Okay, can you describe what happened?

Josef: There's only one way to get out of 170, you have to run for it into the forest.

Faith: Yeah, I know.

Josef: It's quite dangerous really, you se-

Faith: I've been there, Josef, I know the drill.

Josef: Oh, uh, yeah. Place's infested with Reviooks. About halfway through the forest, I ran into this blonde kid. Leather jacket, matches the description you guys have.

Faith: Alright, what happened?

Josef: Didn't really talk much to him. He told me to stick close to him, that he'd get us outta there. Didn't really think much of him, but he ended up fending off a few of those Reviooks long enough for me to escape. Don't know how he did it or what happened next, he didn't even have a weapon, but he seems to be alive. Must've had some trick up his sleeve?

Faith: Anything else?

Josef: No, not really. I think he mentioned having a lesson to attend? I wasn't really uh.. paying attention, sorry.

Faith: …

Josef: …

Faith: You're free to go. Thank you for your time.

Josef: Okay. Thanks, I guess?

Interviewee: Michael Braiser
Age: 22

Faith: Well Mike, tell me about when you ran into Tom.

Mike: I was strolling around Level 11, I don’t really know why, though. I think I was looking for some Almond Water when it happened? It feels like it's been ages…

Faith: What happened?

Mike: He fell flat on the floor right in front of me. He was uhhh… perched up in a window, apparently. Lost his balance, plummeted into the sidewalk, and cursed in portuguese.

Faith: Portuguese, huh? That's a new one.

Mike: Caught me off-guard, too! It's his native language, just like me. We chatted for a while. I also helped patch up his arm. It was a pretty rough fall…

Faith: So, what'd you two talk about? If you don't mind me asking.

Mike: Home, mostly. Seems he got here at a very young age, asked me a ton of questions about the Frontrooms. Told me about this library where he lived, too, and about something he had scheduled with this "Spec" guy. It didn't last long, but he said he'd like to talk again soon.

Faith: Seems you made yourself a friend. What did he look like?

Mike: Blonde, curly hair, about 1,80 meters. He was wearing a black leather jacket with this… winged emblem on the back.

Faith: Well, matches the description. Anything else?

Mike: Don't think so.

Faith: Alright, thank you! You're free to go.

Interviewee: Samantha Valentine
Age: 20

Interviewer's notes: This interview log was purposefully written to replicate Samantha Valentine's tone during the interview. As such, it features abbreviations, slang, and errors.

Faith: Alright, tell me about when you ran into Tom.

Samantha: M'kay, so check it: pretty sure it was prolly like 5 IN THE P-M when this dude saunters on in with a vibe cooler than an ancient scribe all like 'ayo check it can I hit this bitch UP?' Obviously he didn't say that, guy was SUUUPER profesh at first.

Faith: I'm assuming this took place in Onslaught Station… keep going.

Samantha: You fuckin' KNOW IT, gurl! Wait shit am I allowed to swiggity-swear in these?

Faith: Yeah, no prob. Can you describe what he looked like?

Samantha: Aight sick- so this kid walks in with insta-ramen lookin' hair, some jeans, a shirt, some kicks, y'know the dealio, then this fuckin' WICKED jacket with a rad design on the back! Kinda looked like an angel now that I think about it.

Faith: Alright, matches the description we have. What did he do at Onslaught?

Samantha: YO, ok, so he comes in n' he's all like 'Mmm, yes, maye I tryeth one of thouse fahncey traine rides?' and we're all like 'Oh he probz saw one of the posters' so we're like 'hell yeah get wicked with it bro' then this dude pulls out a motherfuckin' STICK OF LIGHT n' turns the place into the fuckin' PAIN TRAIN! So we're all like WHAAAAAAAAT and skidaddle on over to pisces station and he's like Wante tou see-eth me do that againe? AND THEN HE FUCKIN' DOES!

Interviewer's notes: Italicized sections of this interview were spoken in a horrible attempt at mimicking a british accent.

Faith: Why the british accent? We got witnesses saying that his native language is portuguese…

Samantha: Man, he just gives off those vibez to be a hundred percent corpo-REAL with u. I bet he's got like rich parents or somethin'.

Faith: Well, he is Blanche's butler, after all… anyway, that weird light stick you mentioned, can you describe it in more detail?

Samantha: It was this BIG ol' rod of light, kinda looked like a spear. For real, it was fuckin' rad to watch it in action.

Faith: Wait, slow down a second. Was he carrying that spear with him? No other eyewitnesses, as of now, mentioned anything like that.

Samantha: Duh, its fuckin' ONSLAUGHT STATION™️! He just pulled that shit outta NOWHERE!

Faith: That's… a new one. Did you see him do anything else worth noting?

Samantha: Y'know, I did.

Faith: Care to elaborate?

Samantha: (under her breath) dammit, you were supposed to say 'and that was'…

Samantha: Yeah, I'll elaborate. I saw him fuck. shit. UP in that car! Home-skillet probz woulda beaten Vi if he didn't biff it on the last round.

Faith: Do you know what happened? Did he throw it? Bad luck?

Samantha: Not entirely sure, but it kinda looked like a throw. He was havin a throwdown with The King n' then he just sorta… jumped into The King's blade? Then when he got back he was all like 'I shalleth be backe at a latere pointe in time' and then just dipped.

Interviewer's notes: During the course of the interview, Samantha's mocking british accent gradually shifted to scottish.

Faith: Strange. Anything else you wanna note?

Samantha: Kinda, yeah. After the kid left, The Conductor showed up, which he NEVER does. He was like 'hope to see ya soon, my good Bromium'. Then he spent some time talkin' with us but that's not the important part here.

Faith: Think they knew each other?

Samantha: Fat chance! his tone of voice was more like "hot damn that was pretty rad, hope that happens again" than it was "damn, been a while since that fucker's been around", the fucker is endearing in that context, if you couldn't giggity-gleam that.

Faith: I see. Well, I think that's all, thank you for your time. You're free to leave, unless you have something else to add.

Samantha: Well, while im at it, I might as well give it a shot. YO, IF ANYONES LISTENIN' TO THIS, ONSLAUGHT STATIONS A- (End of transcription.)

Additionally, the following notice regarding Tom Von Haderach, was recently published by the U.E.C.

U.E.C. Notice
Tom Von Haderach is currently a wanted criminal, due to his allegiance to Entity 140, as well as interfering with the Coalition's operations on Level 797 on its' behalf. As such, the two of them have declared themselves to be enemies of the Coalition. Any and all information on Tom Von Hadearch is highly valued, and should be reported to the nearest outpost. The Partisans of Arete are undergoing various search missions as to aprehend or eliminate this dangerous threat to our people.
