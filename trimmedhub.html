Trimmed Pages Hub

These trimmed articles are oftentimes old and flawed pages that have been archived under a special category labeled "trimmed:." They will not be deleted but will no longer appear in the main list, freeing up space for new entries. This category is reserved for pages deemed uninteresting, unsalvageable, or lacking sufficient value to warrant further attention. These articles will either carry the "trimmed" tag or "to-rewrite" tag for easy identification (the latter being available for anyone to rewrite). Pages can only be trimmed during the limited time Mass Trimming Project. Once this project is done, it will NOT happen again, and the category will not be expanded.
