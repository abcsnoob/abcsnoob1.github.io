UnMasked


TO MASK IS TO REVEAL…


rating: +34+–x
We, the Masked Maidens, are a product of deception, betrayal, and corruption. We are the harbingers of the end of the tyranny that has taken ahold of The Backrooms, and we will bring it down no matter what the cost, piece by piece. - Madam Evangeline Holmes.

Welcome, Masked Maiden.
We understand that the affliction of the mask may be confusing and disorienting at first, which is why we've compiled a database of articles here for you to read through. Get yourself caught up on events and the such, and familiarize yourself with our history and methods. Doing so will be the first step towards taking the anguish and rage of your mask and harnessing its reins into something far greater than your wildest dreams. Our purpose is quite simple: The ending of the corruption that has been spreading across The Backrooms at a steady pace — starting with the greatest offender: the M.E.G. You still have several ways to go with your initiation. The process of becoming a fully fledged member of this hidden society is a long and weary one, but once you have it all covered…

You will be ready to reveal.
- Amanda Wright

What is UnMasked?

This canon will follow the efforts of the group known as "The Masked Maidens" on their mission to cleanse the corruption of the different groups of The Backrooms — mainly the M.E.G. — from the supposedly virtuous values they claim to uphold. Each of the members wear a 'Mask' that not only conceals their identity, but also affects the user in a variety of powerful and dangerous ways.

They work in the shadows and accept new members when they find and use such masks. There will be stories detailing the feats of many members of the Masked Maidens in their quest to stop the spread of corruption; triumphs against evil and displays of courage are plentiful, but so are losses at the hands of forces of those most corrupt…

Any author may contribute entried for this canon; message TrailmixNCocoa via wikidot PMs or just hit us up on the Backrooms Discord. We'll set you up on contributing!

Canon Crossing

It is 100% allowed to canon cross, but be wary; Not all canons are connected perfectly, and your entry may not reflect the canons you use all that well. The connections don't have to be perfect but the Entry cannot be posted here if it directly and significantly contradicts other entries in this canon.
