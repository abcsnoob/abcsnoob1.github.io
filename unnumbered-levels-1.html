What are Unnumbered Levels?

Unnumbered Levels, as is implied by their namesake, are levels that for a variety of reasons either cannot or just don't have a number. Sometimes, giving a level a number doesn't make much sense, and other times, it simply might be not be warranted. Prime examples of this are the Basement, a location that has yet to be discovered by anyone that can catalogue it, or the Blue Channel, an area encompassing the Backrooms as a whole. Whether it's just personal choice or some lore-dependent decision, you can have any number of reasons for not using a number; as long as it can be inferred from your page, anything goes, so be creative, and make sure it makes sense!
