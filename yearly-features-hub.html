We have seen some absolutely incredible pages posted to the site in 2024 that have showcased a variety of genres, styles, and even formats. The features for this year were once again voted on by our community with a google doc and below are the chosen few. We are grateful to all of you who write for and read our site. We have a lot to look forward to in 2025, starting with our first con featuring POIs: characterizacon.

Annual features

Happy New Year, everyone! This may be coming a little too late, but The Backrooms Wiki staff is delighted to announce the annual features of 2023! It sure was an amazing year for the site and we saw some articles that can only be described with one word, and that is excellence. Which articles were the most excellent though, and which ones for each month? That is the purpose of this collection. Using a Google form, the community voted on one article from each month to be featured as the best of that month, which has now been compiled below. This page features a year-in-review of the best of the best from this year, along with featuring the author(s) who wrote it. Thank you to all the authors and readers who have contributed and read to the wiki in this year of 2023, without you, the wiki would be nothing! Let's hope for the best and let us have a brilliant year of 2024!

The Greenlighting team has voted on one article from each month to be featured as the best of that month, which have now been compiled below. This page features a year-in review of the best of the best from this year, along with featuring the author(s) who wrote it. If this works out well, we’ll do it next year as well! Please go check out their author pages and show your appreciation for their hard work! Thanks everyone for a great year, and here’s to a great 2023! Enjoy!
