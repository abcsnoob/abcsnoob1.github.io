Before we get started, let me introduce myself. Hello, I'm zaskou, but I go by Zask on discord. i love using apostrophes when i talk, but i almost never capitalize things or use commas/periods when chatting, so since i'm lazy, the rest of this intro will be in my chatting format. i present myself as a minecraft enderman online.

i joined this site back in april because i was interested in the concept of more than one level in the backrooms. i was the 707th user to join, and i am the 69th person to be an author! [top 50 if you don't count banned people]

i enjoy video games and pissing people off in the official backrooms discord server. you can find me on discord with the name Zaskou#8867. my dm's are always open!

I began writing this for the holiday exchange. Honestly, the original first draft of this level was much different from the one that was posted today. It was originally going to be a complete cave levels with crystals that gave you severe hallucinations and unknown entities would have been lurking about! It was a very exciting and intersting concept, even when I changed it up a lot. But I think I wrote this one well and that it's just what I wanted to write. Not too long, not too short, and interesting.
